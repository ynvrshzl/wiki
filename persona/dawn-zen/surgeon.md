> "We live in a world that demands we perform at our best... we must live as if there are children depending on us, as if there are young adults looking up to you... as if carrying where our ancestors have left."

This man is inspired by [Ben Carson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ben_Carson), the famus surgeon who performed the first twin surgery. There are few acts of humankind that show our ability to better life, as much as this. This story hopes to capture it's essence.

The surgeon took his job both seriously and elegantly... "It is the duty of a doctor to help others believe in life."

Both a masterful elegant symphonic orchestra with his hands
