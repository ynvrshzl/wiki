# C.
C is the "Constant".

This might be an extremely controversial perspective but I believe suffering, is a neutral constant, condition of all living things. 

However, cruelty, injustice, evil and ethical violations, are crimes. This is not the same argument.

The most beloved artworks, were created by some of the most broken people. The brightest minds have suffered deep torment. The greatest inventions took more life out of the inventor, than it gave back. The most cherished works...

This is not romanticizing suffering, rather, it is an argument to establish it as a constant, a condition. Thus "C"
