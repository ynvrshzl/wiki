Instruments & Devices for authoring.

We can think of human imagination as a [Tesla Coil](). It recieves ideas, information, and thought, almost like electrical voltage running through a current... a stream.

This means we separate...
- Working evnviornment (usually a computer, workstation) 
- Recieving enviornment (usually away from the workstation)
- Thought enviornment
- Capture enviornment (could be any device, or medium).
