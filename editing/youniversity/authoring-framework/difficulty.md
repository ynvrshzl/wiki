## Why is telling stories hard?
I was inspired to create this field, after realizing how hard it is to write, and someday, complete my own stories!

Like solving any problem in life, we have to identify the problem's form. 

Hypothesis: __Telling stories is hard becuase there are many parts that work together. Many details, and unknown variables.__

Tools we can use: Wiki's, Timelines, and structural tools that help us model, shape and constraint the infinite possibility of storytelling, into something cohesive.

This is a principle in all major creative and analytical fields such as UI/UX, Science, even Mathematics.
