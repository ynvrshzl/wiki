# Mechanics of Stories
_Observations on the mechanics of stories, and their unexplored power for pyschological healing of human beings._


**Abstract:** the ability to tell stories is a powerful expression of human capacity. There remains largely, unexplored and under-researched areas within authoring and stories. 

The purpose of this document/article is to explore observable and behind-the-scenes processes of telling stories. We can reverse-engineer our favorite works, to understand how great stories function, so that someday, we can use these knowledge bits to power the engine of our own stories.

## History
Throughout our human history, our human ancestors have told stories, long before they were written, they were spoken. Perhaps, that is the most important origin of this ancient medium. Even before history could be recoreded as history, we were telling stories.
