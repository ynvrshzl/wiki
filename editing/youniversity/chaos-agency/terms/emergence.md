"Emergence"

The reason I love working with logic gates is because; __it's hands-on chaos in real-time.__ 

We can _literally_ see in real-time, how we go from; a few possibilities, to enourmous unpredictability, and yet, somehow, we don't collapse. 

This raised the qustion that basically birthed this branch of study for me; _How do we actually work with complex things?_

That is the [Core ethos (purpose)](editing/youniversity/chaos-agency/ethos.md) of this field. 

From basic electricity flowing through a wire, to a basic transistor (gate) to the NOT gate, OR gate, AND gate and basically, work our way up to components, adders, modules, circuits, systems to manage systems and then computers.
