## 2026-05-03
Visual research

- Discovered limitations of  visual programming as an interaction model. For systems such as UE5 Blueprint, or Blender's Visual Nodes, the visual interaction approach slows down the progress of construction, thus increasing general understanding of systems. However, text-based source code has the general benefit of speed, hotkeys, and a keyboard-based workflow. Arguably faster, more accurate and expressive than a point-and-click interaction method (via the mouse.)
