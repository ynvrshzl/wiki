In the context of this word, I believe it is one of the most primal human griefs; **the regret of wasting beautiful things.**

Regretting time wasted, is a difficult feeling to describe. By definition, all time is spent, wether we wasted it or invested it well.

One thing is for certain, time cannot be restored, reversed, or re-arranged. But it can be remembered, and recalled.

I geniuenly hate this feeling. It's one of the worst forms of the human experience. It's almost like... a science solution of... multiple negative chemicals... all interacting at once... desperation... regret... _"why didn't you realize this sooner? Why did you make that decision? Why didn't I know this?"_

By default, the emotion is to beat the self, but a healthier perspective, is to release excess system pressure. Literally talking out loud about the way I feel, changes my nervous system in real-time, from feeling like i'm drowning in regret, guilt and stress, to release.
