# Aethereus
From the Greek words "Aether" meaning "Upper air", "Heavenly"

The worlds of dreams, fantasy & torment

Ethereon/Etheon are two worlds in one space. THey represent your individual version of heaven/hell. Imagining your version of the best, possible human life, or the worst, pain you can possibly imagine. These worlds are bridges from the [self](), to the [external]() worlds of the youniverse.

"An ether. A space inside my imagination, where my mind shapes reality itself."

"Imagine you could imagine... a world beyond your dreams. A world so real, you would never want to wake up from it. A life so beautiful, it hurts. Would it be torment?"

Everyone has their own ideal version of what life should be like for them. But then when the personas explore their own ideal realm they realize it's not the real world things would never be this easy things would never be this perfect part of what makes life worth living is the imperfection. Perfection does exist but only because imperfection exists. You cannot experience one without the other. You need both you need everything not just what you want but what is real.
