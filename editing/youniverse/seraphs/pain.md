The little cherub angel feels sadness: *"There is so much pain in this life... I don't... I don't know what to do about all of it..."*

An archangel walks over, his armor heavy to the step. He kneels down and pledges to the cherub *"Sometimes, simply being is enough."*

*"The mortals are limited in their knowledge. We can see everything. They, can only become as one. They are, one. That is the number of the mortal. And yet, without another mortal, one is not enough, it will die. One, always needs someone... Perhaps that is enough for you. To be, one... someone... to be you."*
