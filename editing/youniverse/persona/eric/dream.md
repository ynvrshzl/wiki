Her sweet smell. I remember now: *orange burst blossom.* That was her favorite smell. She would wear it all the time, with her favorite dress. The fabric as soft as silk, her skin, squishy & youthful like honeydew. Her smile was enough to give life back to my dead insides. Such a bright, little star in my void. She came to me at the perfect time in my life, when I needed her the most. We lay on the fields, a place of hope. Inhale. I fill my insides with the breath of life. Hope. I feel it now. This is what I fought so hard for. To be still. I fought endlessly. I destroyed eons of sleep in my restlessness.
