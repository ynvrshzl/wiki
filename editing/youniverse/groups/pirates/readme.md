The Cyber-goth-pirates **(CGP)** are a group of underground criminals rogue outlaws who once worked in the military, but betrayed the party.

They have created camps in the most toxic, dangerous parts of the city.

They wear gas-masks, not as accessories, but survival. Radioacitve, toxic, infectious waste lives in the underground, awaiting to be processed into energy for the city... [!citation needed] but the struggle for survival pushed many people to desperate measures.
