# What is the mission of our work?
I started these stories becuase I couldn't live with the trauma I had endured. I dreamt that one day, I would share these stories with someone, and it would help them to choose to stay alive. That's what the greatest stories have done for me, and we're simply continuing this ancient lineage.

As far as our scientific, technical work is concerned, there is no tool, better for the medicine of the human psyche, than a great story. That's the ethical mission.

But, I can't dedicate my life to solving a problem bigger than all of us, even the msot brilliant detectives work on one case at a time.
