CSAD is different from [Principia Cybernetica](editing/biblotheca/principia-cybernetica/readme.md) as this field is a study and collective research of the human process of designing, authoring, and creating computer architecture and systems. 

This field integrates several sources, as noted in [Citations](cite.md)

Whereas [Principia Cybernetica](editing/biblotheca/principia-cybernetica/readme.md) presents a philosophical, appplicable cognitive framework and foundation for the construction of new systems, [CSAD](#) is the study of already pre-existing systems, and our eventual evolution of reaching cognitive humaneness with our technological tools.

In this sense, the core identity of CSAD is the fundamental [theory](theory.md)
