__Core model:__ Learning is a feedback loop.

1st we explore, then we refine. Then, we explore again, but with more refined understanding. Again, we refine our exploration, into further understanding.

__This is not the ideal scenario,__ this is literally what happens when we learn [citation-needed](#). Fundamentally, through trial and error. With each attempt, we correct our course and gain control over our autonomy. This is how we learn to walk, or play the piano, or even write stories and theories.

This is a feed-back loop, but it is not a closed loop; we explore the outside world, and then we refine our abilities internally (during sleep, for example, or introspection).
