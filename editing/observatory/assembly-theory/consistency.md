# Consistency is a finite resource
Consistency is not merely discipline.

The principle of consistency is, arguably one of the foundations of learning new abilities for us human beings. The more we use an ability, the more we strengthen it. This goes in both positive and negative directions. Which is why consistency on it's own is not a fundamnetal principle, it is not enough to guide ethical ability-building.

Just becuase you can do something, doesn't mean you should. This is the principle of responsible usage of power.
