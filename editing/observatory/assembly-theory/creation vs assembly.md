**Abstract:** We do not create new things, rather, we assemble pre-existing things in new ways.

According to [the first law of thermodynamics,](#) energy cannot be created or destroyed, it can only be transformed from one form to another. Therefore, we do not essentially create new things inside of vacuums, rather, we transform things. (Assembly, to put things together to form new things.)

Semantics aside, thsi means we assemble parts. Everything can be broken down into parts, however, that is an infinite rabbit-hole. Rather, the practical application, is expolored further.
