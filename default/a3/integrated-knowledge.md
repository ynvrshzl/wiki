# Integrated Knowledge

This appears to be the highest form of human knowledge: the ability to synthesize information into wisdom.

When knowledge becomes integrated into the body (muscle-memory), you no longer have to think about it, it becomes expressive, fluid, automatic, second-nature. 

Spending thousands of hours learning an instrument or a language, and finally being able to play it or speak it, rather than thinking about it. But what if the process itself could be meaningful? What if the only sustainable progression for the human being, is if you enjoy the process to the destination?
