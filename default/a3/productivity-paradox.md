productivity paradox; the more time you spend in the same place (working obsessively) the worse your work becomes. Why? becuase our "work" is actually a product of our being. Meaning, the human being, is not a human doing. We are being. Becoming. Being. So the person is the real source of the work, not the work itself. Any meaning or joy we derive from our work, is just that, a reflection of, not a source of. consequently, it seems that to spend less time working, and more time living... is actually the only sustainable way to produce incredible work. *How is this explained from a biology perspective?*
