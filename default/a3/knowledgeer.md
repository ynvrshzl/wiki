## 1.0 - What is a "knowledgeer?"

What is a knowledgeer? An astronaut of knowledge? A student of life? A data collector? We could imagine this through the lens of astrophysics; like little astronauts mining the youniverse inside, for star fragments of knowledge, wisdom, puzzles and ultimately, meaning that points us back home.

Being a knowledgeer means you dedicate your life to the reduction of suffering, primarily through the work of study, knowledge, wisdom and autonomy. Those are our core principles. Imagine us to be like a little cute wikipedia globe, studying things that interest you, and organically growing expertise in [the things that shine forth to you.](shine-forth.md)

## 2.0 - Equillibrium

_"Thinking is only useful when it reduces suffering."_ This is the fundamental rule before we discuss anything.

## 2.1 - A warning to all deep thinkers. 

There are [information-biohazards](information-biohazards.md), things you should never ask or ponder, becuase they are harmful and dangerous to the human psyche. Questions like _"what is consciousness?"_ or _"am i real?"_ are immediate red flags. They are harmful becuase they do not reduce suffering, and can lead to psychosis...

**The most important part of my writings, our writings, is this idea.** Before we proceed with anything, our first idea is **equillibrium.** Zooming too far in, or too far out, breaks the scales of meaning. This is the fundamental basis of our work. Like zooming inside an infinite mandelbrot fractal. The more you zoom, the more you get lost in details and maddness. There is a point in which analysis becomes not only redundant, but harmful. The human being should not try to hack their own consciousness. Seriously. **this is the fundamental warning to all deep thinkers, knowledgeers, and brilliant human beings whom will read this.**

I only discovered this becuase I paid the price by suffering deeply, just becuase I was lost in thought, and in the wrong thoughts. Some things have no answer, and some things aren't worth questioning for the human being. It is really, the only guardrail I can share.

_Everything in balance, everything in equillibrium._
