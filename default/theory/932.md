How do we human beings create things? Why is it important to understand this fundamental mechanic?

As human beings, when our creative ability (problem-solving, composition, improvisation, etc.) is fundamentally an oscillation between these headspaces:

1. __Locality:__ Expecting an end __product__ goal from one's efforts.
2. __Theorycrafting:__ Learning and sampling the boundaries between __possibility__ and __practicality__
3. __Improvisation:__
4. Introspection: Learning from feedback through introspection

THis is also a feedback model, ideally sequentially, cyclically, but realistically, non-linear progression, or dual-mode, which is the source of error.
