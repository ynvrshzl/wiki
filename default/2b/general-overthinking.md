## The mind as a superspeed information highway

_"I am not my thoughts, I am the governor of my decisions."_

Let's envision our thoughts to be as if asteroid space dust, superspeed information highway. Thoughts _(asteroids)_ travel in this stream in one path, one direction, but they feel chaotic becuase it is noise. The superspeed information highway is more of a belt, than a one-track mind. We can imagine then, that thoughts are cyclical in nature. And thus, the important things, are, biologically, astropysically, and by faith, going to resurface.

If we imagine the self to be like a particle, then there is a good reason why being engaged with meaningful things, feels more than just good. It makes you feel alive in the best way  possible.
