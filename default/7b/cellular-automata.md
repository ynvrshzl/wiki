# Cellular Automata 

Celllular Automata is a computer science topic, observing how **complex systems emerge from simple rules**

## Principle 1 - Complexity is a structure/system
 Regardless of complexity, is is fundamental to remember that no system is just "one thing." It is a sum of it's parts. Systems always come in parts. **Everything sustainable is always simplifiable to it's constituents.**
