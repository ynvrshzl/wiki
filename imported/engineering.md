# The Craft of Engineering
Engineering is an incredible craft.

I mean... this is one of the most essential human abilities we have as human beings... and engineering is not only about "optimizing design"... if it becomes centred on "optimizing" then, Cybernetically, the Symbiotic relationship becomes self-feedback... isolation... decay of systems. In essence, Engineering is only as sustainable as it is useful and integrated to the world, external of itself...

e..g., when Engineering solves problems in the real-world, a clockwork stops being about "optimal gear ratios" and the design becomes oriented on inquiry + problem solving... "what gear ratio reduces  energy consumption at this stage? how does that change the overall function of the machine? how does the machine become a more effective problem solver, as a result?"

I wonder what the ancient Greeks, Romans and other civilizations thought about Engineering... it's as integral as Language and Socializing... perhaps if there were such a thing as "The base strata of human development and flourishing (eudaimonia)" we would have [these domains](domains-of-human-life.md)
