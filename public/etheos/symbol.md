# $\sum$ 

The letter $\sum$ is the __summation__ function in mathematics. [^1] 

This symbol was chosen to represent the core ideas of [ethiology](public/etheology/readme.md) becuase it encapsulates the "summary" of all things. 

If there could be a summary to the optimal human life, this symbol would best describe our work; _"The summary of the human condition, and exploration of an eventual cure."_
