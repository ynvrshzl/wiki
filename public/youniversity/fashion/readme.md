# Introduction to Fashion

Fashion, in the context of designing a character, can tell more about the person, than story does. Even before any story has been implied. That's the power of the visual. It conveys dense domains of context, without explaining. That's the _"show"_ in _"show and tell."_

## Why does fashion matter to human beings? - Significance of the visual

Perhaps, most significantly, fashion expresses the self. Fashion, is a subjective subset of attire, which is the profession of designing clothing for human beings. It could be described as expressive first, utility second.

## Fashion, Levels of Analysis
There are several levels of analysis to fashion. For example, jewelry can convey attention, but at the emotional level of analysis, jewelry carries a _"guardian"_ aura.
