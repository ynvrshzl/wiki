# Building a wiki for your stories
Exploring the structure of a story wiki

## Levels of compression
If we examine the web structure of a wiki, some mediums are compositional compressions. Meaning, they connect ideas together as one body of work.

- At the top level, we have the book. A book is a composition of all ideas in a wiki. A book compresses things into story-form.
- The persona bio is a folder with a collection of details. These snippets are usualy reserved for details, whereas narrative literature, should go inside of books.

## How do I know I'm actually using my own wiki?
A full sweep of a large wiki (1000+ files) is humanly unrealistic, therefore, the function of a wiki, is to act as an information source web, exactly the purpose of [Wikipedia]()
