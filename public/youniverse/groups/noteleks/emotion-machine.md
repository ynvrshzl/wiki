# The machine that cured the human condition
The original design was stolen from a farmer scientist robotics mechanic tinkerer rebel[^1]

in Lost angeles, scientists created a machine, that could generate any human emotion, and simulate human feelings. like love or lust or anger. this machine was first created as research, but then a scientist, realized that a fundamental tragedy in life is that some people don't experience life the same way you do. and emotion was one of the core factors. but then this story gives a lesson, that even if you don't feel happiness or Joy or love in your life, the answer is not in what you feel, or at least the answer is not in having perfect emotion like perfect beauty... 

it's a really important idea for me because I often imagine what would life, what would my life be like if I could feel happy all the time? I would probably have no reason to heal from trauma or to do most of the deep work we do everyday. so perhaps some level of hurt was necessary for me to survive and heal from, even if I don't know why I had to go through these things...
