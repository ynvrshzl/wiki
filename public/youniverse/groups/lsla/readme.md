# LSLA 
Law System of Lost Angelis

The LSLA... (Abbreviation of "Law System of Lost Angelis") is a security branch within the district of Law, in charge of Justice, Law and Humaneity.

All martial law, order, security, shares departments within the LSLA.

The LSLA has reduced all **physical** crime to 0% across all branches and districts. However, **new forms of crime emerged.** Crime that could not be visibly detected. As technology and human beings re-connected, a "parasite" within the human code, was discovered. Something that wants to infest and destroy life.
