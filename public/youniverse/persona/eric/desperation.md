# Desperation
Eric finds himself desperate after losing his best friend.

Eric screamed to the member of the school of gothica with their hooded cloak and oversized sleeves they seemed more like a shadow than a human being and yet they sat down with Eric and listen to him. He screamed to the cloaked shadow "I don't want to be here I don't want to be in this world anymore it's driving me insane get me out of here."
