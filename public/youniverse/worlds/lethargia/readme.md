# Letharia, the world of Depression

Letharia, is the world of depression. If it were a world, what would it look like? A dark, foggy forest, where no path is clear. Nobody knows you're here. You don't remember anything. It feels like you've been here your whole life... forever. It feels like there is no escape. At every corner of your eye, death stalks you. Ferocious beasts, rendered completely motionless, asleep. In this world, everythign is asleep. Your body, is a shell of it's former self, you, feel like a hollow frame of bones, carrying the weight of your past, and the burden of your failures. 

If depression were a world, this is what it woudld be like. A dark, lonely, ghostly forest, where you can't even feel your own body. You feel nothing inside. As if creatures came into your very soul, and took everything, leaving you empty. So, what are you left with? A forest, that you must escape from... for the longer you are here, the harder it is to get out.

Why is everything so motionless & dead in this world? It is the toxic air, which everything breathes. Here, even the trees exhale this air, and back to the soil. Everything that is not already dead, is dying. This world, could be the world of death itself. Depression, renders the body & mind, asleep. Yet, no amount of sleep in this world, will help you awaken. It is a world where time itself is slowed to a halt, and yet, the moments of light, that do reach through the dark skies, remind you, that it's probably, too late.

This horrific feeling, of being dead, while being alive, feels as if your very existence is punishment. "What did I do wrong? What did I do to deserve this?" You think to yourself. But this world answers to nobody. It will consume you until your body, a pile of ashes, and the soil will eat your insides. "From dust we came, to dust we shall return."

There are many figures who walk in this world. Wise sages, your child self, all of your past versions, you've had to bury, are tombstones, deep underground. They are part of your Youniverse. 

This world is not about finding a way out, but remembering. When all your emotions & memories are taken away from you, and you no longer have the will to keep fighting, what is left?
