The forge is the world of fire, rage, fury & passion. It is a world in constant construction, and while itself does not have any ground, it is a floating island, completely sustained by the energy of all it's living beings. Fire, is what keeps this world alive, and spirit weapons of all kinds are forged here, using anger, as the imbuing element, to bind all things, by soul.
