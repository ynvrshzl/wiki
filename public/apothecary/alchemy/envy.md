# Envy

*(figure .01 "the fallen angel, a rendition of envy.")*

Envy is such a terrible, draining feeling. It is part of the emotional spectrum which narrows one's perception lens of view, until all you can see is what you don't have.

Consequently, the nearest alchemical reaction (emotion) which soothes or protects the human self from the damage of envy, is gratitude. To be grateful for what I do have. To be grateful for everything I am doing. 

## 1.0 - Genesis of envy
Where, how, why, when does envy begin?

Perhaps envy stems from an earlier protogenesis: [ambition](ambition.md) *"I should be doing so much more."*
## 2.0 - Alchemical analysis
The significance of envy, is additive, meaning, envy in isolation is...?

Envy with rage becomes jealousy. Envy with desperation becomes fatal.
