# Plot and Cultural study
This story is partly fictional, part case-study of human history.

Across the society of Lost Angelis, Olympus, Imperia and (...) there is a universe class of (...) to define the (...) of Citizens.

This is a study on the [Industrial Revolution]() and a neutral study of both; it's effects on human health, and it's impactg on civilizational life. In essence, it is a core source of tension... for some things to increase, others decrease... and often it is not justified by the population.

Few people end up living a good life in Lost Angelis. Most people are barely surviving, and overworked beyond ethical justificiation. We see this all around us, in the world we live today.
