"IDE-A" stands for **Integrated Development Enviornment for Authors.** It is a computer program designed to help authors write stories. But also, it's a pun. THe main source of the program is the "IDEA", the "Ide-a" is what gives the program purpose.

It's not meant to be a permanant studio, the long-term goal is to help you publish your stories. That's why I created this. To help you get your stories out there, and not collecting dust in shiny archival systems!!

This tool was born from a necessity to express our stories, but having no space or time for them.
