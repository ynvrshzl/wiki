## Prologue

People belong in social enviornments, when other people irritate someone, that means there is both a disharmony of the enviornment outside, and the world inside.

## 3.0 What does it feel like? Irritability, described from the lens of the human
When i'm irritable, everytime i drop something, or bump into something, or worse; someone bothers me, it feels like a snake, biting it's fangs poision that soaks my bloodstream, and from one second to another, i am a completely different person. Angry. Irritable. Unrecognizable. Like an overheated circuit, that is somehow, still alive, way past it's melting point.

But it's also two-way, meaning, the surrounding enviornment seems to react to oyur inner state. It is bizzare to say the least, and only makes the feeling worse.

Irritability is two-way, in the sense, that the external world contributes to your inner poison. 

## The second side of irritability.
