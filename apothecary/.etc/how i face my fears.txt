how do you do something that scares you? I think it's a lot like fire, and when fire is strong enough you have to fight it with fire. 

practically speaking, if something scares you that won't change. it won't ever stop being scary. however, once I found something that scared me more... that's when I said, yes I'm afraid of making this decision, but I'm much more afraid of not making the decision. 

ultimately that's actually what my life compass became. essentially I'm still afraid of living some parts of me, but I much more afraid of dying without ever having lived fully. that's the thing that constantly orients me.
