Topics: Facing too many choices, being overwhelmed with freedom, analysis-paralysis.

I like to say that having ADHD, or any neurodivergence, is alot like a heavy train. It's hard to get going, it's even harder to stop, but the distance you can travel & the things that only your kind of train can do, only you can do! 

Potential comes at a cost... and often, the most intelligent people are the ones that doubt themselves the most.
