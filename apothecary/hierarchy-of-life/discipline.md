### What does "Discipline" actually mean?
A quick history of the word Discipline

To have a skill, is to have a discipline. That's actually what the word _discipline_ means. Originally, it comes from the latin word: _disciplina_ which originally meant teaching, training, learning, knowledge. [Source](https://www.etymonline.com/word/discipline) 

Discipline was originally about learning, not about punishment. It was in the middle ages where the word became twisted into the modern context of punishment.
