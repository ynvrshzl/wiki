__What drives the clockwork of human autonomy? What defines the human experience?__

In Principia Humana, we define the human experience as;

> "An ecosystem, as unique as the fingerprint, of intergrated domains."

A large portion of the human experience is relatively unmapped, unexplored and unseen. This is why it is so diffcult to translate what one __really, objectively feels__ and __what the external world can subjectively understand.__

Distinction between __Subject and Object__ is a core concept in this body of work. [Read more here](object-subject.md)
