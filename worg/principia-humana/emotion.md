# Emotion

A core domain in the system of human experience, the processing domain;

A large part of the human experience is mostly hidden. 

- Emotion
- Stimuli
- Sensory processing 
- Intrinsic processing

If the external world casts "signals" as stimuli and sensory information, then the purpose of our senses is to interpret and make sense of the information. This is a bi-directional process, as we interpret information both from; external sources (such as the natural world) & internal sources (such as thoughts).


Our internal processing is somewhat of a "Filtering" system. That is what the [human nervous system]() is focused on. [citation-needed]()
