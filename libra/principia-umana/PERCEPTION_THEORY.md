# "Perception of mind theory"

I am not stating "we can shape reality with our thoughts" but I am proposing this hypothesis; "is it possible that our thoughts, our active beliefs (axioms), our identity, shapes the perception through which our lens of life (perception, mind, etc.) is shifted?"

[Isaac Netwon](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Isaac_Newton) studied [Optics](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Optics) (how human vision works) and developed the first known concept of color theory; how a single prism reflects different wave lengths of pure light. We "percieve" these wavelengths as color, but the pure light itself already contains the full visible spectrum. 

This is fascinating, becuase it suggests while we cannot sense all of life at once (i.e., pure light), our senses can be thought of as "light cast ray filter." Not only is this an autonomic process; the human being actively shapes it, wether knowingly or not.
