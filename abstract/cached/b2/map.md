# "To solve the most pressing issues in human life, we must map the domains of understanding."

the most serious ethical idea of in our work, is the idea that in order to solve all the problems or at least the fundamental suffering and human life, we must map out the universe to understand it. and I don't mean the universe out there I mean the universe inside the human being. the mind the body and what we may call the soul. 

> _"What is animating this puppet of Earth and biology?"_

this is what I believe all of the domains of human knowledge such as mathematics and science are intuitively arranged they are observations of life through different fields of analysis. mathematics is the study of quantity. philosophy is the study of why. and why, is the most powerful kind of question because it reveals the purpose. and the purpose of things is the life source of the human being. without purpose there is no sufficient reason for the engine of life to continue. but with purpose, unbearable suffering becomes justified and maybe even meaningful.
