# Abstract 
The 'yagi' were ancient warriors who crossed the edge of time. They fought, as human battle angels, against the void.

During the training of  the  Rebellion, Dawn needed to leave something behind, for when he wiuld be inevitably executed.

Everyday he meditated at the fountain/river. Writing insight and wisdom. *"if I could leave behind what would strengthen their resolve... what would it be?"*

he could not overwhelmedm their senses,  so he  wrote a collection of all of his ancestral teachings. these are  thise writings. "How could I guide them in a broken world? They do not need enlightenment as I have  failed... they need to stay and protect this fallen eden."
