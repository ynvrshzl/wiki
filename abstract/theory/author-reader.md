# Author Reader Perspective Theorem (ARP)
This theory describes the relationship between creating something, and viewing it. 

## "ARP" - Foundational mechanics of human creativity

"ARP" Stands for "Author, Reader and Perspective." Meaning, the author is a perspective, a mode of operation. The reader is also a perspective, a mode of experiencing. These are perspectives of creation, vut there is also a third emergent element which makes this cycle sustainable; the ability to switch between these perspectives. 



## Significance 
The significance of this abstract theorem is implied in all creative human fields. We are not machines that can output endlessly; our organic capabilities, which make us expressive creatures, are also the constraints from which we must learn to operate within, effectively and sustainably.

This relationship is the hidden driver of how much work we can create. It is not about mere productivity, rather, learning to spend it time and energy, where it matters most. 

## Real world samples of ARP
- A Musician writing music (a), and  listening (r)
- A software engineer constructing an app, and testing it's functionality
- An author writing a story (a) and re&reading their own work, as if experiencing it for the first time (r)
