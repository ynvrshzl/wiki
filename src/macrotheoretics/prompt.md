# "Signal casting"
An enviornment almost "casts signals" in the form of sensory input, etc.

We can think of an enviornment as; a space containing prompts or conditioning for action, thought or behavior. 

What does this mean? An enviornment contains prompts/conditionings, such as;
- A computer operating system, prompts the human being for interactive action. Icons, Commands, Options, Settings, Notifications, etc.
- Programs are enviornemnts, with their own set of prompts/conditions.

This is important becuase it means; our enviornment conditions us for specific modes of operation. This means the curation of an enviornent, is that which will direct our limited human resources & abilities, over sustained seasons of time, to nurture & nourish work or abilities.


## Application
If we apply this to a real-world scenario, e.g., organizing complex information in a wiki, we can strategize our workflow to prioritize prompts and conditions, rather than structures.
- E.g, a text authoring enviornment which queries "prompts" rather than showing an arbitrary file system.

This helps us to both; __Filter the enviornent, and Prompt for further action.__
