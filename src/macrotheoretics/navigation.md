## Why navigation?
The reason navigation is the most important function, is becuase once systems become spatial (multi-dimensional), they require new structures in-and-of themselves, to be interacted with. A system that has poor or no navigation, is essentially, a world without a map; fundamentally unavigable, and unuseable.

In a complex information system, the filesystem becomes insufficient as a tool of navigation.
