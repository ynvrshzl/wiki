# Objects of human cognition
It is useful to study human cognition, not merely as an orchestra of neurotransmitters, but as fields of external + internal processes, interaction & relationships.

## Summary
Attention is like a cursor, Focus is like a cone, a field-of-view.

## Cursor

If attention is like a cursor, then we can imagine the cursor as being a positional object in information space. 

If attention is like a cursor, then the line is the current mode of focus.

## Properties of objects
- The cursor has position.

## Posision of cursor
A cursor can move forwards and backwards.  Just like a cursor, human attention can move forwards and backwards, in the context of a line.


- Focus is similar to the fovea + periphery of the human ocular system. A dispersion of resolution, progressively expanding outwards from the central cone of focus
- - Time is deriven relative to the position of the cursor, similar to Timezones.
