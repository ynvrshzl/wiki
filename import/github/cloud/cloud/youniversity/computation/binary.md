## The abstract psychomodel of binary
0 and 1 are the only numbers in binary. Rather, the concept if binary is either-or. True or False. Stop or Go. This is entirely structured based on how [Electrical Transistors]() work. Which explains why computers use binary, instead of real numbers.

| ------- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Binary  | 0   | 1   | 01  | 10  | 11  | 001 |
