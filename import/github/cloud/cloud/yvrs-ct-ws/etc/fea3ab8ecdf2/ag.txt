Dear reader, dedicated to all who feel greif for being a human being, suffocated in a world that doesn't hear you or see your pain. 

The following thought is something that nearly killed me. I only discovered the answer recently... after years of wrestling with this fundamental problem of existence.

when i think about how cruel human beings can be, i see the children of the east, or the children that are missing. What is this madness? This absurdity? And then i'm expected to just "don't worry be happy?" I have both the rage of a mountain lion, and the sadness of a clown who's late to the party... like is there something i'm missing here?

Authors note: Now, i think it's better to put these thoughts into a character... a persona... they are honestly too overwhelming for me to process on my own...

Essentially i ask myself "how am i supposed to be even care about being happy or even want to continue existing, when i'm in a world as crazy as this?" Cynycism is a step to wisdom, but it's a doortrap if you stay still for too long. Keep moving. Keep questioning and seeking the good in life... virture...love...respect...sacrifice...honor... those are the highest forms of human meaning...that's the real answer here..

So I believe that although i can't help those poor children that didn't ask to be in this world, I can atleast choose to not end my life and stay alive, and stay alive not just to merely survive but to devote myself to my life's work.

Unfortunately, I can't spend every living breath of my existence working in something meaningful... that's why i've accepted the parts of me that need rest and play... as annoying as it may be at times... it's the only thing that keeps me from falling off balance... a little bit of chaos in the order...
