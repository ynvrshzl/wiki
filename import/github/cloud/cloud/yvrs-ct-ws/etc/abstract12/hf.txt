there are things that you simply cannot capture in stories not because there doesn't exist the language to express them but because writing a story that captures life in its most raw detail both the glorious beauty of life and also a horrifying brutality, a book or a story that shares the unfiltered truth of life would be too catastrophic and would certainly be banned... so then I tell myself the purpose of my stories isn't to capture everything I see in life but rather to capture the most important parts and to make you feel something.
