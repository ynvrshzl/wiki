revenge personified is inspired by kalista from league of legends. her story is she was betrayed, and killed by someone she thought she could trust. 

now she is no longer a human, she is a wraith (ghost) of revenge. in the game, she carries her oathsworn, which essentially means she harvests their soul and kills them, bringing them back to life. her ultimate protects her oathsworn from dying by pulling them, and keeping them inside her.
