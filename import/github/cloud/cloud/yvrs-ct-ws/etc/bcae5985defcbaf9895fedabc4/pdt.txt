"perception"

I think I've understood that we human beings are creatures of perception so what that means is when I ask what's the meaning of life what I really am asking is... "what is the meaning of life through my eyes? was it is the meaning of my life?"

I don't know if perception and perspective are the only two things that can really change a human being, but from personal experience I know that emotions and experiences shape most of the way you see life. 

life could be sunny and beautiful if you're in love, or it could be torturous and rotten if you break up. 

I think it's incredibly important to understand that life itself doesn't change with our perspective it's only what we see that changes. that's what I mean by we are creatures of perception and we need to understand that in order to avoid deep suffering.
