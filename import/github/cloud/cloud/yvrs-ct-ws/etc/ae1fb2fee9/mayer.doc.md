# mayer
"my name is Nihil Mayer. I was dispatched with the anonymous detective sentry unit A group of undercover officers that watch over the city and protect the civilians from everyday harm.'

"last sunfall eyewitness the most horrific crime of My Life. too terrible to record but too real to forget... this is something common in this line of work it leaves people Disturbed there's no one else to clean up after a crime scene... and we're often juggling night shifts without rest..."

"but there's something deeply rewarding about this job... I've met some great people. I've met some terrible people... "


(context: nightfall? winter? in lost angelis, numbers and time are replaced with human language... using the natural cycles of nature to tell the time)

"winter, early morning, a school student tragically ends her life by jumping infront of a service train. early morning, next day, two students have hung themselves at the school cafeteria."

"these are my service logs. they are disturbing and potentially dangerous to read without formal training. I was given only a year of training... but ive seen people twice my age who had to quit this job."

"are you off in there look up to the night sky after a long day of work I come home and sit here by the window... watching over the city... if I could save each one of those students from having ended their lives... or from having ended the lives of others... these violent ends... where are they coming from?"

authors notes: here you can see mayor's character developing because he is placed in a situation where he has to confront the evil of human nature... most people in his situation would be demoralized and nihilistic hands his first name nihil... and yet he remains true to his detective nature he asks the right questions... instead of pondering on philosophy
