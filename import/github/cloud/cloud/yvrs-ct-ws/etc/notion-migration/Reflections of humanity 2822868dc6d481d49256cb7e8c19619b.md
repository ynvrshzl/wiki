# Reflections of humanity

Created: October 4, 2025 1:48 AM
Edited: October 7, 2025 12:00 PM

this will be one of the most important books that I'll ever write. 

imagine a badass female author who uses writing and books to observe problems in life and find solutions. a badass capable female, that's who I am, hazl the polymath!

this book touches on a lot of topics including nihilism existentialism hopelessness apathy philosophy psychology religion history. I essentially externalize my observations and ultimately the answers I've found I have sustained me during the most depressing hopeless isolating moments of my life. 

as with all of my books, it will be available for free on my website.

“I'm not in control of life. I'm not in control of the evil cruel things that people do to each other. I'm not in control of the tragedies that happened every day. I can't control the brain chemicals that cause a human being committed crime. the only thing I can change are the tiny parts of my consciousness that my body allows. I can only change my belief system my perspective and my decision to stay alive even in the world that makes me back for death. I've seen the worst size of human beings and I've seen the best sides. I've seen the tragedy of life and the gloriousness of healing. that is the answer, healing. it is the bridge between.”
