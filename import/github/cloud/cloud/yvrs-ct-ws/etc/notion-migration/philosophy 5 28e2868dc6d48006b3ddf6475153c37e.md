1. signs of life. this section is dedicated to the human being who is questioning if it’s even worth it to stay alive, since we will all die in the end anyway, why keep fighting? 
2. I’ve seen almost everyone who attempted to seriously end their own life, and they survived. there is almost always this immense, unbearable appreciation that it didn’t work and they are still alive.
3. the logical mind will try to analyze “why stay alive? this hurts.” the reason to stay alive and continue fighting. the only way i’ve survived these unbearable periods is by trusting in my future self, or god. when that doesn’t work, it’s the most brutal kind of isolation, where it feels like your own body is betraying you. the darkest part of the abyss.

the core lesson of all of this? i learned that even though i cannot analyze or think my way out of suffering or problems in life, if i can survive there is a rebirth in my biology that overrides logic… paradoxically, you have to experience it in order to understand it, and the analytical engine doesn’t like that, because it’s too uncertain
