# how to survive the abyss.

Created: October 17, 2025 2:56 AM
Edited: October 17, 2025 2:58 AM

i find peace and sadness knowing that someday i will not be here. i just won’t exist. that’s… that’s terryfing. but also…

when the infinite expanse of dreaming as a child, is replaced by the infinite world of problems and issues as an adult, that is a terrifying place. life becomes unclear, uncertain, but not in the dream-like, eye-opening way. It’s overwhelming and deeply traumatizing, that’s why you absolutely must have something to guide you, a little lucky charm, a symbol, a song, something that is fo such significance that if everything were to be taken away from you, you know that’s the one thing that can never be robbed from you, and you can always rebuild everything you had becuase in a way, you already have everything, in your source.
