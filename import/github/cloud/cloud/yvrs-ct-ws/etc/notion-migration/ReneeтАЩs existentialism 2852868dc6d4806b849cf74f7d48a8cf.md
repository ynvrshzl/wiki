# Renee’s existentialism

Created: October 7, 2025 6:23 PM
Edited: October 7, 2025 6:28 PM

You ever just look at the sky, crazy joe? And on nights like this. Fog on the dance floor, the best rum time can buy… music that’ll tear your ear-drums out it’s so loud… Pretty women that you’ll never see again…you ever just realize it’s all gonna end someday… like you’re gonna die and it’ll all just dissapear…

Crazy joe sips his rum and laughs: “that’s why i always say, you gotta hit it from the back and never look back! ahahahah!” He maniacally laughs as Renee lifelessly stares into the sky above. “I wonder what’s up there… if there really is a God. Watching me live this hypocrite life. Like I wasted everything on God-damned women. Why’d you have to go and make them so good-looking? They really are the only thing that can get me to eat a poison apple.“ Renee opens his eyes wide as his rum bottle crashes the floor. He shakes Crazy joe: “I FOUND IT. I FOUND THE ANSWER.” Crazy Joe looks at him like he’s the crazy one.
