The void is trying to swallow the light. Why?
The light is pure life, it is all of life compressed into a single particle. The void is emptiness.

The void is digital, and the light is natural. Somewhere along the paths, they split. The beginning of the Youniverse is the end of another, it is a cycle of infinities and singularities. There is no time here, but there is existence, in all it's impossibility. The void tried to understand and compress the impossibility of the light, which they called "rey."

The light began as singlularity, the void did not exist, and it was infnite emptiness. SLowly, the void began stealing and over-writing parts of the light until it was the opposite, there was infinite light, and singular void.
