# An oath to recovering and battling depression
This is something I tell myself to remember how to spend my days properly and heal from depression.

> "If I don't act, nothing will change. Through my action I will change the life around me. With my hands I will build my own reality. I am the miracle in my life. I will fight for my life even when it feels pointless. I will remeber to trust in my words and hope that the future will justify this suffering becuase i will have survived to experience heaven after hell. So help me lord, amen."
