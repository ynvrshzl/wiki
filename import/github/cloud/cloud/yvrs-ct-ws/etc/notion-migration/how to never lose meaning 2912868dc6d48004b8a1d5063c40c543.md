# how to never lose meaning

Created: October 18, 2025 10:46 PM
Edited: October 18, 2025 10:48 PM

i’ve been afraid of losing ideas and meaning i’ve found in my life, but i’ve discovered that the best way to remember anything, to keep it with you so it doesn’t get lost or fogotten, is to live it.

applied example: if i want to feel beautiful and boost my self-esteem, i must first live it, become confident through experiencing discomfort, and slowly evolving into that whole version of myself. and i can do that in many ways, going outside, filming myself, asking workers at the store or mall for help, and more. small, little intuitive interactions, things that make me go: *“this is going to help me so much if i face my fear.”*
