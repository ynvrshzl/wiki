# The fragility of life, the gloriousness of life.

Created: October 4, 2025 1:48 AM
Edited: October 7, 2025 12:00 PM

Currently in my life it feels like my life is a container filled with memories, dreams hopes, secrets, answers, visions & incredible things that I know can make our world a better place.

But that container is glass. It's fragile. Everyday i treat myself with kindness & respect as I would to someone I love.

But i know if the glass were to fall I wouldn't have the will to fix or heal everything. My life has fallen apart so many times that I'm extremely delicate. It hurts because I don't even know whats the right way to treat myself. Like im the glass fragile, or like im the strong connections of the world inside me? treating myself like im both is very confusing.
