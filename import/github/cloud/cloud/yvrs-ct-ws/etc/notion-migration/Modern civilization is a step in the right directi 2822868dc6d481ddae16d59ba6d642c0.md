# Modern civilization is a step in the right direction

Created: October 4, 2025 1:48 AM
Edited: October 7, 2025 12:00 PM

Sometimes I think too much about how modern humans have created this civilization. And I think… isn’t this just a horrible thing? Why are so many human beings allowed to suffer? While the few at the top are playing with human lives like a chessboard.

Then I think further, none of us, wether at the top or at the bottom of the chessboard, we will all die. All of the accomplishments and towers we built will someday turn back to dust, just like us. We don’t own anything, and we’re part of the larger eco-system of life. However, I think to myself, is there anything positive to live for?

Sturyindg history shows me that life is getting better, as difficult as that is to accept.

Technology can solve problems in nature such as excess suffering, mental illness, and I hope for someday, someone invents solutions to life’s greatest problems. But even if that utopia is far away now, what drives me is that I’m like a little flame that has been given alot of fire from alot of people. Over the course of my life, battling with mental & physical illness, and trying to figure out my reason to keep existing, when both life inside me and outside me, feels pointlessly cruel.

Just like a book doesn’t make sense if you stop in the middle, that’s a metaphor that creates a strong wall against suicidal thoughts in my head.

Despite how many voices I hear in my head, I understand the real me is when I speak out loud. Sometimes I try to speak in my thoughts: *“What’s going on in here? Why are you so loud? Why are you in so much pain? How can I help you?”*
