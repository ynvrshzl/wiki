# My dream

Created: October 7, 2025 4:04 PM
Edited: October 7, 2025 4:06 PM

My dream is for someday my life’s work, stories, games, music and more, to save the life of that neuroscientist. To inspire the musician not to give up. To motivate the person on the edge of ending their own life, to remind them of the fragility… the reason for their human existence. 

I’m not in control of my stories ever reaching someone beyond myself, but I can choose to stay alive and dream and hope. That in itself is worth living for.
