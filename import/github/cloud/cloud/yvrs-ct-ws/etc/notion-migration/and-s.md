# And I told her, “you are enough.”

Created: October 7, 2025 7:47 PM
Edited: October 7, 2025 7:47 PM

Why did I choose to wake up early today? I have this feeling today, that i’m actually respecting life and loving being alive. It’s not rose-tinted glasses… it’s more like a hope that someday I’ll truly be healed and happy to have survived, so that I could finally live.

Now I remember, I was in bed, but I felt something wake me up. I looked over to my curtains and saw the faint glimpse of daybreak, shining through the windows of my room. That’s what first made me realize: “the morning is so beautiful, it’s exactly how I want my day to start.” But what got my booty out of bed, was a song that I was listening to, the night I fell asleep. “Repo Blues.” a deeply beautiful song written entirely by AI and yet, it evokes such human-ness. My existential desperation to live a meaningful life and make the most of myself, not in a narcissitic way, but in a healing way. That’s what you’d do for a child you love. You dont’ have to save the world, you’re enough as you are, and you deserve to see it externally, everywhere. 

I found this song randomly, but it’s a gift from life.
