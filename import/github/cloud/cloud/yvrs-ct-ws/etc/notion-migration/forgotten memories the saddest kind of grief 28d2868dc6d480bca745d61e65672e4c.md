# forgotten memories: the saddest kind of grief

Created: October 15, 2025 2:39 AM
Edited: October 15, 2025 2:40 AM

i'll accept that i'll never know the names of those songs i deleted. it's almost like in those movies where a character sees a beautiful stranger passing by on public transport... and they frantically try to search for them... but they can't find them... and even forget what they looked like to begin with. all that remains is a ghost... a silhouette of someone/something that once existed clearly, but now will never return. that's a quiet kind of grief... like being buried underground alive... it's silently suffocating... but after the initial sledgehammer of grief and shock, there is a calm acceptance, that if something as incredible as that happened once, something just as incredible, could happen again.
