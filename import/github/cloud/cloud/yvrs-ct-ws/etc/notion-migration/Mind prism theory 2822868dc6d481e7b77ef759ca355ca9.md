> The Prism Mind Theory
The self radiates as a unified light of consciousness, but creative and cognitive processes act as a prism — refracting that light into diverse expressions (writing, coding, music, world-building, journaling). Each medium reveals one color of the full spectrum of being. To understand the whole, one must integrate the colors back toward the source.
