# the reason i write stories is…

Created: October 4, 2025 1:48 AM
Edited: October 7, 2025 12:00 PM

the reason i write stories is…

1. my mind is a storm. there is noise & voices & so many ideas. if I don't put them somewhere, I literally cannot survive with the noise in my head.
2. i get to explore topics & problems that would otherwise completely overwhelm me. this is the original purpose of personas

## Writing stories saved me from ending ny own life.

it saved my life from ending my own life. This is one of the few things I'll keep re-telling for the rest of my life, it is absolutely critical that I remember when life gets incredibly painful, that you cannot even see the next step, or worse, you don't even want to or care to see the next step, that even in my most demonstrably numb moments in life, I still have something within me that screams to exist

when i was at my most suicidal & in severe psychological danger, I learned that only I jad the choice in the end to live or not. These characters, music, stories & imaginations saved my life. But the real reason im still alive is because I chose to listen to the little part of me that wanted life. That little part kf me is what keeps me alive… i believe it is what keeps all of existence alive. I don't have a name for it, but in my Youniverse, I explore it.  Science & existentialism nearly took my life. I pondered I now understand to avoid these topics that triggered hopelessness in me.
