being a polymath has many frustrating parts. with a little rewarding glimpses of light make it all work.

something I commonly experience is the feeling of dread, like I'm stuck in the same cycles the same loops the same problems feeling unable to get out it feels like I'm not amazed and it's suffocating and it feels like even though I can do it myself, I never really solve them. my community incredibly isolating. and demoralizing. we have to remember that we are a human creatures not machines no amount of logic will solve all the problems in our life. perhaps it's your environment. perhaps it's the people you're living with. maybe you need to declutter your mind.

this is an area where you have to experiment and hope for the best. it's completely intuitive and logical only make it worse.
