# What it feels like to….

Edited: October 7, 2025 12:49 PM

That’s almost like a silent desperation… deep within you that you’re just begging to get to what you really want… becuase you know we don’t have forever… Like being trapped underground and desperately making lists or plans just for nothing to happen… but suddenly, when you’re about to lose hope… that’s when it happens… you break from the ground…
