you remember that scene from Arcane, where Viktor is running again, an adult? he spent his entire life unable to fully walk, depending on his crane for everything. and when he finally let the arcane element fuse with him, it gave him demonstrably strong legs. he was able to run again... he ran as fast as he could. it was one of the most beautiful scenes we have ever seen!

so when he fuses with a machine... he loses his humanity on the outside... but gains everything he wish he had... adn that's when he regrets letting the power control him.
