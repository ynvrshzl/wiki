Dawn Zen taught a mix of Tae Kwon Do, Kung Fu, Karate, Jujitsu and many more martial arts.

He realized it was the only thing that could save the young humans from "the eclipse."

"My ancestors told stories for centuries... of a great eclipse of the soul. One day, the world would have complete total blackness, nothingness. Many will take their own lives, Many will be driven to madness... It is now, more than ever, that we must teach them the ways to defend and protect their mind, spirit and body... One day will come where I can no longer breathe...But you will continue on for me... for our ancient humanity. And also bring me good food."
