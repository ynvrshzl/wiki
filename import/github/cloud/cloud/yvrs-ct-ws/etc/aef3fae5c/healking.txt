healing from depression

one of the most  important things i've learned is if you are priveledged  enough to have privacy... maybe a room with a door, or some space in your life... where you can immerse yourself in the things you love... that's what saved my life. no literally. i say that alot. but if it wasn't for my own world... my youniverse... you wouldn't be reading this right now becuase i wouldn't have existed long enough to write these words... in other words, without my worlds, i would have 100% ended my life.

key insight: world-building can save you.
