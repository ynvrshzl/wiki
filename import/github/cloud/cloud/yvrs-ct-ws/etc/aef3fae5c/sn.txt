to all the sensitive souls who feel alone and confused and maybe perhaps sometimes you don't even feel like you're a human being like it almost feels like we were brought to the wrong planet. here's a little story I wrote for myself it opens my hearts to the possibility of other worlds that maybe we were once more than just human and we feel that in our bones and that perhaps the purpose of our life here is to help fix this broken world...

we can't save everyone most people will be doomed to the void and most people will live lives stuck in their own bubbles and that is a tragedy of its own story, that's not my purpose in life that shouldn't be yours either. 

this is also expressed in my exploration of the duty of the artist. the duty of the creative person... the power of art is found in the interpretation, and how the person receives it not the exact message or the purpose of your art but simply to create it and share it. 

because I too have felt throughout my entire life that I am not just more sensitive than others but I feel like I belong being alone I'm alone wolf I don't even know if I want to settle down someday and have a stable relationship 

my mind is not stable at the constant storm but there are little things that do remain constant and those are things that matter most and also remind myself to be playful... if i can... 

"we are in this world... but we are not of it. we came here to heal... to guide... to protect. we agreed... before we had a body..."
