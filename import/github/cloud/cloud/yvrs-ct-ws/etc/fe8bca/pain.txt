here's an important idea we've learned while healing from trauma and illness on our own.

pain... is like sample data in science... now... not all pain should be generalized... but in our case... the pain we felt was external and internal... in other words... it was sample data across biomes and environments... but with the same patterns.

any time there are patterns in science, we make sure to pay attention becuase they reveal hidden secrets of life's inner workings. patterns are one of the many hidden languages in nature, and they help us understand why and how things work.

this doesn't matter when you're suffering psychologically and physically: what did matter was experiencing the opposite of pain.

pain can be considered signals from the body... "this hurts... but we can't forget it, becuase it could happen again if we don't remember how bad this feels."

that's the biological function of pain i think...

and so... you need the opposite of pain... otherwise the scientist is only studying one side of an experiment sort of speak... and... sometimes undiagnosed mental illness is a timer... people reach a boiling point... and we know what that's like... that's where scary things like delirium and nervous breakdowns occur...

we felt the edge but never descended... if we would have... we would not be here.

essentially... things that make human life worth living... but also... there are things specific to your unique fingerprint biology... that makes your life worth living.

we discovered those things... intuitively... they are the only reason we did not end our life... we were steps away from doing it... but we were saved.
