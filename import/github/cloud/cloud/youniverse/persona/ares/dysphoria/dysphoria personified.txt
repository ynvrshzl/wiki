section: "mechanics of dysphoria"
from what we've observed and lived with gender-dysphoria, it overlaps with anorexia. it's dysphoria, about body, the self, identity, image, your voice, your reflection in the mirror

section: "prelude"
i think one of the saddest parts of gender-dysphoria, is that, it literally changes how your eyes work.  like you aren't seeing the real you, on the outside. 

section: "tragedy"
you are actually more beautiful than dysphoria let's you see, and that's the saddest part. it's essentially, a vision-warp-debuff from video games, only this is real life, and it hurts way more becuase it's real.

but don't leave with that, if any tragedy is worth remembering -> it's that you're more beautiful than dysphoria let's you see with your own eyes.

if dysphoria were a person... i think they would be a beautiful person who really hates themselves becusae they think they are ugly, worthless and never good enough. the only way to combat dysphoria is with love, and you need the real kind. the kind of love only you can give yourself, only God, the source, the engine of life, can give you. that's what saved my life.
