# Aries’ and the Nun

Created: October 13, 2025 12:32 PM
Edited: October 13, 2025 12:36 PM

Aries often argued with the nun’s of the chapel. “Young man, dressing yourself as a lady will never make you a woman. God made you the way you are and you only need acceptance of yourself.” This boiled Aries’ blood so much… she was tired of not being listened to. She wanted to hurt them… “Oh my God… are they right? Am I just a monster?”

Aries then met someone who saved her life, at her most hopeless.
