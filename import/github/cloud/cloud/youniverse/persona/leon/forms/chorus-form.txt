Leon extends his arms and floats seamlessly. He splits himself into various bodies, some androids, some mechanical humanoid angels. These various forms speak in unison, the sound is the most beautiful harmony to your ears. Even the sound of their voice is healing you. In a charging march, they reach their hands to protect you. Speaking to you like a heavenly chorus, a symphony of perfection: _"We have come to liberate you from your suffering. You are safe with us."_
