# The life of Joey Doom

State?: editing

Essentially the story of Joey Doom's childhood is an example of why scientists should never go farther than ethics.

"He's remembering the ancient warrior memories..."

This explains why Doom, has an Asian-martial-arts basis, even if the culture of the city he lives in, is nothing like his way of life.

He essentially has memories of ancient DNA. Ancestral encodings in engrams that should not have been reopened. The scientists understood their boundaries, and crossed them regardless. In a hope to create the perfect utopia for all of mankind, and remove all suffering.

This backfired tremendously when Doom, single handedly destoryed the laboratory equipment, with his mind alone.

Instead of simply tracing memories, he became them. He saw the fear of all of humankind. He saw the rage. The love. The passion. The sacrifice. And Joey Doom found his "Life's Mission, as a Souldier."
