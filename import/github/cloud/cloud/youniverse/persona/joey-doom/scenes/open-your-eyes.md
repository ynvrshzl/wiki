Someone tells Doom: _"Open your eyes."_ Joey Doom, slowly opens his eyes. Light enters his eyes, darkness surround his pupils. Exhaustion. Years of torment. Shaped a human being into a disturbed warrior. Relentless in the search for "liberation." Doom, emotionlessly looks at the person. The person yells desperately: _"Can you not see that this life is full of pain?"_ Joey Doom remains expresionless. He resembles a ghost of a human. A shell of life. For a rare moment, Doom speaks: _"I am a souldier. There is no time left."_ The person is confused and furious.
