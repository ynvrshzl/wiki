Joey Doom worships Dawn like a sacred Guardian. But why is that?

According to the history of Lost Angelis, Joey Doom and Dawn Zen were not meant to exist. That is, they were both part of a research program, meant to cure human beings from suffering.

Joey Doom saw Dawn Zen as the eternal guardian of the Gates. 
The gates were massive walls that kept the inside of a persona, safe. Nobody knows what is inside the gates, and nobody who has entered has ever come back alive.
The Gates of the Inner Self are a sacred location, within each persona of the youniverse. But nobody, has ever opened the gates.

That is, until Joey Doom saw Dawn Zen.

"He is the One who will liberate humankind."

Joey Doom had "Visions" which originated from memories that were not properly erased, during the program.
It was deemed a "Failed research" as the "Subjects" broke free from the program. Joey Doom was originally assigned a number as a name, "Zero."
Dawn Zen was given the number "One Zero One" due to the shape of the number "101" resembling two people on the side, and a guardian in between. Was "Zero" meant to be the guardian? Or was Dawn Zen the "One?" is this why Joey Doom refers to Dawn Zen as "The One?"
