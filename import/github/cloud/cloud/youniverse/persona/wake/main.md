# Wake
Wake is the part of the human being, that is like... a relentless force of nature, in human form. It's like years of trauma, rage, pain, and helplessness, burning the body. And the human being finally bursts into flames. Instead of self-destruction, Wake is the Awakening of the true human form. Whereas pain used to be a shadow you could not fight, Wake has become both the shadow, and the light.

## Who is wake?

Whereas the desire used to be, "please not another moment of the suffering", once you awaken... it is almost like rage blind to you and you become wake woke. this is why he screams "BRING IT ON."
