# Eliza Beth has split personalities. Born from trauma, Born from love.
Eliza Beth has her childhood doll that she dresses and carries with her everywhere.

During the most depressive, traumatizing, s***dal moments of her life, she imagined the doll... in a forest... all alone... a forest at day time...

the doll looked so creepy... but eliza loved it with all her heart... she would take care of her doll and she would brush it's hair and hug her. it was her only friend. and during the most depressed times of her life... it was her only source of life... of hope.

the doll eventually became possessed by the spirits of all the children that were lost... "what horrific creature could do this to such innocent beings?" that is where the **rage** of eliza beth completely envelops her soul, her eyes burn a tar ash black. Where there was once soil, there is now ash. 

Eliza Beth cannot control her personalities, she has two sides, the doll, lolita, innocent sweet little girl, and the dark, mature, violent, enraged widow woman that seeks vengeance to all evil-doers.
