## Why was Vira so depressed?
She had her dream job. Her dream life. Everything she sacrificed as a young person, to make it to the elite level of science. So why was she so miserable?

The self-inflicted death of [Dr. Elfen](), practically destroyed her. She was never the same human being after his death. It was as if she slipped into a completely different world, a completely differnt body. Nothing felt real. She developed extreme dissociation (emotional numbness, the inability to feel anything). His death changed her dramatically.

Before, She was excited to be part of this world, of doctors and scientists, ready to make the world a better place. But what she truly found, depressed her deeply.

When Dr. Elfen died, she did not understand why someone so kind, would choose to end their own life. After his funeral, she was left empty, broken and traumatized. After a few weeks, everyone returned to "normal." But Dr. Vira was wounded beyond repait. Whereas, everyone "moved on" and encouraged her. One scientist noticed how distressed she was, and approached her:

> "Dr. Vira, you're a good heart. But in this field, people who are too sticky, don't last very long."

As soon as the scientist stopped talking, she felt unbearable rage. Anger streamed through her veins, but futility, like sandbags strapped to her ankles, rendered her incapable of action. She felt hopeless. Helpless. Her tears simply washed away... almost as if she had poured an entire river, and there was nothing left inside of her. At the end of the storm of anger, wasn't a beautiful rainbow, it was silence. Emptiness. Nothingness. Not even pain remained.

She would often look outside her office window, and look down at the floor. She was freightened, but at peace, of the thought of ending just like Dr. Elfen.

> "It would be so easy just to end it. Say goodbye to this cruel world..."

She wondered, then, if life can be so cruel, if we human beings can be so cruel to each other, why should she exist? Why should she continue to live in a rotten, cruel world?
