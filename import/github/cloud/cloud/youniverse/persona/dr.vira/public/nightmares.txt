Dr. Vera had nightmare-dreams. They were difficult for her to study and understand... because it was signs from her body that she was in danger and needed to get help as soon s possible.

"I dream of jumping off the science laboratory I work in. It's a giant window, with the perfect view of the outside world. In the dreams, I always want to smash through window... but I can't seem to touch it. It's like there's a world behind the glass that I can see but never touch. My nightmares always end up in my jumping off the window because I can't take it anymore... but every single time right before I fall to the ground... I wake up."
