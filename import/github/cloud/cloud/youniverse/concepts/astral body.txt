in the universe, especially in the rebellion and the gang of LA stories, there is a concept of the astral body. this is the purest form of the human soul. if each persona has a one-core personification, the astral body is the autonomous personification.

in other words, this means that the powers of each persona, come from the astral body.

to enter the astral body, the persona must embody a personification. that means all other things must become integrated into a body.
