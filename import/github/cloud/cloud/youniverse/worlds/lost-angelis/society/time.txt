in Lost angelis, becuase numbers are forbidden in the public, time is one of the fundamental things that is replaced with a natural language to tell time.

essentially, time has sources of anchor points throughout the day, designed by the most intelligent psychologists and mathmeticians of the founding members of ls.  "meridian" means "middle." to tell time from this anchor, the people of angelis speak "proto-meridian" as "morning" and "post-meridian" as "evening".
