In Lost Angelis, the dream was to completely destory the old ways of human life, as they nearly destroyed life itself.
-> This meant no money. No stock. No economics. No hierarchial systems.
-> However, as the city began to grow in health and people, the founding family realized, there would soon be another war. If there was no group to provide control, there would be disaster.
-> This was the first compromise of the founders. They believed in no systems whatsoever, however, the founding scientists and 'lesser' members, urged the founders to form branches of government.
