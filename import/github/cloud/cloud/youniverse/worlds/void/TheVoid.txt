"The Void"
the origin of the youniverse is essentially a cycle. there is no timeline beginning or end, but rather cycles, the beginning of one cycle is the end of another. and so on. essentially nothing can stop the haunting rhythms of time. in the universe, they are several elements that are invisible and yet they are the basis of all existence. time, space, energy and mass.



trauma in essence is a force that you cannot confront. it's something that casts a shadow that you can never touch... but it always follows you... and so in essence logically the only way to remove the shadow is to remove the object that costs it, or even worse to remove the light source all together. this is essentially the logic of the void. the void is pure logic pure order. whereas the light is pure chaos, pure diversity. paradoxically one would think of the void as entropy and chaos swallowing life, but actually life is only possible because of the harmony between chaos and order.
