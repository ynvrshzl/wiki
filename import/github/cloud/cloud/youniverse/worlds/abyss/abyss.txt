Philosophically... the Abyss is infinite. It has no time nor space. It is an echo-chamber of darkness, doom, despair and torment.

But also, it has the darkest, most abandoned parts of the persona-self. 

This means integration is only possible when you willingly descend into your own darkness and face what you have abandoned.

It is not necessarily evil, but extremely dangerous. The human being is not designed to withstand an infinite free-fall. We need a source of gravity, just like stars. Otherwise, we disintegrate into chaos.
