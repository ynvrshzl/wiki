# Modals of The Human Engineer
Abstract: it is important to understand the modes of a human engineering, becusae it guides the work of an engineer, ethically, and helps avoid lost energy and time, working in the wrong areas.

There are two fundamental modes of engineering:
- Edit mode
- View mode

it's basically an engineer version of the author-reader mode. The engineer has no idea if their work is of any practical human use, until they switch from editing mode, to viewing mode. so far, that's what's helped us avoid deep hours lost in engineering.
