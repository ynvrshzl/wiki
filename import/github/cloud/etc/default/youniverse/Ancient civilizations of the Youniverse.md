# The Ancient Civilizations of the Youniverse


It's paradoxical because we see, at civilization scale, what cellular life, the smallest forms of life, express. cells divide and conquer and protect and sacrifice and destroy and infect and heal and grow. we see the same patterns scattered across all grasps of life. 

Fundamentally, what is left is Consciousness. Will. Choice. Some civilizations, some personas in the universe argue or believe that there is no such thing as freedom or choice. that everything is predestined and time doesn't exist. and other personas believe that there is Free Will and that we all have a responsibility as moral conscious creatures. and that we cannot escape the effects of our choices. and that everything is deeply interconnected beyond even what our instruments can relay to our senses. 

And then there are very few personas that simply share, in their own words, whether we have free will or not, we are just arguing theoretical semantics. what is real is what matters most.
