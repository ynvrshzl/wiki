# Meaning as Medicine.
"Time as a component of healing"
## Recently, i've observed that regardless of external factors, regulating my internal eco-system is just as powerful as external factors.

> [!note]
> So in summary, livign meaningful days seems to be a natural remedy, healing agent, or prevention function, of health decline in the human being

Our personal observered components of a meaningful life:
- Significance in the smallest pockets of life
- Integration with some kind of real, physical contact, even if digital, aslong as we are seeing other human beings... our social systems are regulated. This is evident by the fact that my health severely downgraded when I surrounded myself with games and no human contact. We need some kind of human contact to keep our deep primal systems regulated. I don't like socializing in real life becuase it's painful, but seeing human beings in videos, seems to help just fine. 

Personally, i've observed that even if i don't go outside for a perioud of time, and have reduced human social contact, aslong as I give myself a varied day, with lot's of experiences, however I can create that for myself (mostly using technology + routines)... my days become memorable and therefore weeks become memorable, and when time becomes significant... i think the enviornment becomes meaningful... and life expands... which is the opposite of what depression does, which is a narrowing of perception into naturally dark aspects of existence.
