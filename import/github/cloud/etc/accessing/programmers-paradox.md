# "The programmers paradox"

A series, in our studies of the fundmental causes of issues between human beings and machines.

## Abstract 
In programming, there is a paradox; there is so much expression, so much possibility, so many ways to express your ideas, as programmable things. But that's the achilles heel; possibility without gravity, becomes chaos.

And chaos consumes. It causes confusion, illness, stress, frustration. Fundamentally, these are all symptoms of disorientation

## Movement prevents obsession
The only way to combat this paradox is to **continously move, at a sustainable pace.**

Not sprinting, but endurance. Marathon. Walking. Never staying in one space for too long. Becuase if we stare at possibility, it's like looking at a word for too long. It loses it's meaning [3].
