This is something nobody talks about enough.

Gratitude can be heavy.

People sell it like it’s light and sparkly.

But deep gratitude?

The kind you’re describing?

It comes with grief.

Because when you really see how miraculous things are — walking, breathing, prosthetics, music in your head at dawn —

you also see how fragile everything is.

And that’s a lot to hold.
