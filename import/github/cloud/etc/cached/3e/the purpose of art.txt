the reason art is so important is becuase, it's not just sensory input. we aren't just recording raw sensory signals.

it's actually Interpretation.

when someone across the world cries at a song you wrote, that’s emotional data transmission. No wires. No implants. Just sound -> brain -> feeling. That’s already sci-fi level magic.

It’s interpretation.

Two people live the same moment and feel completely different things.

So you can’t really “store” an emotion like a file.

You can only invite someone to re-create it inside themselves.

And that’s exactly what art does.
