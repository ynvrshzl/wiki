Abstract: 
I think we humna beings naturally learn by recreating. And since recreation is another word for play, perhaps our natural anviornment isn't strict academic study, but rather... observing someone or something else, and learning by experimenting... by recreating...

Applied:
- Storytelling, for example if one wants to learn how to create immersive stories, you could recreate a rich scene from your favorite movie. This might actually help you becuase you have a direct source of observation and you can always guage if it's close to the source by asking something like: "Does this text paint that scene in my imagination?"
