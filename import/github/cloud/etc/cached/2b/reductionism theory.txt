Reductionism is harmful when you apply it to whole things.

Like reducing an adorable puppy to just DNA. Reductionism is harmful when we apply it to whole things. 

I think this is becuase human beings have a highly specific local maxima (idk how to express this... it's pretty much our phsycological, somatic, emotional field-of-view) so when we reduce anything, including technology, to it's fundamental parts, and judge the whole from that, our perception actually follows the same pattern -> you start to see pixels instead of images -> you start to see shapes instead of letters -> you start to hear sound instead of spoken language.
