# Burnout
Why does it matter to understand this?

I think, fundamentally, Burnout is the enemy against prolongued, sustainable progress in any field. But more imoprtantly, when you are healing from trauma, chronic pain, or just recovering, burnout is like a shadow that continously creeps on your sunny skies. It seems to follow, no matter where you go. I think it's at the worst when you are irritable, sleep-deprived, and really, don't feel anything inside. Nothing motivating. Nothing pushing you forward. No spark. That's one of the scariest places to be, becuase it's errily siimlar to being stuck in an Abyss or Void.

## What is it? 
Defining Burnout

Burnout can be defined in many domains. In our most natural habitat, (i'm looking at you, Science nerds) burnout can be defined as unsustainable prolonged output, without enough sustainable input.
