1. Radix – Latin for “root.”
Clean. Strong. Root-layer thinking.
2. Fundamentum – foundation, base.
But that might feel too literal.
3. Logia Prima – “first logic” or “primary discourse.”
4. Ars Substrata – “the art of the underlying layer.”
5. Scriptura – not just writing, but inscription. The act of encoding reality.
6. Forma Mater – “mother form.” The form from which forms emerge.
7. Initium – origin, beginning — but active.
