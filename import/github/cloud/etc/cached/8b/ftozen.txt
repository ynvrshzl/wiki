it's pretty cold right now. but I'm realizing I don't feel cold unless I stay still. I think that's symbolically and physically accurate for when you are battling mental illness... or even just facing a storm of painful events. I think when we start ruminating that's a sign that some part of us is freezing. when we stay still we feel the pain more than we have to, and so when I'm moving around I feel less of the cold. the hardest part isn't to move to stay warm but rather to get up in the first place because in order to get up the human being needs a reason. because from the moment we get out of our comfort cocoon and start moving around there will be a period where the pain will be magnified. and that used to be justified with a reason.
