# the language of the youniverse

I sometimes ask myself, how is it that not only, are all human subjects and Fields interconnected, but also that a polymath can see them?

if there were such thing as the language of the universe, I believe all studies of human field would be formed into a super language, probably an ecosystem of language. 

and the reason this matters is, fundamentally, back to the core of our life's work here, what matters most in human life is [Ethics.]()

and so if we are to reduce suffering, we have to understand the system we live in. when we form a language, we have symbolic translation layers, from the system we live in, to our insight. and from there we can formulate solutions. it could be in the form of structures or conceptual models, or even just making really cool fucking art. 

that leads us to another radical idea, because we live in a interconnected , something is always connected to something else. That's the only way that things can't exist in this universe, through co-existence. co-authoring. nothing is self-creating. nothing is perpetual. That's a radical idea because it means the properties of existence as a human being, suffering included, are not perpetual. they are not immortal eternals structures. they are properties, not entities. 

This might be where language reaches its terminal velocity, because we have understood through unspeakable pain, that there seems to be something in this universe that actually hates existence. in our personal universe stories we describe this as chaos, the void. The reason the voice is so destructive is because it exists entirely and isolation, and it infests and eats the host from the inside out like a parasite. That's why we most important part of the human being always begins on the inside. That's where true healing begins. 

And that's ultimately why it's so important to understand the language of the universe, because we can use language to navigate the world we live in and reduce suffering. The goal is not to completely eradicate suffering, and live in some mythical utopia, I think the goal itself should always be to do what's right no matter how painful it is. The world we live in actually only exists because of coexistence, and because agents of sacrifice and virtue, they give justice where there is only cruelty. I really fundamentally think the only reason our world is even existing right now, is because there are people who refuse to become the monsters that have been created in them. whether trauma or suffering, or just bad luck. whatever the causes may be, suffering of any kind, for a human being, always changes the structure. 

That's what's important to have a language for that structure itself, the structure of the inner world of human being. I believe that's what we're exploring here as well. not only a super language that combines all human fields to understand the world we live in, but also language that helps express the life that is inside, the world we live inside. That's the concept of the inner youniverse.
