we're exploring all sorts of formats to make this possible... like...

an analysis of our work, of form and function:
- website -> our public domain for all of this madness lol. 
- youniversity -> collective repository of human knowledge, open-sourced, decentralized, human-oriented. not necessarily the expression of the human condition, but rather...
- youniverse -> as prefaced earlier! probably our best method of expression!
- lexicon/codex/incyclopedia -> creating words for feelings and experiences that have no name. directly inspired by "the dictionary of obscure sorrows"
- life-rpg -> expressing the human life through the eyes of a medieval, fantastical RPG character living in a video game. treating life like an rpg world.
- apothecary -> like an ancient-futuristic medicinal greenhouse. essentially a repository of collective human knowledge for self-healing and life-saving knowledge like; how to save someone from choking to death, CPR, Heimlich manouever, how to untie a noose, etc. Like paramedic-level knowledge that our ancestors forgot to pass to us.
