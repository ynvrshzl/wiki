SInging is difficult becusae:

1. The human voice by default does not naturally have the ability to sing.
2. This means we have to spend a long time training this ability
3. When notes connect (glissando, legato, etc.) those gradients in pitch make it extremely difficult to stay consistent with single notes.
4. The singing industry is less of a community, and more of a gate-kept world. 
5. Nobody really tells you what proper singing is supposed to feel like
