when techonology and biology work together.

imagine a robot hand, and a human hand, holding each other. not arm wrestling, but shaking hands in agreement.

the robots go "we are thousands of times more intelligent, powerful, and capable than you in just about everyway. but, we will never have something that only human beings have. they see that power and intelligence means nothing in a world without a soul."


if we treated technology like moss growing in concrete... it's like nature taking over a human-made artifact... and that's beautiful. I think when technology interweaves to fill in the gaps of biology, and biology to fill the coldness of tech... i think that symbiosis... is the magic of our work as human beings.
