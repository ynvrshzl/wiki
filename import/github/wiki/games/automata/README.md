# Automata
A game about an inner youniverse of the human being, emotions, memories and thoughts as elements. 

## Significance of title
Automata - "machine imiation of human life." The term automata comes from the Greek word *`αὐτόματος`*, meaning "self-acting, self-moving, or acting of one’s own will"

## Premise
"Can you find harmony and balance in the youniverse of a persona, before chaos consumes the light?"

## Significance
This is like our own little youniverse sandbox game where we record our observations about emotions, concepts and more, and how they ultimately sustain a human being, especially when dealing with trauma and damage. That's the main purpose of the game. The second ethical purpose is; to explore the deepest philosophical, parts of human life, in a mythical, narrative lens, where you play with elements and see their reactions like chemical chains!

## Ethics
Stress itself isn't always bad, evil is defined by context, and joy isn't always good.

Stress in isolation is -negative -> Stress + Care = a sign of meaning. And meaning is **the most important thing for the Youniverse**
