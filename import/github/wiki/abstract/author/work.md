# Work as Perspective Expression
Most of the work on our website, could be thought of as __different forms of expression.__ It's not all ethical. It's not all about human suffering. Sometimes, it's purely joyful. That's an important distinction, especially with a project of this scale.

From the words we imagined, to our various theories, especially our stories, these are all expressions of the same thing (life) through different perspectives (mediums). Words are a form of expression. Music is a form of expression. Theories are a really crazy, wierd form of expression, and so  on...
