# Mechanics of Depression
- dp (depression), as a functional mechanical structure, is an echo-chamber. reverberating only the negative parts of life, and deafening the rest of life. until all that remains is a black void of meaninglessness and death.
- the paradox is that delusion can only be remedies through contact with life. through friction. but dp not only magnifies negatives, it steals away your energy. your desire to live. everything becomes colorless, because emotions are the colors of human life.

# In order to protect oneself against the harms of mental illness, we must first understand the mechanics, from lived experience, not purely external analysis.
