# Family, relationships, and social life
In my adulthood, I chose to recluse myself from the world. I would spend months at a time, completetly isolated, but I was doing the most important work: listening to myself, and healing my inner child's wounds. Refusing to talk to my family. Refusing to talk to anyone. I felt like I had nobody, but that wasn't true: There was someone, since the very beginning, who knew me better than anyone else. Me. That was the beginning of true self-love, and true inner healing.


### Healing was painful, but i'd rather feel everything, than nothing at all.
That quote, is actually inspired by a very special song to me, [Ariana Grande: Hampstead.]()
