# The cure to ecxistentia crisis
In this writing, I share what saved my life from severe existential crises.

Existential crises. Sometimes, it's caused by medical conditons, accidents or something external. But rarely, a person could travel & descend so far within their own psyche, just by thinking, they can go insane. 

That's not a surprise, for a human being to drive themselves insane. Again, mental illness plays a major role here, but it can also happen when we are isolated from other human beings. For some reason, we cannot isolate ourselves & cut off social contact. No matter how comfortable or safe we may feel, being isolated sucks. Litreally.

However, what if you can't socialize in your current life? Is it better to be alone than with people that hurt you?


This is what saved my life. I was literally two steps away from jumping off a building, but something, somewhere inside me kept me alive. 

Life. Life is an impossible thing. Actually it's happening right now... and then i'll just... stop... or something else will happen... gosh this is confusing. I love to start there, with that word. Life. Whenever I am deeply afraid or anxious about living in such a vast space, I jump back to the ape-like, human part: feeling, emotion, sensation.

That's how we recieve our input, through feelings, and sensations. I have to remind myself I can't save anyone's life, but I can share what saved mine.

One of the worst parts of existential crises is death anxiety. Somehow, it's like depression completely inverted and now, i'm afraid to die. Whenever I am sleep deprived + napping, this feeling is amplified to such depths, that words only mute the feeling.

I don't reccomend sleep depriving yourself, but it is a method of treating depression, and one that also, saved my life. I still have access to my emotions. That's incredibly important
