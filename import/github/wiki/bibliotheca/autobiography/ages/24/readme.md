# Autobiography, age 24
Recently, I've realized we survived psychological hell... but somehow I am grateful and at peace. How did this happen? I want to share it!

## The most powerful form of knowledge - Integration
You know what my attitude towards my life is recently? ever since we survived depression... we imagined a Life vision that was so powerful... it's almost like we've integrated into us. like I haven't forgotten, but at the same time we don't really remember every day why we wake up early. we just do it. I think automatic behavior like this is a form of integrated knowledge but I believe true integrated knowledge is more than just autonomic muscle memory. I think it's consciousness plus muscle memory. because muscles don't really have memory but consciousness does. and history encodes meaning, and so memory reveals why you do things. which is extremely important when you are healing from depression or trauma, and you don't have support or anyone in your life. you do have someone, yourself. 

and my attitude towards life is like, well we're here for the Long haul I'm looking at myself in the mirror so adorable princess queen girly.
