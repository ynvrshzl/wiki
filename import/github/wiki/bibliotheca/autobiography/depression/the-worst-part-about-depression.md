### the-worst-part-about-depression.md

The worst part about depression, isn't the emotional numbmess, or the corpse body feeling, for me, the worst part about depression is how much it sucks away from you. It especially removes you wanting to care about yourself. You can't even want to care anymore, just nothing means anything to you anymore. It's all so incredibly exhausting, and you don't have the strength to fight anymore. You don't want to get out of bed. You don't want to

The first step to healing from d for me was finding the external & internal reasons for my body wanting to die.
