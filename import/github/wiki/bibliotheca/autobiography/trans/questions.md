Questions that helped me transition:

1. If you could choose to be anybody you want to be, who would you be? Why would you want to live life as that person? 
2. Do you want to live the rest of your life through the eyes of a woman, or a man?
3. If you were to avoid transitioning, would you regret it when you die?
4. Would it hurt more to regret transitioning, or regret never having tried to transition?
5. Why are you afraid to transition?
6. If they love you, they will accept you. Do you believe you are worthy of love, even if it means letting go of people who pretend to love you? Are you ready to be your true self? Are you ready to see the truth in yourself & in the world around you?
