The universe is beyond comprehension. Yet our monkey brains are desperate to understand it.

I realized something profound yet stupidly simple: If the universe is so big, and it makes me want to die, then I can zoom back into my life, and forget the universe even exists.

This is, undoubtedly, ironic, as most of my work focuses on the youniverse... but it's not... the universe out there, it's the *you*niverse in you, in me.

Suffering, especially cruel, evil, are all things that made me want to die. Recently I survived a suffocating existential death. But I feel part of me has been reborn... not in a glorious pheonix kind of way... more of a subtle "I just don't fucking care anymore" type of attitude. That's one of the mst difficult things about pain, it's impossible to truly express it with words. It's difficult to even remember it.
