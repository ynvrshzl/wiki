# Why should I keep living, if i'm dead inside?
That will be the most difficult question that you could ever ask in your life, and even more so, to find a good answer to. But the answer does indeed exist, inside the Youniverse... inside you. The answer already exists inside you, and only while you're alive... that's the biggest reason [I started the Youniverse to begin with.](why-i-started-the-youniverse.md)


Many people give up too early — not because they’re weak, but because no one ever told them that this painful clarity… is a transition.
