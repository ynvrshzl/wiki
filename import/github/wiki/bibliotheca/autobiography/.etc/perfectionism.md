# Perfectionism
**The perfect thing does exist, but not for us.**

The perfect "thing" does indeed exist... the perfect tool, the perfect method, the perfect technique, but the problem is, it does not exist in our dimension. Here's what I mean: if you have a problem in your life, that you're trying to solve, it exists as a 4-dimensional thing. What does that mean? Basically, we can only ever see cross-sections of things
