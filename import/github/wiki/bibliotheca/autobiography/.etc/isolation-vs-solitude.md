# Isolation vs. Solitude

I know you want to be alone but we really are nothing without each other. so tell me why tell me why I hate people so much? I don't think you really hate people, I think you just hate feeling alone while you're around so much. you need to create a place where you belong find people like you. find someone who feels the same way you do.

I woke up on a alternate universe. all my depression all my anxiety all the trauma all the pain washed away as if it had never happened. I slip into the dream it's a beautiful sunny morning I hear the birds outside my body feels so warm and cozy underneath these bed sheets is it so unfair for me to feel this happy? I feel so naturally good I want more of life I feel like I'm falling in love with myself I wake up and somehow I know my daily routine like the back of my hand. then I see the family my beautiful family they're all eating downstairs and there's a chair just for me.

Are you lonely or alone? I've heard so many times "it's okay to ask for help." But you can't always ask for help, and sometimes, people who try to "help" actually end up hurting you even deeper. So what's left? There are ways to not only survive on your own, but become in tuen with your truest nature. 

"The loneliness epidemic" feels like a modern problem, but sages, monks, saints, philosophers, our very ancestors thousands of years ago, would spend years alone, in search for meaning. We have, as a human society become civilized as a whole, but you will quickly realize that is a fragile egg shell, and someday, it will crack open.
