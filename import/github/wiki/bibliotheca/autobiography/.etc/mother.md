# I'm my own mother now
I didn't want to believe it, much less, accept it, that perhaps, the family that 'so loved me & protected me from the nasty, big bad world' perhaps shattered me more than helped me become a whole being. Life happens, I get it. Trauma here, there, everywhere. One bad accident could change your entire life, but the same can be said for one good moment, one good decision, one song, one poem, one lyric, one life. Many things have saved my life along the way, wether it was the symphony of my teenage enlightenment, backed by heavy metal screams & guitars. The endless worlds of games like Minecraft, Skyrim, Oblivion and more. Or even something as simple as staying awake for 24+ hours, just so I could help myself feel deeply powerful emotions [(read more about that here.)](sleep-deprivation-healing.md)
