"Fission theory"
In nuclear theory, "fission" is the act of a cell splitting into smaller parts. This happens to conserve energy. 

In my life, I often realize I can't do the big things I envision... atleast not now. But I can apply my own "fission" to parts of  my life... they are still going to make a whole someday... even if it takes a little longer


The gap between what you imagine and what you can do... that's painful... and for some it's unbearable.

The more fluent you become in your tools, your thinking style, your writing style, your organizational logic — the less energy each action drains.
Fluency is like reducing friction.
It makes expression feel like breathing instead of wrestling.
