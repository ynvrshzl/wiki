# Youniverse
Essentially this is our main storyverse in our websystem.

This directory contains all of our literature ideas, processed works, and work ready to be published on our site.

This is the domain we spend the most time in. Metabolizing theory, personal lived observations, philosophy, and sometimes being absurdly silly.

It's a chaotic, careful balance we're constantly fine-tuning, learning from the anime that influenced us. Aswell as pulling from all different sources of great human art, like they're asteroids and stars, of cosmic stardust matter. Pulling them into our central gravity field, and giving birth to a collective youniverse of the most important parts of being human.
