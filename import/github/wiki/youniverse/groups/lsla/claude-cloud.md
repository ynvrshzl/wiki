# Occupation
Claude is the head chief of the [LSLA]() 

# Summary of Life

> "Crime has dropped, near-zero since we updated our systems. The children deserve a better life than what we had." 

He experienced unbearable crime, trauma, and pain. Witnessing the worst sides of humankind left him broken beyond repair. And yet, he realized perhaps it was his purpose. "This is why I was sculpted."

# Personified
Mastery.

Claude "Cloud" is mastery personified. He is the study of a human being, conquering the world inside, before the world outside.

# Personality
His features reflect his personality; He's incredibly stoic, resillient, and fatherly. His sharp features also embody resillience and mastery over one's emotions, desires and imperfections.

Amongst these, Cloud often emphasized anger, to be a posion you either swallow yourself, or fuel into your work. He never explained "how" exactly you fuel it in your work... perhaps that was the real lesson... to discover one's way, through the shaping and pain of life.

He was sculpted not by clean hands, but by the catastrophes of human life. He often described people to be like a block of stones. Perfect when shapeless, but life chips away at you until you have shape. His description of the crimes committed by human beings, is "some people sacrifice themselves, some people shape themselves, and other people... some people just want to smash the stone."
 
> "We... we're alot like stones... marble. We're perfect at the beginning. Square. Not a drop of madness in a baby. But the longer you live, if you refuse to be shaped by the sculptor, you're perfectly square. It's stubborn. It's shapeless. You can't serve anyone when the criminals show up. Gotta break and chip away at it, till' you have shape. Till you've got form. Stone alone can't move. A mind, shaped by pain, sculpted by life, can move mountains."

# Philosophy
His philosophy was simple; we are shaped by pain, and we must not resist. We must show up for our duty, and become as strong as we can, to and protect the innocent.

# Appearance
He has stylized, sharp features. Short-length white hair and facial hair. Aged around his mid 60s, aged incredible for his age. His body is as sculpted as his philosophy; sharp, accurate, and flexible.


# Inspiration
He's inspired by the white guy detective with glasses that looks like ken kaneki, from Tokyo Ghoul S4

# Role
Investigation branch. 

# Relations
He is a colleague chief of Officer Nihil Mayer. He works in the investigation branch of the Law System of Lost Angelis (LSLA)

He becomes closer to Officer Mayer, after remembering how isolated and traumatized he himself was, when he first entered the world of Law. That's when he spent time with Officer Mayer, and gave him the support he wish he had. He could have bee bitter and resentful, and for the longest time, he was. But something... someone changed him.

Later on, he was like a father for Officer Mayer. 

# Personality 
A stoic, badass attitude. So much so, that in his death, soaked in blood, defeated, he regrets nothing in his life.
