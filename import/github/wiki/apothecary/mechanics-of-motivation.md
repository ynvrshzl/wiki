# Mechanics of Motivation
The mechanics of motivation are critical to warding off Depression, and other disorders.

This is because motivation is actually not just "I feel like doing abc." Motiation is actually **The core driver of living things.** Without motivation to sleep or feed oneself

Thus, we must first inspect the source of motivation, and the source of things that are chewing on your motivation. We can consider these things "void parasites," mythologically speaking.
