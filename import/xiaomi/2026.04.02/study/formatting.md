## Chapter
The chapter is more of a literature structure, but it's is essentially The  literature version of a scene in a movie. a chapter is like an episode. it contains complex ideas in a sequence of narrative. 

The chapter is still a fundament idea in this stud, because we are understanding how we work with complexity at the plain text level which is essentially the recorded form of spoken language.

## Typography 
(e.g. style, typefaces, color, weight)

since we are dealing with plain text, naturally experimenting with the style of letters and colors, is a human creative process, for something that is incredibly psychological, structured, technical, cognitive and complicated; language. 

type typography is an entire field. type face designers focus on every single aspect that you could imagine that makes text legible. and it's an awkward field because it's split into physical typography, like that printed on magazines, as well as visual typography which deals with screen sizes and technology ambiguity. 


Regardless of medium and application, typography has a creative function in the art of literature and human written language.
