# Soul crisis, Existential crisis, Spiritual desperation
Have you ever lived for a time in your life, in absolute hopelessness? That feeling that you're trapped in your own consciousness, and nobody else can come & rescue you from yourself. They can't even see what you're going through. That is "silent suffocation" or as I've come to learn, a **Soul crisis.**

Is there a solution? Yes, and it doesn't involve taking one's own life or the lives of other beings. There is a very clear distinction to me, that there is a physical hierarchy to a human being's life. There are foundational elements and things that create this experience, and this knowledge is what saved my life.

It seems like the only escape is death, but I assure you, unless you are in a terminal condition, all you need is one answer to fix your entire life. Do you want to fix your life? That itself, is the most important question you'll ever ask. That's why I always tell myself ["the hardest thing i've ever done, is chosen to stay alive, when I had thousands of opportunities to end my life."](the-hardest-thing-ive-ever-done.md)

# Tools that saved my life
- **Music:** I use music as a medatative, evoking tool in my life. I soak in the atomospheres, late at night, writing, coding, and searching my soul for answers. 
- **Imagination:** Remnants of my imagination

This is why I say music, anime, video games saved my life, and will continue to save my life until I draw my very last breath. It's becuase these things are deeply emotional. And when I've got nothing left to live for in this world, these are the only things that keep me alive.
