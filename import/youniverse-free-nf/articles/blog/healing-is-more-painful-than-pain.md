title: Healing is more painful than pain
---


How is it possible that healing from pain, becomes more painful than the pain itself? If you can reach the end of the cycle, the light at the end of the tunnel, you will come back to life. I believe one of the many reasons we were created is to find the reason to make the suffering of life, have reason. When you discover your reason, it will change you in ways these words cannot reach. My reason is that I simply love life and I believe a happy child is worth all the suffering I can take. If this is the price I have to pay for consciousness, THEN LET THE BLOOD IN MY VERY VEINS SPELL IT OUT: I LOVE LIFE.
