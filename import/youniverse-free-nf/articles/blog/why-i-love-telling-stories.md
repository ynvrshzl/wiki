Why writing stories is so powerful for me.

I believe with all my being, that emotional stories are the medicine that saved my life. For today's Godless world, I believe in the ancient power of language & art. Studying some of the oldest known human civilizations, that's what I discovered most. Amogngst the horrific traditions & injustices, stories were what kept everything together.

**Stories help me to make  order outside of my chaos inside.**
I have a very noisy, stormy mind. Throughout the day, i get thousands of conversations, stories & scenes playing in my imagination.

**Stories help me to pretend i'm someone else.**
I also wish I could be the personas. Some part of me wishes I could be a hikikomori like [Magdelene]() or be as resilient as [Officer Nihil]() The original purpose of the personas were to express parts of me that I can't in my waking life, and I'm grateful that I found a way to cope with my multiple personalities, and stormy inner self.
