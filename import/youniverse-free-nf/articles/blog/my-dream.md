**My dream is to share the stories in my heart.** The most painful moments in my life, have been captured in my life's work. But also the most beautful, silly, romantic, daydreams. I hope to share this with many of you someday. *I know this will save many lives becuase these stories saved my life.* I'm doing it cause I love it. I'm doing this, becuase I love life. :) 


Here's a little short story I wrote about the kind of impact I want to make with my life's work. Enjoy!

Today is my 17th birthday. I woke up with this pain in my stomach again... it felt so awful I couldn't move. The night before, I wished that I wouldn't have woken up. I can't imagine how awful that would have been. Missing out on this gift... I looked at my desk, and I couldn't believe what I saw. My parents, with the little money they had left, bought me a ticket to go see the new Youniverse movie. I instantly jumped out of my bed man. I love these characters. They mean so much to me. They helped me during the most painful parts of my life. I can't believe my parents got me this for my birthday. I actually felt sadness in my chest, thinking about my parents. They are so hard & strict on me. But with the little they had left, they chose to give that to me. And you know... that's amazing.I went ot the theatre, and there was the prettiest girl with pink hair. I tried my best to keep my eyes on the screen, but I kept glancing over her... She was so beautiful. When the movie ended, Yami was on screen, before he died. He said to the viewers: "There are a million reasons to end your life, but all you need is one reason, to keep living." I decided to ask her something... and as soon as I looked over to her... she was already staring at me, smiling. I can't even hide how nervous I was... It took years, but we got close & made a deep relatonship. All because of a movie we both love. She told me all about her life and how much she's suffered.
