If you are reading this, and you're in a crisis/have been in one, i want to start by saying I am in your shoes, I know what it feels like to want to die, while your body is screaming to survive. I'm so sorry, but most people you meet, will never have the capacity to comprehend the immense amount of suffering you have experienced, and it's not their fault. Before the pain, you had no awareness that life could get this difficult. In much the same way, we can never go back to who we used to be, and that's the first step in healing, realizing we have to become something new... an older, wiser, more complete psyche.
