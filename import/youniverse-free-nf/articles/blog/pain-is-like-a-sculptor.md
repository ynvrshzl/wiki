 I feel like God treat some of his children the same way a sculptor would refine their art. Everyone knows that you can't keep the beautiful piece of marble forever, you have to chip away at it and break off pieces. And that's the part that requires the most skill because from the outside it just seems like a maniac is destroying this beautiful marble. But only the sculptor understands the process and through faith, sculptor keeps chipping and breaking until what is left is a beautiful piece of art. Sure the marble was beautiful to begin with, but the sculptor created something that can never be replicated by accident. I feel like God treats us the same way. In fact it is my faith — that God lets some of us go through pain — that is keeping me alive everyday right now!
