# Consciousness
consciousness is awareness. in much the same way, we start out with life as a blur. we don't pay attention to details we simply experience things as they come. but the more life we experience, the more we evolve, the more consciousness becomes aware of the details in life. almost like a blurry image being sculpted into form. the deeper your consciousness, but more of life you get to see and the more experience you get to be aware of. the details of Life are theoretically infinite. like a Mandelbrot fractal, there is no end to consciousness or awareness.

This is why consciousness must be disciplined but not enslaved. A sandbox, not a cage. Playing within meaningful limits. Those who do not channel their consciousness to something worthwhile, something that gives their life meaning, something that you love living for, if you don't use your consciousness at best it will torment you, and at worse your consciousness will be the destruction and death of things around you.
