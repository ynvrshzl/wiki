# Arguments against non-existence.

If life can be filled with unbearable suffering (torture, disease, death, etc.) then why would the benefits of existing outweight never having been born in the first place?

The biggest argument against non-existence, that has saved my life, is that human beings are the only creatures in existence that can solve problems that are naturally occuring. Even without human evil, suffering & pain is still a problem found all throughout nature.
