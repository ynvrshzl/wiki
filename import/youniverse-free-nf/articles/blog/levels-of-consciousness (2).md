# Levels of consciousness
![](https://images.pexels.com/photos/724994/pexels-photo-724994.jpeg?auto=compress&cs=tinysrgb&w=1260&h=750&dpr=1)

Don't you get that feeling sometimes? You're around lot's of people, but you feel so disconnected. You try to blend in, play small, and learn how to hide as much of yourself as you can, so they accept you. But no matter how hard you try, you can't ignore who you really are, and what you really see. They can feel it too, you're not like them, you're rare. Almost as if we're speaking different languages


[Carl Jung's](#carl-jung) work has helped me so much in this area of life. To understand that human beings can be both physical, and psychologicakl creatures. Spirituality emerges when we look at the world outside ourselves and begin to ask: "What is the origin of all of this? Where are we going? Is there a reason to be here?" These questions slowly chip away at the psyche until all that is left is the delicate, fragile insides


We are all operating on different levels of consciousness. There is a beginning level (childhood) and there is a final level of consciousness (???)


The more I love, the more I live. The more I see of the world around me and the people living in it, I see that very few human beings are good.


IF you live in an eniovnrment that is out of sync with your inner eniovnrment, it will hurt you, but it's not hopeless
