# How human beings learn

We recognize patterns. Think about it, when you see a foreign language, it looks alien. This is essentially the same in all knowledge fields. The process of learning is recognizing patterns & remembering what they mean.

Practical examples:
- In basketball -> learning the distance between you & the hoop is impossible to calculate, instead, we intuitively learn how far they can reach through thousands of hours of practice
- In learning languages -> both visually & auditorially
