# Dark truths about this world, and me.

1. Always be careful of someone who seems to know something about you, that you never told them.
2. Always be careful of someone who is selling you anything
3. Always seek to hear the person's story, rather than the false persona we all see on the outside.
4. There are dark, hidden truths about this world, if you seek them, they will find you.
5. The darkest, hidden truths about this world come from human nature itself.
