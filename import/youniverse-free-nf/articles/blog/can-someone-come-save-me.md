title: Can someone come save me?
---


Short answer: **Yes!**


The right person could come & save you, but if you don't put in the effort to make your existence known, they won't be able to find you.


Is it possible for someone to save you from a terrible situation in your life? I've seen stories of people who were homeless, *and all it took was one, right person to come into their life, and completely transform their life.*


So that alone tells me it's real: people can come into your life & help you. But this idea that we need to be saved, is a deeper need: **what we really mean is we need to be rescued from a toxic enviornment.**
