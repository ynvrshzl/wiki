title: The healing power stories
---

I couldn't travel, or see anyone, or have friends. But in my stories, I tapped into my deep, inner-child imagination. A play with words. A dream, in written form. My words entrace you to fall into your own imagination & use the most powerful human ability: [Imagination.](imagination)

When i'm telling stories I feel powerful. Maybe artists feel this way to. Ariana Grande when she's singing. Michael Jackson when he would dance on stage. Ian Curtis when he would... sing and... kinda dance. Kurt Cobain when he would smash guitars & scream his music for 1000s of teens. 


There is something powerful about the expression of a human being, through their work. I think, an artist, even at the most dysfunctional phases of their lives, cannot give up on their art. It is perhaps, the only hope they have of expressing the impossible.


I always felt like I was speaking a different language. Not like an alien, but maybe an alien inside a human body, so I looked normal to everyone.


This is why I was always so deeply influenced by artists
