# How to heal from pain

![](https://images.pexels.com/photos/2627945/pexels-photo-2627945.jpeg?auto=compress&cs=tinysrgb&w=1260&h=750&dpr=1)

Pain is part of this ride. Unfortunately there are some kinds of pain that are so horrible, you can't live with it. I hope whoever reads this, never has to experience that kind of pain. But my hope is that just as cruel as we human beings can be, we are the only creatures in this dark cosmos, that possess the consciousness to heal others from pain, and even completely destroy pain from life, someday. That is one of my biggest hopes in this life.

But this note is about healing from pain.
