# Cyborgs
Human beings are simple, at our core, we just need someoone that loves us, fall in love with them too, have sex, make a family, have a stable, secure shelter, food, source. Be part of a larger community. Socialize & keep your various bodily systems healthy. You give that to somenoe, and most of us would be satisfied & die happily. 

# Few of us, want to explore the cosmos for meaning
But there a few of us, crazy enough to look at the skies, and instead of seeing the clouds & stars, we see the vast emptiness behind everything. Almost as if we are staring into the abyss itself. But the abyss lives inside all of us... That must mean there is both a personal darkness, and a collective darkness. 

Our imagination wants to tap at the glass of the cosmos, but our ape bodies are pulled down by the forces of gravity & sex.
