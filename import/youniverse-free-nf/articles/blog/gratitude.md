**We take for granted what doesn't cause pain. The things that just work for us everyday, without error, they go unnoticed. Like a clean room, you don't appreciate all of the work that goes into maintaining it a clean, liveable space. Only when it's rotting & digusting, do you notice the lack. That's like gratitude forcing itself for you to notice it.**

Gratitude isn't just about feeling, aslong as you're even aware of the little miracles...

Being grateful feels like pulling out the spears from my heart. It's both incredibly painful, but immensely relieving. There are no words to describe the most beautiful kind of gratitude: **being grateful to just be alive & have your life.** I've cried many tears to this thought.


**"Gratitude is the bridge between hopelessness and living."**
