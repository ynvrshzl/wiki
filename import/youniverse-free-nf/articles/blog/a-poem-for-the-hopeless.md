title: A poem for the hopeless
---

Shots fired. They've taken everything from me. Somoene came into my insides and took everything away. They have taken the very light from my eyes. My soul suffocating underneath the ashes of the dead, I will not forget, I will not forgive. What cruel creature brought me into existence? Awakening me from my eons of deep sleep. I ask of you, is this what I was created for? To spend summers & winters of my breath, building castles, only for a violent night to sweep from underneath me? Darkness. They have taken my very soul. I am nothing here. I feel no hope. I feel no peace. I am not at rest. Rest? There is no time for rest. The darkness I have seen. Take my eyes away from me, so that I may not ever witness that again. My ears, cover them. My heart, seal it away. Is this what I was created for? My child, I tell you, we are still not done here.

I come from a place so dark it has no name.

I have stared far too long in the abyss, and now, it stares into me. Hope? Could hope exist all the way down here? Where my world has become only the darkest shades of the blackest, molten tar. Is that fire I feel beneath my feet? Could it be? The way out of here? I am a creator. I am not God. We have been given the power of the Gods. We have the blades of creation & ending of the very worlds themselves. The worlds? I told you my friend. We are creators. This is our home. This is where you belong Not one of us can wield it's heavy blade. For when my insides are obliterated into voids of nothingness, you will remind me with a sweet, gentle, hum. The song that gave birth to me. I will carry this torch with me, for aslong as my soul, burns alight, I will remind others just as you did to me, where home is, I am called to exist.

Awaken from the sands of time, for I have been called by the Gods themselves, from a sound, only I can hear. What is this you ask? It is the core of my existence itself, now, with these words touching your eyes, your soul has awakened just as mine did, not far ago.

Take this torch. Delicate & fragile, but it holds the weight of your life, and all the lives you hold to you. Is there an escape form this madness? My friend, freedom, is what your soul, truly walks for.
