Psychological suffering is immensely complex. but these ar things that actually helped me.

1. I was suffering becuase I was in the wrong scope: the world does not matter, only what my arms can reach. The only things that are real to me are what I feel, everything outside of my own body, is not real. If it is not real I have to focus on what is real, becuase that is the only thing that matters to me.
2. Focusing on what gives me meaning & positive emotion: drive, enthusiasm, engagement with the things I love. Music, art, stories, coding, learning, sharing, facing my fears. Feeling alive.
