# Why do human beings enjoy horror?
Psychologically, there is a strange paradox. We feel safe, watching such dangerous situations, form the comfort of our screens & books. It is perhaps, some part of the human being that desires activity, rather than being bored. Infact, a study showed that if you put a person in a room with a shock device, they will purposefully hurt themselves, becuase for some reason, we go crazy with nothingness, and we'd rather have pain than nothing.

# Does this make human beings monsters? 
Don't confuse an internal, survival instict, with the human self. Most empathetic creatures would not cause pain onto themselves or others, becuase, it's horrible.
