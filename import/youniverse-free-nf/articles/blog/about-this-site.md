# What is this site?

**What's the point of this website?** 
So I can share my private writings, host my stories & have a platform for my youniverse.

**Make a website inspired by my obsidian vaults.**

- Help people like me, effortlessly find information that could save their lives.
- Focus on systems, not aesthetics. UI adapts to systems.
- Images capture attention, content contains meaning

Hey. If you found this site, amongst the digital wastelands of the internet, you found a safe place. Come, sit here next to me! We're going to do amazing things here. We will explore human meaning, existence, the existential void, answers, beauty, art, music, stories, knowledge, wisdom, video games, anime, and my cute face all over this site lol :)

# The reason I created this place

I've created a couple of sites, but my intention is to share the work i've been hoarding to myself. 

I started this website after realizing, one day, I'm no longer going to exist. I am happy that the pain I feel will no lnoger continue. But I am terrified that the happiness I feel, will die with me. That's why I'm sharing this with you.

# A public resource for you
I want this site to be a public resource for human beings stuggling with meaning, knowledge, and the human condition. I try my best to structure things so it's an enjoyable reading experience, weaving links in-between notes to connect things together. It's also a place where I can leave behind little trails of my existence, for those who want to know more about me. It's like my public second-self.

# This website is a project for my life's work to be shared with you
This website is a project to share my life's work with you. Whoever reads this, I encourage you to explore & be curious, I write about alot of things that give my life meaning, and I know there's something on this site that will help you in your life, if you look for it!
