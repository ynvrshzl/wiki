If I could only watch one movie for the rest of my life, it'd be **I, Robot.**

I watched it for the first time as a child. My father bought this movie from someone... that made it even cooler & more mysterious. 

Throughout my life, I've re-watched this movie so many times. With each watch, I discover something new about it. New details, new stories, new parts. It's as if the movie evolves with  me.


I remember one time, I woke up at 4:00 A.M and I didn't know what to do with so much free time, so I decided to watch I, Robot, while building an early vault in obsidian. That was simulatneously the first time I had ever discovered you could preserve your memories using text files. I started writing so much, everything poured out of me. **I didn't want to lose any more emotions, thoughts, feelings, memories.**
