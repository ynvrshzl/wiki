What it feels like to be transgender
In my experience, I feel like my soul is female, but my body is that of a feminine man. I feel like an imposter in my own body.

I try to match how my female self would take care of herself, and that's where the dysphoria peaks. Think about it, if you didnt take care of yourself, would you feel happy in your own body? Most likely not. That's the exact same feeling for a trans-person, only magnified by trauma & other mental health issues, though you don' thave to be mentally ill to be trans, that's a disgusting, de-humanizing stereotype.
