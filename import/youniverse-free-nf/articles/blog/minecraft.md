Minecraft is alot like life, it infact, taught me how to game-ify my life, so that I switch perspectives & follow the path of life.

Here are some of the most important life-lessons minecraft has taught me.

### Analysis-paralysis
To avoid analysis paralysis, I've realized I need to always be moving. Wether that's going on adventures, or knocking stuff out on my bucket-list, aslong as I have a direction, the journey itself is the meaning, **and that is the key to avoiding staying stuck, having somewhere else to look forward to!**
