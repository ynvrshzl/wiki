title: Being grateful to be alive
---


I'm so lucky to even be here. To even be breathing. People who have survived near-death experiences always have this feeling. People who have survived suicide attempts, people who've been in accidents, they all say the same thing: they are grateful to just be here. For me, whenever I get lost within the projects, todos, plans & ideas in my life, I remember that I need to step back and remember: **I already have the most precious thing: myself.**
