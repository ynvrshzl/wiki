# How to learn anything

Main concept: make mistakes, and iteratively improve.

This is my self-learned knowledge source of everything i've experienced, learning new things in life. You can apply this wether it's a new hobby, or the most challenging time in your life.

Most of the systems in our biology are designed to help us learn.

The only way that things learn in this existence is by trial & error. We try, and we learn. You never learn by just thinking, you always learn by doing. Sometimes analyzing is the key to preparing your next action, but this can lead to the familiar trap of overthinking. It's becuase it feels safe, comfortable. You feel in control when you are imagining & processing, like a master on a chess board, commanding all of your pieces & analyzing possible moves. But until you pick up a piece & move, things on the board will stay the same forever.
