# Prayer
Dear person reading this, if you are going through any disturbance in your life, share a couple of breaths of your life with me. Nobody told you where to go. Nobody has seen the pain you're in. You've gone to places without names, and sometimes, the pain hasn't felt real, and it seems you're trapped in a glass wall, unable to reach the world outside. It feels like i'm reading your mind, right? That's the power of human language. I want you this to be a sign for you, from the universe, from this world, that there is hope for you. I hope you find nothing but peace & health. 

Amen
