Taking care of yourself as a creator

I'll say it with every breah of my life: the only thing that's keeping me alive is music, games, stories & creation. Energy in this universe is neither created nor destroyed, only transferred form one form to another. This is why Steve Jobs & Picasso said this alot: "Good artists copy, great artists steal." 

But how do you create art when you're physically exhausted? Emotionally drained? What do you do when the one source of life you have, is something that becomes work rather than therapy?
