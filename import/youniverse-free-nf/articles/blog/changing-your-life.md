# Replacing bad habits with good habits
When learning the "correct" way, or starting a new habit, the old way of doing things can come back & make it feel like you're pushing uphill. 

**The key is to remember this:** when starting a new habit, it will feel uncomfortable at first, but give it the time it deserves and eventually it will replace your old habits.

A mental goal can be to remind yourself that this way is better than your old way
