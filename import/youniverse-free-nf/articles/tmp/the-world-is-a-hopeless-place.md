title: Is this world a hopeless place?
---


If I see a starving family in the east, especially the children that were brought into this world... and this is their childhood? DOn't just stand there, seeing the cruelty of humanity, **DO SOMETHING GOOD ABOUT IT BY CREATING NOT DESTROYING.**


If it feels futile to try — remember — **we have no idea what the capacity of a human being truly is.** That terrifies me. And inspires me more than anything.
