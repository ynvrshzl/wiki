At the climax between Jack & Joey Doom, all that remains of Doom is a walking corpse... a spine without body, and somehow, it follows Jack & rebuild's the body of Joey Doom. This terrifies Jack Mooder, for the first time in years, he feels true terror. He screams: *"What the hell are you? A machine, or a man?"* Doom's spine rebuild's it's own body & voices from his inner world leak into Jack's inner world. In a terrifying, haunting chariot of voices, doom's spine speaks to Jack. Jack tries to run away, but no matter where he goes, he always runs back to the same place.

The voices haunt Jack in a foreign language... he feels the terror of his child-self. He understands in this moment, what he has done. Flashbacks of Jack's memories during his childhood play in his mind. It is, his life flashing before his eyes. He sees his sister, is sick & dying. He slams the glass walls but cannot save her. He feels the regret as the weight of what he's done.
