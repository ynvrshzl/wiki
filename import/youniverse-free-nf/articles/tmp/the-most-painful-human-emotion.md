It's agonizing. Like you've wasted a beautifully good life. Could you imagine? The weight of regret as you watch the light fade from your own eyes? What more will it take for you to wake up from your eons of sleep? Has your soul not suffered enough? Is this what we were created for? Have you ever felt that? The faint glimpse of agony, that some part of you is suffocating, deep in the sands of emotionlessness. You can almost hear the cries of your tormented soul/psyche, as you remember times in your life, where you felt truly happy to be alive.
