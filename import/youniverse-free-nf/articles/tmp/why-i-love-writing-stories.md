title: Why I love writing stories
---

We don't know what we have until we lose it. If there's any sentence that could save my life, it's that. With the stories I tell, with the work I leave behind, I want you to see what you have. I want you to see it fully. In all the raw glory & ugliness of being a human being.

When I die, I want these stories to continue on for me. I want others to tell their own stories from their
 youniverse. The way I see this world, is equally beuatiful as it is horrifying — and I want to capture that in
 everything I do.


I want these stories to heal me as much as break me. [Sometimes, that's the only way to know what really matters in life.](#what-matters-most-in-life)
