# About this song

This is a song that I wrote, trying to express the feelings of hopelessness, futility, and desperately wanting to live, despite your body dying & falling apart.


In this song, a character is dying from exhaustion. They have tried to end their own life. Everyday felt more & more hopeless. They lost all hope in life & jumped off a bridge. Their body collapses on the water, it feels like a thousand pounds of concrete, yet no different from the hpoelessness of their daily life. They think to themselves: "This will only hurt for a moment." and they want to close their eyes. But just before they close their eyes they remember what made life so preciuos as a young person. They then despearately fight for their life. They can't move their body, but they can remember memories. Memories that made life precious. In these brittle moments, the faint ghosts of happy memories overshadow any traumatic memory, and in those moments the character realizes that life continues after suffering. Life continues after death. Your life continues inside someone else... inside something else.
