A short study on the common reasons why a human being is led to do barbaric things to others.


"Monsters aren't born, they're created."


When someone harms someone else, they are transferring energy onto another living being. This works the same for when someone does something good for someone else.


Note: I am in no way condoning or justifying evil, I want to understand what causes someone to do these things, so that we can notice the signs in other people & in ourselves.


**Short answer:** regardless of the reasons, foundationally if a human being has unanswered pain, and they live thsi way for too long, they will cause harm to others as a way to retaliate against a 'system' that never acknowledged them.


### Common symptoms


Common symptoms of most, if not, all people in history who've done evil things is they have had a higher-than-average intelligence. They only chanelled it through the lens of harming others, as an answer to the questions they had about life.


You have to channel your intelligence to the right thing. I've seen the most barbaric criminals & human beings who have done barbaric things, and I realize, they were all similar: they all had a higher-than-average level of intelligence, awareness & processing of the world around them. HOwever, they also had a larger-than-self sense of righteousness & being better than you. Perhaps they adopted this personal/alter-ego as a way to cope with their miserable lives. No sex. No friends. No memoires. No experiences. Live that kind of life long enough, and anybody will turn into a monster. That's just how our human nature works. If we don't have what we need, we turn into ugly, decying things.


This is why having a support system is first-priority in my life. But having a support doesn't always have to come from others, sometimes, you can manage on your own. Sometimes you have to cry to be heard. If there's aything I've learned, you have to do whatever it takes to survive, and live. **That's the only reason I'm here. Becuase I love myself & I want to give myself a happy life!**
