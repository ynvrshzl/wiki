"I see the other girls my age... but I never had a chance. They have so many friends... they seem so happy... I... I wish to God... that I could have friends... for my birthday..."

But then I met them! I... I hated when I woke up... I couldn't believe that I felt so comfortable in my dream world.. I couldn't beleive that they were all just... *gone.* My heart hurs so much...
