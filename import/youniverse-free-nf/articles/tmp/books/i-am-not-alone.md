POV: You are a child, waking up from a long nap. Except you don't find yourself in your room... it takes a second to realize: you have woken up in the mall your parents left you in. To your terror, it is night. Heart, immediately pounds, your breath drawing short. The mall has closed. It is too uncomfortably dark to see anything, the only lights coming from the moonlight, from the windows in cieling. You can't hear anything but the vast, hollowness of the empty space. The creepy ambience keeps you sane,

You decide to walk around, there you see, the store mannequins, are all gone. 

You must find anything you can, to survive the night.
