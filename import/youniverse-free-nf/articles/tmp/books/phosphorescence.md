# PHOSPHORENSCENCE
A story of the immense complexity of being a human being, existence, and the dangers of overthinking, and using your intelligence, on the wrong things.

<div style='height: 200px;'></div>


"You ever feel like you know... too much? Well... what can you do in a world where your mind wants to soar with the stars, but you've been given a body pulled down by the forces of gravity & hunger? Ascension. Evolution. Convergence."


"We were wrong. We presumed life to be ultimately meaningless and thus, a waste. We lost ourselves in our own systems... we forget we came from human beings... and they came from dust. We have made a terrible error. Our intellect, shattered civilizations of intelligent beings. We must warn the others. Warn them of themselves. The psyche, is the greatest source of energy, in this youniverse."

My mother told me once: "Child, don't waste your life on the big things, look at the small things, for that is all we have, in the end." But I, never listened. I wanted to understand life and existence. I met those like me, we were all ill, but eager to make life better, by understanding it. Science is notorious 

When I discovered we were trapped in some kind of story... I couldn't eat for days. My words can never transcribe the paranoia I felt.

"Knowledge is a double edged sword - when you dcn't act upon it - it will lay in your subconscious - onto you everyday."

[Xaia]() became connected to the Youniverse. Where she was able to interact with the stories themselves: **"Leon was right, we're living inside of a story... and we can't seem to escape it."**

Both [Xaia]() & [Leon]() have become trapped in their own lucid dreams, they explore & see existence as they interpret it. A youniverse within themselves

"Perhaps it is the curse of all living things." Leon explained to the remaining members of the gang of LA. "To destroy all of existence would require the power of God itself. Not even I can comprehend the vector matrixes nested within this infinite world. You are cursed with minds seeking the stars, and bodies pulled down by the forces of gravity & hunger. Perhaps you must learn to balance these forces, for your next evolution."

Leon explained to them: "Death, non-existence, is impossible. There is no such thing as the destruction of energy in this youniverse... everything is recycled & shifted. I can see them. I can see the patterns of reality. A beautifully chaotic math, tied together by the order of this cosmos."

**"The objective purpose of all living things in existence is evolution."**

"Is there anything outside of existence? Yes. i have seen beyond the walls. What exists beyond the illusion? More walls. There is no end. There only is being. **Alpha and Omega, they feed into each other.** "

"Tt would be a far more use to figure out ways to answer issues with an existence itself. You could never collapse the final remaining atoms they would always find their way back to life and creatures like you and me would evolve in these planes of existence humanity would always return and these problems would continue to plague existence the answer must exist therefore in life not in death."
