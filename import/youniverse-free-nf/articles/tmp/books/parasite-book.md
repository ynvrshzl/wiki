### Summary


Parasite is a book about a world without human beings. It explores [Anti natalism](#anti-natalism) & Existentialism. The book describes a world left behind by human beings who decided to end all life on Earth. They only succeeded in ending all human life. Plants, animals, and other life forms continued. Miraculously, the planet survived. After millions of years, creatures with primitive consciousness emerged, similar to great apes, they showed signs of human beings re-emerging from the soil. Thousands of years passed and human beings were on the planet again. It seemed a never-ending cycle, that human life must exist, that it cannot be extinguished. That so long as there is a single cell out there, we will stubbornly emerge from the ashes of the soil, here to destroy & create, in all our beauty & in all our disgusting nature. The book
