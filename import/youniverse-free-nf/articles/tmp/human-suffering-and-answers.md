# Is there an answer to human suffering?

When depression, or any mental illness comes into your life, it feels like you're living in a different world.

Pain, why is it so terrible? 

This question has haunted me, even when I'm not in any immediate pain, I ruminate & think about the things I've seen & the pain I've felt in my own life. But we're forgetting a crucial point: healing. Which is the bridge between life & death itself. The human body has an incredible source of healing, but how do you heal pain?

I discovered deeply important things, that we are psychologicakl & biological beings. We have physical & emotional needs. Sometimes, all a person needs is a change of environment.
