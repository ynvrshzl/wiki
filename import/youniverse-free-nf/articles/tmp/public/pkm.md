# PKM (Personal knowledge management)

A **second brain** isn’t about dumping all the details into one pile — that just recreates the overwhelm you were trying to escape. The brain itself works by compartmentalizing contexts: work, reflection, play, planning. Without walls, everything bleeds together and you get decision fatigue + rumination.

What you’re discovering is:

Details live where they belong (code details in the repo, therapy details in your journal, etc.).

Your second brain is like a map of links, not a landfill of everything. For me, it has never ever worked to put everything in one place. *Organizing where things exist, is the balance.*
