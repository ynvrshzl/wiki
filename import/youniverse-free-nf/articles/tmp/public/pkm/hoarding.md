Do you often find yourself recording memories. Are you afraid of losing ideas, memories, feelings? I am too. It shows you're sensitive to these parts of life, that most human beings, don't even think about.

If you treat your notes like ephemeral organic things, nothing is permamant, everything is temporary. That relieves a huge weight form trying to make everything as perfect as possible.


This makes writing notes less about "archiving life" and more about harvesting seeds.
