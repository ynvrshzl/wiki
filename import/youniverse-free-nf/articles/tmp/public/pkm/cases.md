#### Organizing parts of your life that your brain can't hold itself

If you have a lot of scattered and disorganized areas in your life especially digitally, you can link to them using a simple PKM. 

#### Creating systems tuned to you
Sometimes that calendar or mail app doesn't have the correct size for you. You can't force yourself to use something that isn't designed for you.

Sometimes there are apps or tools that don't fit to our needs, like a stone Smith creates their own tools, sometimes you need to create your own system. but you need to understand what problem you're solving. you need to understand why.

you can't just write it down and expect to remember it. 

think about how you learn to write a bike did you write notes about it or study it in books? no you learned by trying and failing. this is the only way human beings learn through experience. 

having a PKM system is an effort to reduce the pain of failure, however if you're never willing to fail you will never learn.
