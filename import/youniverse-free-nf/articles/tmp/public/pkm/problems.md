### Problems
*When PKM creates more problems than solutions*


1. Learning
2. Remembering

PKM won't help you learn or remember: If you want to learn a skill and remember it, you have to continuously practice it. That's how you retain & remember things, not by studying or writing. Writing can make things easier to process at first, but there are other ways to externalize & process things. We can also talk out loud, or try the thing itself.

So if it won't help me learn or remember things, what's the point of having a system at all? Isn't it better to just use pre-existing tools to organize my busy life?
