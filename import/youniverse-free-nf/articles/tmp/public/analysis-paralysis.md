it's unclear how to move forward... but isn't this what writers deal with all the time? that writers block... not knowing what to write next. it's like being a steam-engine in a train-yard... with many paths to choose... you don't know which one is the right one... and you could possibly waste fuel if you choose the wrong track... but isn't moving forward infinitely better than staying stuck? that's the core of analysis-paralysis, it's the brain's way of trying to protect us human beings from choosing a potentially fatal decision... but if you think about it, the irony is if you stay here forever, you never move forward, and you decay in place, and that's always 100% fatal.
