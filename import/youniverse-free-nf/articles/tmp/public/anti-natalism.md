Anti Natalism is a philosophy that human beings should not be born becuase life contains so much suffering.


I explore this deeply in my work.


I believe that Anti-natalism is the perfect answer to a problem that doesn't exist. This is why it seems to make sense to me, especially when I feel depressed & hopeless about the world & us human beings living in it. But then I realize: in moments of pure joy & child-like wonder, there is no rooom for the queston of "why." This helped me to realize: Anti-natalism is incomplete.


Let's use shadow & light as an example! If an object creates a shadow, it must require a source of light. However, if you want to get rid of the shadow, you must get rid of the source of light, and the object. In the case of suffering & life, you are trying to remove the totality of existence, which consists of far more than mere suffering. Though suffering is absolutely the worst part of existence, we entirely rule out the possibility of healing & transformation, which are the most poweful ways nature uses to repair & restore damage.
