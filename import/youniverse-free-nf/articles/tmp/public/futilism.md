# Futilism
Futilism — is about accepting what we cannot do — instead of being crushed by the weight of futility & apathy — we are instead — channelling all of our energy & focus on what we can do — and often — that makes the most difference that our souls are hungry for.

Similar to [Stoicism](stoicism.md)

I want to preface this by saying: I'm currently going through the most futile moments of my existence. I am absolutely crushed by the weight of the world, and yet, I realize this is the greatest quest for meaning I will ever embark on. It is absolutely meaningless, if I don't help anyone, so, with what little energy I have left to give in my body, this is part of [my life's work.](#my-life-work) Before anything, we must examine why? Why & how did we get here? To such a place where it feels normal to look at life thorugh the eyes of futility, hopelessness, and apathy. We must examine & interrogate our thoughts & beliefs, if they have no basis in truth, discard them. HOwever, the trickest part about futilism, is that if you take a peek around, you get to see how hopeless things really do seem.
