When you are going through depression & trying to numb/distract yourself, you're actually prolonging the depression. IF it's safe to do so, try to allow yourself to feel & think what you're burying deep down inside.


It's even worse when you look around you and you feel like nobody feels the same way as you. It's like the depression is the only thing that's real, and the happiness isn't real.


When you've been really depressed, for a long time, lost hope on life, everyday feels the same.
