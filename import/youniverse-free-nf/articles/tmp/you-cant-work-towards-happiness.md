Ability is a result of health


You can't be strong without strong foundtaions.


I used to paint illustrations of my favorite characters. I used to animate them in 3D, and create immersive 3D
 worlds. I used to sing & make music, with the characters in those worlds.


After my physical & mental health getting so low, I realized: being creative & imaginative isn't forced. It's a
 result of a healthy eniovnrment. Both internally & externally


Currently 


I'm trying to figure out what's the best way to spend my time & energy. This website is a big passion project for
 me right now. I have over 20,000+ notes filled with ideas, stories, knowledge, and life-saving experienced that
 I want to share with everyone. But it feels like all of my energy is going to surviving another day. The only
 reason I'm existing right now is becuase I love myself. Nobody in my life has ever loved me the way I love my
 inner child. I feel that I really am the mother I never had. The best friend I always needed. One of the
 personas, **Yami,** became this energy. One of his favorite quotes is: Be the person you never had in your
 life. Be your own best friend first.


My writings contain no censorship. I don't care if this will get demonotized, all I want is for my content to
 reach the people that are in mental suffucationright now. I know what that's like. I experienced that today.
 It's the worst kind of pain, and my inner mother self cries so deeply. I'm describing what it feels like to want
 to die. The abuse, violence, self-harm, mental health illness, childhood trauma, unexplainable pain. Remember
 that I am here for you. Our ancestors must have gone through worse pain than us. Our ancestors didn't have the
 knowledge that we do today. *And yet, they survived.* We can survive too. That's the hardest part that
 nobody talks abut when you're going through pain on your own. The surviving feels impossible. It feels like the
 worst physical pain, will never end. The moment feels eternal. Every second that passes feels like the video has
 been put on 0.0001x speed. Sometimes it feels like a little joker is psychotically enjoying watching good poeple
 suffer.  in history or not, his-story, his story, is symbolic & atleast that, is real.

I really, really don't understand human beings. I don't understand myself. and that's okay. Maybe the mystery of
 this life is that you have to find the answer for yourself. I don't know why, but it makes sense for me.


The first thing that helps me feel alive is treating myself like my child self.


I can't give you hope through letters on a screen. But I hope my messages help you to survive through the darkest
 storms of your life. This is the only reason I created **The Youniverse.** 


I long for the day where I find my answers. I dream of the day that all of the pain I have survived, will be
 worth it. For now, today, I exist for another day. I find my people. I find my place. I create a space in the
 world for me to exist. People like me will find my beacon of light, becuase it's not mine. It is the work of
 mother nature. Of the Universe. The Youniverse. God.


It feels like... we are human beings, but we are all experiencing different levels of consciousness. I can't
 explain this in technical terms becuase I'm not God, but essentially, some of us feel deeper than others. Wether
 it's trauma, illness, or any kind of pain. We become more sensitive to the world around us. The negative
 experience makes the good experiences more significant, than for a regular person. Still I sometimes daydream,
 what would life had been like if I was born as a "Normal" person? Like the girls I see in the mall sometimes
 when I go. What must it be like to have never looked into the abyss?


Recently in my life I've been trying to create youtube videos. I feel like it's time I share all the things I've
 been through. I want my life to mean something. I can't die without seeing the light. I've seen the light, but
 the shadows keep me from seeing what's true.
 Healing yourself from mental illness, childhood trauma


Mental illness is the worst kind of suffering. It's a silent scream. Your body feels like there's no escape. Your
 brain feels like you should end it yourself. When I'm at my lowest, I feel like I'm in this world, where nobody
 exists, and I'm just an after-image. I feel like nothing is real anymore and I just drown in this world. It
 makes me forget all of the beautiful parts of life. Falling in love with my future partner, having children
 someday, having friends, crying with people, healing. All of it. So beautiful. That's my only source of hope. I
 cry out to God everyday, take my pain away.
 I've been isolated from people for years, ever since I was 15. I'm 23 now. So much in my life has changed
 internally. I've evolved as a creature. I discovered I was a woman all this time. I remembered the pain I caused
 to others. I did my best to heal those wounds I caused in others. I'm still trying my best these days. When
 you've seen it, you realize how precious life is. Most human beings don't have access to these deeper levels of
 consciousness, but it exists inside all of us. IT's painful. It's so fucking painful. The good, the bad. I can't
 see it any other way sometimes, I feel like we're living in a world where the base is pain. The universe is
 infinite darkness, all around us. That's why we need art. We need love. We need comedy. 


I'm realizing how little energy I have in my body now. I feel like a 90 year-old grandma. I hope to live to those
 years, people when they reach that age, are usually so careless about stuff us youngins obsess over


There is so much pain in this world. That quote is something I feel everyday of my life. I've seen so many
 terrible things that humans have done to animals & other people
