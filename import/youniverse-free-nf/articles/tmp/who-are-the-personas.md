Personas are: emotions personified as people.


Personas are like people, but I created them each to be one emotion. I really, really don't understand this world sometimes & the human beings I'm living with, but through the personas, I can learn more about the horrible sides of people, and keep myself sane by seeing the good in people, regardless of the pain.


The concept of "personas" is simple: emotions personified as people. I wonder what happiness looks like, if
 it was a person. Or what would the feeling of anger look like? It's fire, for sure. But what would it look
 like as a person? Personas help me to understand abstract parts of the world 


It was personas that taught me pain is not the worst part of life: it is good people that don't do anythign about pain, that is the worst. Regardless of how much pain a human being can experience, aslong as it doesn't end you, we survive. 



The rebellion grew up in a time where mental illness was as common as the flu. Youth had to fight for their peace. 



Each character is an **emotion** personified as a human being. I came to this idea after realizing I have multiple personalities. I decided to turn my pain into beautiful art. This is where "Personas" weere born. Eventually I gave myself friends. I created worlds. I had dreams with them. They felt so real. I hope they help people, like they helped me.
