title: Trauma

If I could put into words the things I've survived, there would be no words. If I could put into images, the trauma I have healed from, 


Trauma can feel like a creature, that is always watching you, making sure you never escape. It has it's roots attached to your most personal self.


![](https://youniverse.free.nf/imgs/abyss-monster.jpg)
 
![](https://youniverse.free.nf/imgs/trauma.jpg)
Trauma can also feel like a noise. It can make everything feel like a nightmare, like 'real-life'
 is not real. People just look like shadows, you can see right through them.


Often times I feel indescribable pain. I lose my senses. I can't hear, or see, or touch or feel. Physically i'm
 alive, but it feels like a demon, or something possessing me. I pray to God everyday to free me from this. 


I am happy to say that I've found hope. I've found the answers I was looking for. **It was in life, not in
 death.**Trauma is simply a psychological response to a physical experience, that felt like we were going
 to die.


The best storeis I've seen are of people who survived trauma. So it seems to me that pain, is a necessary part of
 a beautiful life.


The reason I believe this is becuase in order to experience darkness, you have to experience light. The darker
 the black, the brighter the white. In essence, this is what Yin yang is all about.
