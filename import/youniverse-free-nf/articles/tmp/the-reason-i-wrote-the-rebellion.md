title: The reason I wrote the Rebellion
---


A note to my future self: the reason you wrote The Rebellion was becuase you were tired of seeing young people end their lives & end the lives of others. You were exhausted of seeing cruelty in this world and no way to help. You were being swallowed by the void of nothingness, you thought there was nothing you could do. Until you decided to sit down with the demons, and the angels, and listen to what they had to tell you. You didn't want to end your own life, you wanted to live fully and leave behind so much. With this book, it will someday be adapted into a movie, and eventually, our dream, an anime. So keep going! *Love, your past self.*
