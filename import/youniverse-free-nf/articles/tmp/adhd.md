How i learned to make the most of my ADHD. 

Turning the 'weaknesses' into strengths, requires more than just productivity or work, or even the best therapist & strategies. If you don't have a personal, deep vision for what makes life worth living for you, nothing will matter.

I learned to use my highly distractable nature, and multiple interests, by juggling between them, rather than working on one thing for a long time pretty much impossible for someone with ADHD, right? Actually, this is a topic that I don't see talked about often enough. ADHD doesn't mean that we can't focus, but rather, we don't have control over what we focus on. This is made most apparent with hyperfixation/hyperfocus, which is like the ADHD version of the neurotypical "flow" state. 

Infact, being in flow actually heals mental health.
