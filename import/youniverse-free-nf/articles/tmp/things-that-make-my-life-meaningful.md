1. [Being grateful to be alive.](#being-grateful-to-be-alive)





![](https://youniverse.free.nf/imgs/rats.jpg)
As the image above shows, I really *really* like animals. They remind me that we human beings are so serious and "intelligent." but deep down, inside our bodies, we have all the same systems as mammalls in the wild do. It shows me that human personalities, cognition, and our social masks, are just illusions. The real stuff is what makes us feel alive. 


Love.


I don't have support from anyone in my life, I don't have friends, I don't have family, but I do have God, and I have myself. God to me is the mystery of life. God is a woman to me, like a mother. But whatever God is, I want you to know that I'm only here today, becuase you've been there for me. Even at times when I really wanted to escape this world and die. You were there for me, not as a feeling but as hope.


After lot's of unhealthy amounts of porn, gaming, youtube, obsessively writing, I learned to quiet the nois of the world around us, and listen to my own inner child.


I imagine my inner child and me, holding hands as we run through a beautiful field of grass. Healing with every step. I know someday we will both come to an end, but for now, we live forever, in this moment, *and that's all I need to die happily.*


living in todays world just makes me feel so sick. We should find some place far from here, perhaps, in another plane of existence. That's what I cry out to God for.


Attractive men & women. I know. This is very superficial. But when you're going through serious depression, you can't afford to be serious.
