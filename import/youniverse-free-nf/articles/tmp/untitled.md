# Illness & remedies
Here, we roleplay a bit — Life can be played like a Medieval RPG (which is what keeps me sane.)
- Illness: Knowledge hoarding. Obsessively writing everything. 
- Remedy: let it become part of you. Don't try to hoard everything. Observe the things you naturally remember. Those are the things that matter most. Trust your brain and all it's capacities — it's mother nature's greatest work!
