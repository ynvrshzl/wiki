The youniverse is an imaginary place in my stories.


[Why I love telling stories](#why-i-love-writing-stories)


I started writing stories about alternate lives, before deep depression in my life. Stories gave me the blank canvas of imagination, a space completely my own. But it got boring to imagine the same things over & over again. I realized, stories are best when the author is not completely in control of them. How would you do that exactly? This is where the personas where born. They are half of the youniverse. I'd say, the youniverse is half world, and half people. 


The concept of the youniverse is I have a world inside my mind & body. Memories, dreams, hopes, lessons, all of these things make me who I am. Without them, I am dead inside. The youniverse is a constant reminder to me that my life is worth living becuase of all the things that live inside me. I am like a mother to them. I must protect them. Give them my love, time, energy, nurturing & nourishing. I wish I could spend all night & day writing amazing stories, but for one reason or another, I get distratcted by little things in my life. Still, if it's meant for you, you'll always find it, no matter where you are. That's what I believe.


This life is also impossibly complicated. Beautiful chemicals exist in my body right now, awaiting an experience to awaken them. The same is true for the painful emotions. It seems when we don't do anything in life for too long, we decay & die. IT's true for our dreams, if you don't work them, nobody will. Eventually it's like the screaming rays of light they once were, that filled you with hope, begin to die & fade. But if I've learned anything in nature, it's that everything can heal. *Given the right stuff: time, habitat, energy & love.* 



 The youniverse is like a reflection of you. All your memories, relationships, beliefs, thoughts, dreams,
 hopes & every element that creates the unique creature you are.
 The universe, is a terrifying, empty void. THere are some stars that shine, and that's what makes it
 beautiful. Otherwise, it's like when you close your eyes, there's nothing to see. If death is like this,
 then there can be nothing more terrifying than non-existence.
 However, it is becuase of the vast emptiness of the universe, that we are able to appreciate the full glow
 fo the stars, cosmos, nebulas, in all their hauntingly beautiful colors & arrangements.
 I see human beings as the same. We are fundamentally animals. The only thing that separates us from the
 animals, is **Choice. Reason. Cognition.**
 I started this concept, becuase I want to find others out there, who share my perspective of the world we
 are living in.
 I believe that every human being has the capacity for good & evil. We all have the capacity to choose.
