# What would happen in a world without darkness?
Imagine, a world without pain. A world without sadness, cruelty, injustice, crime or darkness. A world, without darkness — what would it look like?

    "The beings in this world could not experience pain, but, they could not experience happiness either. They became blind. We called this world **"The Void."** Even in a space without darkness, light could not exist. They needed each other to create experience. Mustn't that be the purpose of our short lives?" 

[— The Cosmic Traveller]()
