The goal is to learn, not to finish. 

If my mental goal is to achieve an ending, than anything that stops me from achieving the ending, accumulates stress & frustration. Eventually, enough stress & frustration causes burnout & health issues. So remember that the goal should be to learn. Ideally, the goal is to enjoy the process of things, rather than the outcome. This way, no matter what problems you face, you know that you're experiencing good, even if you don't feel like you're making any progress. 

"It is not about the things we can see, but the invisible little parts, that make the big things exist."
