# How to complete your stories

# 

Remember that no story is completely canonical. This means we have the liberty to weave in-between our main story points, little moments that breathe life into our worlds. For example, you might notice the way a character hugs their friend, or the way that girl walks whenever she's around the boy she likes. These are all little details that help the reader believe they are experiencing the story.
