# Applied knowledge
Translating knowledge into action

Welcome to applied knowledge, we will explore how to use the knowlwedge you already have, to make things real in your life.

Knowledge without action, is like potential without application. Knowledge without action, is like a scientist who never experiments. Knowledge, without the limitation of action, is like an infinitely expanding space, there is pure chaos and no order.
