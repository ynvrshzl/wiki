# Beauty
A study of percieved facial beauty

![](https://i.pinimg.com/736x/39/b7/6f/39b76f00f38f0ced50857843095a7969.jpg)

**Ada Wong** from Resident Evil. The percieved beauty starts in the eyes, and the hair guides you downard to the features of the face. Eyes are always the first point of contact (pun intended). Makeup is not exaggerated, and instead, helps soften & spotlight the features. The face is naturally full & proportions are even & balanced. (Golden ratio)
