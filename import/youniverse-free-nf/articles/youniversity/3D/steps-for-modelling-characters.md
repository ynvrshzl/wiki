# 3D characters

A complete guide of the most important steps for how I build youniverse personas in 3D.

<!-- Consider this like a video game model. -->

**TLDR**
Jump straight to the main content [here](#process)

**Summary**
Here, we will discuss the complete process for male, female, and alternative-gendered characters.

**What's the point?**
With your own 3D character, you can save a ton of time instead of illustrating by hand, or using someone elses' model.

**Why should I know the steps?**
Knowing the process is like having a map: you can visualize the steps & save yourself alot of work! You also know what's up next, so you don't blur the lines between the stages & avoid stress & most importantly, avoid burnout.
