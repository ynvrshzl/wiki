# 3D Artstyles
### A comprehensive guide for visual aesthetics & expression

Artstyle is more than looks — it impacts every step of the process. Generally, more realism equals more work, becuase there are more details to consider. However, this is where the complexity of aesthetics emerges. In truth, an artstyle can be as complicated, or as simple as you want it to be. Generally, we don't target the complexity of a visual style — but rather — we consider how it makes us feel.

# Realism vs Stylization
GTA V is an example of extreme realism. Capturing life-like details, down to the single square-inch of every part of it's world. It is a style very easily to replicate with references, but extremely challenging to express. Anime, on the other hand, is a blend between both realism, and stylization. Often utilizing realistic human scales, with exaggated features, to achieve a stylized, unique look for each character. Anime, is the most expressive blend between style & human. On the far side of the spectrum, is extreme stylization. Into the spiderverse is an example, often utilizing elements from realism, semi-realism, and extreme stylization.
