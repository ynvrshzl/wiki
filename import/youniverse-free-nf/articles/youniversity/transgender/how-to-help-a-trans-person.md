### How to help a transperson

> **Summary:** 
> _regardless of my beliefs or religion, I must treat human beings & all living things with the love & kindness they deserve. That is my religion._

If you are a parent, reading this, and you have someone in your family that has a trans identity, i urge you, set aside whatever you believe in, and love that person. They need to feel accepted, becuase their life is going to be made worth living if even just one person loves them for who they are. The saddest part to me about being a transwoman, is how even the people closest to you, never asked you once: "what is it like to have your life? what do you go through everyday, that we can't see?" i urge you, don't put your beliefs above loving another human being. Suicide is dangerously common in our community, but all it takes is one supportive person, and someone's life could be saved.
