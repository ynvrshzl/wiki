title: Hyperfocus & Hyperfixation
---


One of the biggest misconceptions about ADHD is the inability to focus. *This is incorrect* — rather — ADHD is an inability to control focus. We don't have control over what we are interested in, however, if we learn [how ADHD focus works](#how-adhd-focus-works), we can give our bodies the environment to thrive & be happy!
