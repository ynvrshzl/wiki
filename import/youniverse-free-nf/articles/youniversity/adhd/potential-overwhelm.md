Not knowing what to work on. Not having the right words to say. Having it all in your head but by the time it comes out of my mouth, it's already lost.


After immense amounts of research, self-studying, medicating & finding my way in this dark forest, I've realized one thing: my perspective decides everything in my life.


One of the most powerful perspectives in my life is a simple quote, which I decided to turn into a character. There is no problem in this world that my mind cannot solve. All of the answers exist in life, not in death. It only takes time, for any problem to be solved.


After seeking & searching this world for answers


This is one of the biggest problems in my life that I'm trying to solve. Perhaps it's not a problem, it's an obstacle that reveals something about me, that nothing else can: my immense potential. Like a child, a child is all about potential.
