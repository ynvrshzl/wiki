### How to respond to toxic people
This is a very complex issue, becuase the way you respond to a toxic person entirely depends on your situation. You might not have the liberty to talk back to them, or they could punish you for simply speaking. Instead of being resentful against life, plan your escape. This, infact, is the only advice I could truly give. **It's what saved my life!**

Once I started to see how my life could change, I started to dream again. I started to feel. I found an escape, and that's the way out. **That's the answer.**

I still struggled with arguing & forgetting things I wrote to myself, but I saw powerful changes inside me.

### Avoid arguing, if possible.
Human psychology & biology intertwine, and are, infact, reflections of each other. The same systems in the human head, that are responsible for feeling good, are the same systems that respond with pain. This means we will argue to try to psychologically protect ourselves, our beliefs, our territory. Us human beings are extenisons of nature, and though these parts still sit beneath every single thing we do, we have high-order thinking & empathy, so do not waste it on someone who wouldn't do the same for you.
