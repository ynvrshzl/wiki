Are you struggling to weave together the fabric of your stories? Perhaps you're struggling

You don't have to write a story from start-to-finish. Infact, if you examine your favorite stories, you will realize, almost none of them are linear.

Write small little snippets of scenes. Let them speak for themself. Link them together like building blocks, into chapters. These chapters are now modular: they can be switched & re-arranged. 

1. Small snippets of stories
2. Connect them together in a larger chapter
3. Connect the chapters together
