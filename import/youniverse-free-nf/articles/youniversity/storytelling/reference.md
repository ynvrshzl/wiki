# Reference
Learning to use real-world references to breath life to our stories. Note, this isn't necessarily about realism, rather, it is about believability in our stories. The same way anatomy is not required to draw characters, but to evoke connection in a character, anatomy is just a layer of language.


Just like a painter builds their visual library, or a cook learns by tasting food, a storyteller uses references from life itself. from other books, from stories, from movies, from paintings. storytelling is the most infinite art form, so take inspiration from everywhere to build your stories from your imagination. use your imagination to travel  to these stories.
