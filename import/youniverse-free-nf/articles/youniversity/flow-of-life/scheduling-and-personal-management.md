### The Flow of life
Along the hierarchy/flow of re-buildng your life, it's essential to avoid distractions when you want to work/focus. Distractions however, can save your life, so it's a careful balance between focus + play. You can set that up with a [schedule]()


This is how I schedule my day, which naturally builds the week -> month -> season -> year -> life. Ofcourse, in theory this is delicious (lol what) but actually getting there has been alot of work!

I have a simple second-brain/personal wiki. Using obsidian + javascript, I can manage my busy brain & help me focus on what brings my life the most meaning.

the purpose of my second-brain/personal-wiki is:

bare-minimum input -> insightful. output
daily planner -> contains areas to focus on 
project page -> contains overall abstracted  progress tracking & all tasks in project


managing projects, tasks & chores
this is where having a second-brain helps so much. it's essentially your external permanant storage system. your waking mind is like RAM, it's good for in-the-moment memory, but cannot handle long-term projects, ideas, details just slip away, like memory leaks in a computer program.

too much planning = analysis-paralysis. not enough planning = no direction.
the balance is to not write down everything but have enough direction.
