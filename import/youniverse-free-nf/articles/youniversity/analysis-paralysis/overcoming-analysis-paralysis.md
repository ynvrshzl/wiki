> Key lesson: don't let fear stop you from starting things you love. I don't have forever & death brings clarity to all things.

[Using checkpoints](abstraction.md)

Sometimes if it's hard for me to find a "good" place to begin a project i'll either indefinitely delay it, or obsessively collect information & analyze like a little bookworm (lol, i hope the cute worm turns into a butterfly soon.) 

But i've realized the things i've actually rendered in my life, have been things that i know i could have done better, but had the courage/love to begin them as sloppily & scared as i did. 

I guess my lesson here is that the only way for me to speak a language, is by having the courage to walk on broken legs first, and do it sloppy. That's the promise that analysis makes, but gives me paralysis instead (hey that rhymes!)
