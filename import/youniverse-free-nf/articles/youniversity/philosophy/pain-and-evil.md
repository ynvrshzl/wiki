# Pain, evil & human nature
This is one of the most uncomfortable areas of life for me, becuase i've seen things you can't just forget. They stay with you... forever. Good thing nature evolved healing as a superpower... becusae even if you have the scars of what you've survived, you've passed it.

Pain is a side effect of being alive evil is a side effect of consciousness. Here's a thought experiment: *is fire bad when it touches your skin?* When we use it to cook our food, it becomes a necessity so it fulfills its purpose. it is not fire that is evil, but the intention of the human wielding it. this is where all morality comes from.

It is unfortunate that nature forced us into genders. That is where power structues evovled in nature, and that is also where most of human evil exists, taking advantage of others with less than you.
