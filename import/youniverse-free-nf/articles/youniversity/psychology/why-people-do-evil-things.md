**Why do people do evil things?** Short answer: some part of them is brutally unhealthy.

To me, there is no tragedy that cuts deeper than that of an incomplete life, and when people choose to do evil things, it hurts me the most, becuase paradoxically they know what they are doing is wrong, but at the same time, they have no idea the grave mistake they are making. You cannot judge all of life through a lens, you have to see everything as a whole, and when you do, it terrifies you, becuase you realize at the higher orders of reality, evil & good are actually order & disorder. 

For example, if a paper cuts on your skin, it's not it's fault, it's design met the wrong thing, your sensitive human skin, and it hurt you as a result.
