Organizing your day:

There are countless ways to organize & plan your day. 
Things i've tried: creating flat checklists, nested checklists, checklists with headings, lists, nested lists, atomic files (morning, afternoon, evening), files with properties (linking time & date in file).

All of these aren't wrong, they are just different designs for problems. So we go back to the original question: what do I need in my day? Above that, what brings me the most meaning & fullfillment in my existence?
