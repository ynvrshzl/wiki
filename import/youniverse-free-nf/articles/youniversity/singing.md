# Singing
Welcome to the Youniversity singing club!

When you're singing, the most important thing is feeling. No, not that feeling, i mean the way it feels! But feeling is pretty important too. Think of it this way, when exercising, the trainer will clarify *where* it should feel. That helps so much, way more than trying to copy someone's exact pose. Why isn't it the same way in singing? There's all this talk of position, tone, breah support, vowels, phonation. Dude, what does *ANY* of that feel like?! That helps me more than anything!

# 👉 Singing is all about feeling.

Not just emotional feeling, but the physical sensations you get in your body — the buzz, the openness, the space, the ease. All that “technical gibberish” (projection, resonance, larynx, vowel modification) is really just different ways of describing how those sensations happen. You’ve already found the thread that matters: if it feels sustainable and expressive, that’s the path.
