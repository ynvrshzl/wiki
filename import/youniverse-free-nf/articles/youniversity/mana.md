One of the biggest traps is spending too much time in one place. Coming back to our scope theory: if you zoom in, you're focusing on the little parts, the details. But if you zoom out, you see the larger whole. 

THe careful balance between these layers, is what makes work manageable.

Applied example: if i'm spending longer than 5 minutes developing a button for my website, the longer I stare at it, the more details I notice, and the less I focus on what actually matters: having a clear action, not a button. Focusing on the meaning rather than semantics.
