# Colors of the Youniverse

- **Red:** is the color of beginnings. it is the most primal color, objectively, fear drives us. Red is often seen in nature to signal danger, and errors. Blood, spiders, fire are all red. 
- **Pink:** represents motherhood, healing, peace, and hope. Pink is the only color that does not exist in the rainbow, and yet, it is the bridge between red, and purple, the first & final colors of the rainbow.
- **Purple:** is the last color of the rainbow. Consequentially, it represents the final parts of everything, death, hopelessness, night, endings.
