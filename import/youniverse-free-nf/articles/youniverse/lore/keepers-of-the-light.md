In the Younvierse, there are keepers of light & darkness. They are the balancers of the cosmos within us. They are the angel & the devil on everyone's shoulder.

As the being's eyes rapidly flutter & dance all around, the being calmly speaks:
"You cannot see the co-relations within all of existence. I can see them. I cannot feel any longer, but these eyes I have been gifted with, I have traded my very soul for the power of the Gods. I can see the complexities of life. I see the traces of being. You do not understand. You do not have the eyes to see what I see."
