### Spirit animals
"We are the animated dust of the earth. The living beings who share our code."

In the Youniverse, [Dawn Zen]() was the first to introduce the idea of "Spirit animals." That within each & every one of us, we have a unique inner self, and with that, one core emotion that drives who we are. That emotion, can be linked to a living being. Wether the grueling hardness of patience, like the shell of an ancient tortoise, or the piercing eyes of a hawk, like the focus of a genius, or a predator.

"A human being is two parts, man, and spirit."

When we learn to speak it's language, we learn it's ways, and we are no longer controlled by the animal self. It is no longer the untamed beast controlling us, but rather, an ally, fighting alongside us, in our life.
