Emotions are the core of the Youniverse. They are the points of light that allow us to see in the infinite void of black.

Emotions, experiences & connection are the 3 fundamental parts of a human beings life. In the Youniverse, the emotions are a spectrum. They are like stars, nebulas, galaxies. The emotions shape the canvas of your Youniverse, the experiences create memory, the connections make them real.

Some emotions are in the center, and others are highly specific, expanding to the far outer regions of space. Each emotion, could be visualized as a point of light, and personas, are born from these emotions. 

What are emotions? In the Youniverse, emotions are e-motions: **energy in motion.**
