The mirror world is an imaginary place, where everything is a reflection. This world was my effort at understanding why life is worth living, when so much pain is unfair. This world we live in, it's definitely out-of-balance, and sometimes, bad things just happen. I try to understand why, or atleast, believe that there's a greater, cosmic reason for all of this, but as far as I'm able to see, the natural world reveals to me there are alot of issues in nature, and we, as humans, could solve them. Nobody has the strength to save the world, but my will... in my imagination... I can do anything.
