# 정신계 
**(Jeongsingye)**

정신계 (Jeongsingye) when translated from Korean: "The Unseen Realm" is the space living within all of us, aswell as the world we cannot see through our human senses. This is the space where all emotions, memories, dreams, and the hidden parts of life, all exist in the unseen realm. 

# The story of this world

Nobody knows where the world came from, but perhaps it was born together with the Youniverse... perhaps it was the beginning of the Youniverse.

유령 "Yuryeong" translated from Korean: "ghost" or "spirit." 


### Connections to the rest of the YNVRS
The unseen realm, is a portal for all those worlds we can't see, but our bodies can feel. There are worlds that exist inside us, and outside us. Worlds of pain, of happiness, of joy, of connection. All these elements give life to a being, a persona.

Throughout the Youniverse, persona's learn to harness the invisible worlds. Either to hurt, or heal.

Emotions animate us, but so do demotions, lesser emotions that cross the boundary of "the in-between" and force personas into lower realms of being.

We may fight in the physical space, without magical superpowers, but we live in a shared space — our simple words have the power to shape the inner reality of someone else. Your words can either create or destroy someone. The inner worlds are mostly buried beneath [the waters of the being](the-water-of-life.md), which hides almost 99% of an entire persona. Before we understand the waters, we must first understand the persona. They are a way of the Youniverse to experience itself. 


This is made especially apparent in most the timelines of the Youniverse, we have a physical world, and many, many other worlds that we cannot see.
