# The children fo the research
The researchers examined the children. It was a terrifying experiment. Some children were violent, and others, played with their toys like normal children. Few, however, did unspeakable things, too horrific to share in these documents. 

These children were all test subjects, they grew up to be strategically placed in different life scenarios, in order for the researchers to understand human biology, nature, and most of all, carry out the mission of the founder Mooder: to lessen the suffering of all mankind.

These children, were all given one emotion that would govern them throughout their entire lives. Monitored closely for every breath they took, they were never told the truth of their nature. Their parents, were slaughtered in the researching process. According to the founders, this was necessary, for many to be saved, few had to suffer.
