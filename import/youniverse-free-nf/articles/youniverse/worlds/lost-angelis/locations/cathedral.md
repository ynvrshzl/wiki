# The cathedral of Lost Angelis

There are many statues of angels. The angels are the lost angels of Lost Angelis. In memory of children, in memory of adults, of people who no longer exist. These statues serve as a memory for them all. Mysteriously, some of the statues have tears, as if they have been crying. 


This dark cathedral, constructed from the very beginning of Lost Angelis, was a glorious & holy sacred place, were the early human beings gatherd to worship & remember the fallen. *"Though our brothers & sisters have fallen to the old earth, let us keep them in our hearts, and build the city from their ashes, so that they may rest, in peace, forevermore."*


One of the most ominous sounds is the bell. Nobody knows who's ringing it, but sometimes at night, you can hear it
