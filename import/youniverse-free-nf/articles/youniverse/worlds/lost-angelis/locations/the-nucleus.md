# The core
This terrifying behemoth of pipes, wires, and a beating heart, that runs through the entire city, is the main powerhouse that provides the city of Lost Angelis with the harbringer of energy sources. This city consumes an Ungodly amount of energy, and the pollution to the ocean, creates an even worse enivornment for energy.

The main source of energy is Hydro-thermal. A technology developed by [the early settlers of the city,]() when they first started building the underwater oasis.
