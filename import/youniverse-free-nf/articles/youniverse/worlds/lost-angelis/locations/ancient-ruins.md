Throughout the wilderness, surrounding the city, are scattered ruins. From the aftermath of [The Old Earth.]() These ruins have been aged by thousands of years since the disaster, everything faded into a pale ghostly white stone. Beneath these ruins, are underground enviornments deemed too toxic for human life to be sustained. 

In the city of Lost Angelis, there are underground ruins, from the world that once was, above the city. This place is forbidden, yet not guarded. It is said it is such a toxic, dangerous place, that not even machines can survive. And yet, some adventurous, rebel-spirited youth call it home.
