"The life of a worker in Lost Angelis, is slavery with fairy dust. Sure, you get benefits such as: getting to see all the pretty people and places, as you squeeze your malnourished body into a tightly packed public transportation unit, and then, you're working at the same place, for the rest of your life, until you either die of 'natural causes' or you just give up on life. Alot of workers in this city have committed sepukku, that's the word for self-death. Since nobody is allowed to write the forbidden 'S' word, we were forced to make our own language down here. Speaking of 'down here' there's alot of rich folk, which I'm sure belong to the Mooderian family. That family is anonymous, nobody knows who's really beneath the masks. It could be children, your colleagues, your partner, your parents. You really aren't safe anywhere in this world, other than the few inches inside your skull. But hey, atleast 'smile' becuase that 'boosts serotonin.'"
