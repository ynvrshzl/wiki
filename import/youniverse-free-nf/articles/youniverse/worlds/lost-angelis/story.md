The message of the story of Lost Angelis is one of many, but for me, this story represents the cycle of life & death, with an emphasis on human kind. The nihilists wanted to extinguish life. Policital parties emerged naturally as a cause of disconnection between beliefs. Their beliefs became so strong they were willing to hurt others & even themselves, for the sake of keeping true to what they believed in. The nighilists wanted to extinguish life, so much so that they almost succeeded... an infact, if they went out all force, they would almost succeed, every single time. Life would always continue, the cycles of birth, life, death, and rebirth would continue, and the nihilists would see that life can never be destroyed. 

The mooder scientists had gone too far, they had mixed biological life with artificial technology, birthing a new power that was so immense, even the storyteller feared it's impossible magnitude. "Kinetic consciousness."
