Noteleks is the research hub of Lost Angelis.

*"Noteleks research - making perfect biology."*
The name "Noteleks" is the word "Skeleton," reversed. The researchers of Noteleks use real human skeletons as a basis to create synthetic life. *"Nature gave us the blueprint, and we, the hand of gods."* The research was funded in good heart: to someday reduce suffering of all human kind. But everything went terribly wrong in the end. 

Noteleks research labs is responsible for the birth of the personas in Lost Angelis. From Joey Doom, to the very Mooder children themselves. Each person was given an emotion.
