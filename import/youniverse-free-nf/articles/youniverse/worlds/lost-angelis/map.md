# A map of Lost Angelis
The city is formed as onion layers, with walls circling from the center, to the outside. This design allowed the Mooders to design a rank system, which the city is founded on. The design incorporated a wall outside, guarding the country from the city, and the walls inside, which were much smaller in radius. This design, was intentional, to keep the outsiders in their own level.

This is where the order of the city comes in. The militia are the law in the city. They decide who gets access to the ranks.
