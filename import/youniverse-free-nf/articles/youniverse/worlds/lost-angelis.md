Lost Angelis is the main city of the stories of the youniverse. Including [The Rebellion,]() [the Gang of LA,]() [The School of Gothika,]() [The Pirates]() and [The Mooders]()

The city of Lost Angelis is one of many cities, inside a larger world called The Atlas. This world was artificially constructed as a dream. The dreamer is unknown, but the living members feel alive & real as part of their world.
