# The Abyss
*Where all hidden things dwell, deep within.*

The Abyss is the isolated parts within every persona, that the outside world cannot see. It is where all the emotions exist, all the memories, and all of the parts of the persona, exist. Therefore, this could be considered the one of the most important worlds in the vast Youniverse. 

The abyss is the darkest world in the youniverse. This world, exists inside all of us, and has infinite layers. There is no black that is dark enough to describe this world, it is so impossibly horrifying that suffering can become inverted... akin to a supernova black hole.

The abyss can feel immensely isolating & punishing. This is why the Abyss is mostly cold, made of ice, and entering it's waters burns your skin so much you shed old versions of yourself.

In the Abyss, you will see the most hidden parts of yourself. It is not necessarily a scary place, but it is terrifying, becuase here, you are all alone, inside your own subconscious.

The Abyss is the bottom of the iceberg you can't see. Inside the iceberg, lives your memories.

> "Pain, is meant to be fought against, push against, with every pulse of your veins, you must fight, survive, or sleep. There are no laws, no rules, in this world. Why? Why all this pain? You must find your own reason. Only you can decide what is worth in this world suffering for. You have a self in the abyss. Your self is in danger, they are drowning & their life is draining from their eyes. 

You try to save, to rescue, yourself from the Abyss... swimming down into the depths to save the life of your darkest parts... but only if they want to keep living... they might weigh you down with them. It is not their fault, it is the nature of the abyss.

Only one person is all it takes to save them from the abyss.

The abyss is haunting, it decays slowly, silently. It isn't a catastrophic assault, it is the slow, numbing, cold, isolating world where your soul is silently brittled until there is nothing left of you. Your memories, dreams, hopes, all fade in the abyss, as it desceneds further & further, into the blackest void without end. But descend far enough, beyond reason, you will find the fire that kept you alive all this time.
