The story of the Gang of LA is a tragic journey of troubled youth, lost & directionless, but desperate to make changes happen in the world. They saw the evil & cruelty of human kind, and those that said they were "kind & good" people, were'nt doing anything about the sick children, or the homeless mothers living in the streets. Enough was enough, and one day, a group of friends got together, with the fractured magic of childhood imagination, they imagined what it would be like to take over the world. "What would we do? There's so much ugliness in this world, sometimes I don't want to be alive. This world is broken." The children were already hopeless, and the cruel life of Lost Angelis, only made it worse. But in-between the late-night conversations, the children realized there were differences, even amongst themselves. Natrually, two groups were formed, one group that would fight for justice, and another group, that would destroy the system from inside, out. 

"You can't fix the world by breaking it to pieces." "I don't want to fix the world, I want to watch it burn." 

The groups would be known as "The Rebellion" and "The Gang." Many other groups would form throughout the city of Lost Angelis, none with it's influence as deep as these two.
