# The Rebellion
The Rebellion are a group of lawless youth from the city of L[ost Angelis.]() They were all orphans, seeking to rebuild a world, devoid of meaning & falling to pieces. Where young people were tired of seeing suffering, they willingly ended their lives as a plea of protest, feeling so powerless under a collosal system, they pleaded with their lives.

The rebellion is one of the most important stories in my life. These stories have saved my life countless times. During the most hopeless times in my life, I've recorded my hopelessness within these stories, and they have helped me to imagine worlds, people & solutions to the biggest problems & questions in my life.

The rebellion learned to meditate. To remain calm. The city had a sacrificial economy. If you were not intelligent enough, you would be banned from basic goods in the city, and would be kicked out into the outside walls. The rebellion had to learn to master over their emotions and keep hope, in a world that seemed to crush them from every angle
