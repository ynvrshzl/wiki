# The Mooders
The name "Mooders" came from a single person who decided to form a family, that would lessen the pain of all humanity. "Doom" was the family name of a rebel, who incited rebellion: "each person had the right to die. each person had the right to form their own family, their own beliefs, their own way of life." These radical ideas stirred agianst the vision of the family, which was to create a single language for all of humankind, so that noone would ever have to suffer.

# Masks
Nobody knows why the mooders wear masks.




The Mooders, were a group of people who ruled over the city of [Lost Angelis](#lost-angelis). They had a dream of building a city that never aged… Where health and wealth was available to all, free of cost. where no one ruled over society, but instead, a unique system was created so that anyone of high intelligence, would get higher levels of access within the city.

The story of the mooders is closely intertwined with the city, it was their purpose to create a world without pain. A world without suffering. A world where children could run free. A utopia for humanity, but only, if you deserved to be here. The systems of Lost Angelis have been designed to stop human beings with evil intentions, from existing. That's right. If you have greed, gluttony, or even express free thought, you would be terminated.

During the climax of the story, groups from the city break into the walls, and tear everything apart. The adult figures tell the younger members: "Protect the family at all costs." Frantically, The youngest members run through the halls & underground chambers of the family home, desperately protecting the family.
