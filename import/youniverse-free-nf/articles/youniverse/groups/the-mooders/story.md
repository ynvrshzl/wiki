# The Story of The Mooders
*A royal family, torn apart by it's own children.*

The Original Mooders had a much different vision to what the new-age leaders have created. But, the Prince who created the city, already predicted the downfall of human kind. In his own words: **"Yet, we are so stubborn & beastly, that we will persist like a parasite on this Earth. Refusing to let go of what is dying & falling apart, we leech onto this thing we call life... but weren't the first ones here."**

The evolution of the Mooderian family became corrupt, and wanted luxury for the monarchs, and extinction for the peasants. However, without the peasants, there would be nobody to work for the monarchs. "Their backs will the soil to build this Empire, and at last, my family will have peace, the deserving." This concept of "The Deserving" grew rampant within the complicated political systems of the Mooders. Originally, they decided to keep everything simple: no politics, no economy, no systems.
