The reason the Mooders became so aggressive towards the nomads, rebels & outlaws, were because the Mooders were following a constitution plan, set by the original founding members. "In order to avoid a world-ending catastrophe, we must ensure that all citizens of humanity are to be regulated & kept in optimal health. We must destroy all disease, illness & above all, stop all rebellion against the parties."

They were not always this way, and in the beginning, the Mooders & the Rebels, shared a home. They were united by the aftermath of war. They bonded as human beings, regardless of their skin color, religion, beliefs or intelligence. Everyone had a place in the new world, and all that mattered was that you were alive & healthy. However, as human beings began to get more comfortable, rebuilding modern life, they began to lose sight of the harsh times, and they were dissasisfied with the luxuries of modern life. They wanted more. They wanted more pleasure. They wanted more luxury. They wanted more systems to create a "difference" so that only few could enjoy the luxuries of life inside the walls, while the backs of the enslaved, were carrying the weight of the world, on their tired shoulders.
