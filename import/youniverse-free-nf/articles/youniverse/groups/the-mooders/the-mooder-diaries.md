# The mooder diaries are a collection of the lives of the Mooders. Specifically, Sonya, Mabe Linn, Jack

From Mabe Linn & Sonya's diary:
*"Today we had the best day ever. Woke up nice and early with the sun. Went shopping with my friends for new victory dresses for the ball later tonight. It's going to be a splendid night indeed! Sweet as the strawberries on my skin, as we relax in this sauna, melting all the stress away."*

Jack's diary:
*"What a terrible day. It was lovely, and I saw so many naked women, unfortunately, that's a common thing around here, and I've even seen my sisters do it. Disgusting. Putrid human beings. I will slaughter them all."*
