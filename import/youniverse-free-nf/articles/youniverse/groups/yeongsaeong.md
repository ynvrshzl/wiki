# 영생 Yeongsaeng
Hip hop dance team from the future

They use instruments, sounds & music as weapons to fight.

Yeongsaeong fight against the enemy of their future: the rich who hate humanity. They hide messages within their music, that can bypass censorship & share their message of hope for the youth.

The name "Yeongsaeong" translated from Korean: "Eternal." Describing their music as timeless & boundless, each sound a layer of weapons against the enemy. What enemy do Yeongsaeong fight against? [Read more here](yoengsaeong/enemy.md)

**Yeongsaeong are a hip-hop dance team from the future.** They come from the future with hidden messages in their music. They created music to dance to, as a way to get young people moving around, after a collapse of mental & physical health during their times. It worked, it worked so well infact that they became scouted by a large team of record labels. These record labels promised them fame & fortune at the cost of something simple: their free will. Yeongsaeong had no choice, as a team, they decided to accept the offer. They realized the record label would try to use them as puppets, to control everything they do, for fame & fortune. But Yeongsaeong was always futuristic, they were always ahead of their time. They knew this would happen, and it was all part of the design. "Sending signals to the past, so the future could be changed."

Yeongsaeong use their music as a way to communicate with the past human beings from their times, warning them that the world would become a wasteland without freedom, if they did not make the right decisions. Through their music, Yeongsaeong have been able to successfuly evade censorship from the world they live in, aswell as maintain their freedom in the music. While they are slaves to the system, in the music, the dancing, the weapons, they are limitless & free, just as human beings were designed to be.

"We will fight with you, the music shaping the world."
