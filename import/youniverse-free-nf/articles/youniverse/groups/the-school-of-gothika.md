# The school of gothika
*"Semper fidelis."*

The school of gothika is a secret brotherhood in [Lost Angelis.]() They are primarily focused on lessening the suffering of all humankind, and all living beings. Secondary goals are finding the answers to reality, dark human psychology, and carrying out the prophecy of the end of all humankind. 

# Joining the brotherhood
Despire the name, male, female, and anyone can join the brotherhood.

The prophecy reads: "In the last years of humanity, there will be a collapse of meaning. Psychological amnesia, torture, and pain in all forms will destroy the host from inside, out. The parasite, must be stoopped at all costs."

The school of gothika does not allow any of its members to reveal their identity.

The school of Gothika exists to understand the darkness within human beings. Many of the members have ended their lives after having seen too much. Almost as if something, somewhere has put a limit on how much knowledge we human beings are allowed to have about this world. The school of Gothika eventually discovers that they are living in a fictional world inside stories. The main goal of the school is to save humanity from the darkness of "the shadow psyche." We must understand evil, if we are to obliterate it from our species. It is our only hope remaining.

The school realized that in the study of understanding, we risk becoming what we despise. An alcheimc paradox. Nobody believes them, until someone comes into the school...

They developed a langauge only the members could understand. Entirely symbolic — driven by body langauge.

The school of gothika weren't figures for evil, but infact, during the daytime, many blended into normal society, as nuns, teachers, and towns folk. They believed in "The Virtues" which was a school of teachings, meant to guide the young to live a proper life. Socializing, Human psychology, Inner healing, and more devastating but necessary truths to live a proper human existence.
