# Worlds
In the Youniverse, worlds contain areas of a persona.

Exploring thoughts & philosophies, ideas & possibilities in your imagination is alot like exploring worlds. The more time you spend thinking about things, the more of those worlds you experience. You can tell when someone hasn't thougnt about something, it's alot like being a foreigner to a new country.

Within every persona, exists the worlds. These are worlds that are present in everyone, regardless of your unique parts.
