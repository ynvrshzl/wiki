If you're looking for a story that explores meaning & human nature, read ["The Rebellion"](#the-rebellion-story)

Want to read something more lighthearted? check out [The adventures of pilot Aries](#the-adventures-of-pilot-aries)

- ["The Gang of LA"](/db/youniverse/books/the-gang-of-la.md) follow a brutal journey of lost teens, searching for meaning in a broken world. Each member writes down their experiences in their personal journals, the book, is a stiched-together work of all of their writings.
- ["The Rebellion."](/db/youniverse/books/the-rebellion.md) similar to the Gang of LA, The Rebellion are born in a hopeless time for humanity. Together, they must rekindle the light to stay awake, in a world that wants everyone asleep.
- ["The Adventures of Pilot Ares"](/db/youniverse/books/the-adventures-of-pilot-ares.md) follow the imagination of a girl, stuck in paralysis. Using her mind to explore the lives she could have had, friends, people, places, emotions, memories. "They may never become real, but atleast in their world, I have a place where I belong."
