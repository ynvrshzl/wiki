# Eternal Qombat
*(Video game concept)*

A fighting game in the Youniverse. Like super-mash bros. A collage of all stories into one place.

Top-down camera, turn-table camera. Point & click movement.

The cover art of EQ will be a dark, terrifying image of Joey Doom, with his ninja gear, looking up, in a spiritually defeated expression.

Eternal Qombat is a non-existent game about emotions surviving in a video game world. These emotions are playable as people, objects & worlds.

# Dynamics
Emotions are the dynamics, of life, and of eternal qombat. Depression drains your energy, but it also protects you from sadness, which can be deadly. Narcissism is nearly indesctructable, but is countered by truth. Elements & emotions, like buffs & debuffs in a classic RPG.

# The worlds of Eternal Qombat
Eternal Qombat shares a megaverse, made of many worlds. Each world, is an emotion, each persona, a fragement of that world.

# Story
The researchers created experimental enviornments, fully virtual, equipped with real-time full body senses. These emotions were created to train, to see who in the end could survive the tragedies of life.

# Emotions
Emotions are the core of this game, just like the Youniverse. But eventually, the souldiers learn, this is not a game, but **a life simulator, to see who can survive to the end.**

Each emotion isn't about winning but expressing & evolving. The person who survives to the end, wins the tournament.

# Items
- **The Visor:** this allows persona's to see inside the unseen realms. Emotions, waves & health are all visible through the interface. Some personas' can even read thoughts. Every persona's interface is younique to them.
- **Motions**
- **Energy**
- **Gadgets** some personas are tinkerers. They can create things to help them fight in the arenas.

# Events
Events aren’t skins, but World Events of Emotions: (like "Bloom Season" or "Rage Day") where certain emotions unlock new potential forms, based on seasons, cycles & more.

# Ranks, progression
Progression is not linear rank-up but Emotional Integration Paths (e.g., Wrath evolves into Assertiveness).


# Design
Instead of forcing each persona into a box, assembling "emotion shards" to craft your persona's kit for each match.

- **Progression** is not linear rank-up but Emotional Integration Paths (e.g., Wrath evolves into Assertiveness).
- **Kills** Wins aren’t "kills," but Breakthroughs—moments where your persona evolves emotionally.



# Vision
This game should capture my attention, and keep me hooked with the stories of each character. The main hook of this game, are the characters, just like League of Legends. Except, the game itself is meant to be therapeutic, not rage-inducing. At no point should this game ever cause you to feel isolated or stuck. Keep it moving with rotating game-sets, if you don't do so well in one area, you can always move on to the next, just like life.

A blend of philosophy & game.
