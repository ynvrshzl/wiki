# Imagescape
*Brought to you by the Youniverse*

Enviornment simulator, with an emotionally evoking engine. Collect items from worlds to use in your own, fully interactive enviornments, immersive sounds, and a community dedicated to providing virtual experiences to explore solo or with other imagineers!

# Mentally, emotionally, psychologically healing 
The first of it's kind. We need to tap into the unkown power of art, especially in our current state of human kind.

The name itself evokes "escapism." We live in a very unnatural world, and it will only get worse as technology progresses, however, I believe through the double-edged blade of technology, we can solve nature's deepest issues.
