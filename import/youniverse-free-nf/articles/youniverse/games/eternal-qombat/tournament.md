### The "Tournament" games

The "Tournament" games not only serve as the core gameplay of Eternal Qombat, but also the main story of the game. The game mixes real-life lessons & philosophy, with challenging, rewarding, addictive gameplay, so players are hooked, to something that they will benefit from in their lives. Each character holds their own unique perspective & wisdom about life through their eyes. 

For example, [Yami's]() dialogue contains snippets of his greatest life lessons, like forgiveness, revenge & kindness. Meanwhile, a darker persona, like [Myrrhael]() will share their beliefs about the cruelty of existence. Neither is right nor wrong, they simply share their story with the player, mostly, through the most accessible form: their spoken dialogue.
