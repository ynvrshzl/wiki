### Analysis

Fortnite has a fun, timeless cartoonized product. It's colors are punchy, and characters are expressive with their proprtions, while maintaining a humanoid feel. Despite the cartoonized styles, there are thousands of skins & looks, each completely unique. It's enviornment is fun & magical, inspired by games like World-of-Warcraft. It is an example of simplicity & creativity. 

Into the spiderverse is the most vibrant & unique art-style i've ever seen.

Tekken 8 has a hyper-realism approach to fighting. 

Persona 5 has a super stylized anime aesthetic, Bold outlines, comic-book, juvenile aura. Arcane has an eye-catching, unique painterly look, captures human emotion in a static medium.

DMC 5 is another great example of hyper-realism, magical effects, demonic enemies, badass fighting experience. The charcters take center stage (literally on-screen) and the camera dynamically pans between the character & the enemy, allowing the player to focus on the fight, rather than camera angles. This makes the character look powerful at all times.
