# Main gameplay of EQ
Who can survive the longest? Till the end?

The main gameplay of EQ is called: **"The Eternal Tournament"** are a set of brutal games, set within the psychological insides of your selected emotion. You can choose to play each game on it's own, or take part in the brutal tournament, which you only have one life and cannot restart.


A game about endurance, survival, and outlasting the odds stacked against you. Each character (persona) has someone unique about them, that makes them strong enough to survive anything.

You are dropped into a massive psychological world, set in the Youniverse. Alongside 50 other players. Whoever survives the longest, wins. However, this is not a game against each other, rather, 

**Tournamant games:**
1. **Training:** this is the world where you can train your body, mind & soul, learn new moves. You can fight against different versions of yourself, or other emotions.
2. **Tag team:** fight alongside your friend, in a series of matches, set in different worlds.
3. **Solo fight:** this game requires concentration & skill. 
4. **Final fight:** this is the game of endurance, without an end.
