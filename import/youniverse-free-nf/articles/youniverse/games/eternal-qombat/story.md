the lore & opening movie of eternal-qombat:

joey doom emotionlessly stares into an endless abyss. it is a void inside every human being. except there are beings living in it. the "unseen realms" are coming to destroy human beings. the void/abyss hate human beings. only when you wear the visor, can you see within the abyss & fight. some fight to protect their loves, others, fight because they have nothing left. the eternal tournament is brutal & deadly, a simulated environment meant to train & test the souldier to fight out into real qombat. many personas from around the youniverse gathered & assembled in squads. 

the abyss was growing stronger & more human beings were being taken into it. the ultimate level of training was fighting in the abyss, which is said to be even more brutal than the most intense military training.

nobody knows how the tournament ends, but one thing is certain, the qombat, is eternal.

(when you 'die' in eq, you re-incarnate as the same soul, but a new body. the more experience you gained  the further your evolution.)
