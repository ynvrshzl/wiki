# Umbra
suicide personified

This spirit is infact, shared amongst all of the death-related demotions. They are a straw figure, completely dead inside. And yet, they are possessed by the thing that wants to kill the persona. The straw figure points to directions across the world, either it ends in your death, or saving your life. Do you trust it? It isn't evil, it is the child within you that became sick in the world, now, they give you one last warning.
