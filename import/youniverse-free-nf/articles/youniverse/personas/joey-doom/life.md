# The life of Joey Doom
Joey Doom is yin & yang, personified as a man. Joey Doom has no emotions inside him. He has never felt happiness or joy. He's never laughed in his life. From the moment he was born he was a souldier for the military of the city.

"I must move forward, there is no time left."
The entire reason for Joey Dooms' existence is to test the limits of the human will: how much pain can a human being endure? 


I have seen the darkest of black. It is not in the creatures at the bottom of the sea, the darkest black exists inside human beings. Inside you, inside me. In paradox, the smallest point of light is enough to extinguish the
dark. 


![](https://youniverse.free.nf/media/img/joey-doom/blindfold.jpg)
"It is the darkness inside you, that you are most afraid of."

He was forged to be a biological soldier to be tested in the [The realm of emotion.]()

His eyes are closed becuase he is in a constant meditative poise. His body was engineered to deliver massive amounts of pain in waves, he suffers from chronic pain & extreme sensitivity to the world, therefore, he has trained his mind to protect himself against the immense stress, that would otherwise, instantly kill him.
