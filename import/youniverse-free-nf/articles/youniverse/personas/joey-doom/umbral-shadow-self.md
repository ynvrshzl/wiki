When Doom enters his umbral dark shadow self, he uses an extreme amount of energy. To the point where, if he spends too much time in this space, his body will eat itself in attempt to keep him alive. His body shrivels up like a withered tree. Once, Zaeyn noticed Doom awfully pale & ghostly skinny, and he did the most natural thing he knows: laugh & mock him. Zaeyn pretended to be dead & shriveled up like a skeleton, laughing at him. Katerina, scolded him: "THAT'S NOT FUNNY."
