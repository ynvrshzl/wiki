
# Joey Doom vs a million souldiers
The city of Lost Angelis was being ripped apart. People had lost hope, and threw the city to flames. The last resort? Trapping people in their own minds. This, was the beginning of the torture of Joey Doom.

Off in the distance, millions of souldiers swarm toward Doom. His friends watch helplessly as he calmly stamps his sword on the ground. Slowly, he puts on his  blindfold, and trusts his 4 senses.
