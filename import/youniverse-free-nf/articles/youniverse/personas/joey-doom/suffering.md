# 영락계
영락계 (Yeong-rak-gye)
Translation: “Realm of Soul Falling”

A dimension where the human psyche & body, meet together to form the soul. Human beings begin as lost, formless souls, and the more the soul experiences, the more they are formed by life. Suffering & healing are the primary ways of transcendence. 

The goal of this world is to transcend both suffering & love.

Inside all of us, exists this realm. Inside Joey Doom, he has been given a set of false memories, that will both torture him, and shape him, for all eternity. Doom must break free of this cycle, and see through the walls of hallucinated reality, so that he can come to terms with the highest, truest form of life. This is where the heaven concept 


Joey Doom spent his entire life being tortured inside of a imagined world in his mind. it was programmed within his genes, to construct a human being that could survive immense psychological suffering. but the experiments went wrong, and he was stuck in this world for the rest of his life. it could be compared to being awake during surgery, you are completely immobilized while your body is torn to shreds. and you can feel every excruciating inch. the world is not either heaven or hell, but a ground meant to train at ascend and transcend. this is why Joey Doom is often seen wearing red. this world is red. blood is what keeps all things alive. but is it also red, that keeps them in pain. Doom how to learn how to meditate to ease the suffering. he tried to kill himself but something, some are inside him kept him alive.

# 회색
회색 (hoesaek) translated from Korean: "Grey." The world of two's, of yin and yang. While Doom was tortured in this world, he also experienced the euphoria of healing. A bliss so powerful, no word could describe it's ecstasy. This, unboutedbly, kept Doom alive.
