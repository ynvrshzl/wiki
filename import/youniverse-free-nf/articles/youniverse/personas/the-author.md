**"The Author"** or simply **"Author"** is the storyteller of the Youniverse. We do not know enough about The Author, other than they are very mysterious, and have a connection to the outside world, the world outside the Youniverse. The Author seems to be the designer, of the worlds of the Youniverse. They, in some way, gave birth to the fabric of reality, but they do not control what happens in this world. The purpose of the Youniverse, is unknown, and perhaps, an intentional part of the design. The Author wishes to remain anonymous, neither a figure of light, or dark.
