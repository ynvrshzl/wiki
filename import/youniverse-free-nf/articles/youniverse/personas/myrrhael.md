"Give man bread, he will survive. Give man family, he will split power. Give man civilization, he will devour the whole earth."

Myrrhael is the leader of [the School of Gothika.]() Nobody knows who is underneath the mask. But his words have risen hundreds of people.

"Man, risen from the earth below, seeks to soar the skies above. A tragedy, humiliation... as comical as the body nature forced us in. We must transcend, into the cosmos, through the synthetic, into the void. Come with me, all who are ready, to see truth. To transcend beyond our limits."

"I will share with you something, it may shatter your very psyche. Don't listen to my words if you wish to remain in the illusion. You have been warned, my friends. This world, is a product of our flawed, mortal nature. There is a reason this world favors luxury above all else. Open your eyes, they will never favor your desires, sacrifice yourselves to the cause. Extinguish yourselves. Save this world of it's coming misery. "
