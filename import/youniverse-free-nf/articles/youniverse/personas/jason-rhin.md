# Jason Rhin

Jason Rhin is a tall, middle-aged man, who is often seen walking with a cane. Wearing all black, vintage cowboy leather attire, with a hood covering his face. He covers his face not becuase he's shy, but because his face freightens people. "Monster" they called him, as a young boy. For an accident that wasn't in his control, left his face disfigured, and he was shadowbanned from all of society. This is when he started an underground movement: **"The Rebellion."**

Jason gave a home to the homeless, he was seen as a fatherly figure for the Rebels. He developed a terminal illness from the untreated scars in his face, all becuase of his family name. A Mooderian officer could have saved his life, but after discovering his birth name, the officer left the young boy to perish on the cold streets of [Lost Angelis](public/youniverse/worlds/lost-angelis.md). Jason was so hurt, he was so done with this world, that he knew someday, he would make them pay. 

His real name? *"Jason Doom, the father of Doom, father, of the Rebellion."*

Later on his life, Jason's name began circling the underground streets of [Lost Angelis](public/youniverse/worlds/lost-angelis.md) An officer captured him... the same officer that abandoned him when he was young. Before Jason took his own life in a riot against the injust system of the Mooders, he wispered to the officer, looking her deep into her eyes: **"The Rebellion is coming."**

"You are a human being, capable of thinking for yourself. That's the real danger. That's why they want you asleep. But you, you have freedom. To choose if you want to live, or die. Knowing you could die, but choosing to live, that, is power."
