# Shadow boxing


When you have nothing to lose, how much are you willing to gain?

![](../../imgs/jhaydn/shadow-boxing.png)

"How much longer can I survive in this place. I don't even want to live anymore. It just hurts... It burns..."

Jhaydn practiced Shadow boxing on his own. No teacher, no trainer, no guide. Just him, and his own darkness. Fighting in the shadows.

> "I've survived the impossible. But I feel nothing inside. Victorious?"

There were days where he spent what seemed like EONS in his mind. Time perception slowed to a near halt, it was as if his body became frozen. Depression washed over everything he loved in his life until there was nothing but emotionlessless. He could no longer feel the fire, the rage, the fury, he once had, that fueled his every step.

He had to learn to be still. To meditate. To breathe in. It was when Jhaydn stopped resisting, that he reclaimed his emotions back from the shadows, and his prized fury, the fire that burns the heavens alight. Like a phoenix form the ashes, he was reborn.
