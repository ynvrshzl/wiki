Jhaydn joined an underground fight club, so he could have enough city status rank too fix his life. Nobody wanted to support him. "I have nobody in this world. I'm on my own. Well, I'll have a wife someday, a beautiful wife. And we'll make some more beautiful kids. But right now, I gotta feel this in the moment, the hate I feel for you J? Let that be the fire man, let that push you and make you stronger, cause right now they're right, you're weak, and in this world, you gotta be strong to protect who you love... Especially your future wife." But if he thought life in the surface was unbearable, the world underground would swallow him alive. People twice his size were fighting against him, breaking his bones & his frail body. But something, somewhere inside him refused to give up. He got up every single time. Often in the crowds Jhaydn would hear people talking: "That guy's a loser. He should kill himself man, what a waste."
