# Magdelene
Agoraphobia/Apathy personified

"I would end myself, but I will live & change this broken world. That is my revenge."

"When you've got no fight in you left, and nothing in this world matters, that's what it feels like to be me."

"I see the ground below, it calls my name. It will take all of us someday."

She constantly dissociates from the world around her.  Walls of amnesia protect her from her painful memories

- [Stitches](stitches.md)

Madeline is the most depressed version of me. She is a girl with goth lolita clothes & she loves to live in the dark. Madeline's entire childhood & teenagehood was spent indoors, staying awake at night, without any friends. Her parents isolated her to "protect" her from the big, bad world outside.

She developed permanant vision impairment — her eyes could only see in the dark, she could no longer see the sunlight. It screamed to her. The night was safe. The night, had shadow.
