"If I could solve all your problems & take away all your suffering... I would do anything to see you happy."

This was the dream of [Lady Mooder]() She created a hypnotherapy technique that used [Lucid Dreaming]() to make a person stay in their dreams, forever. She learned, form studying the books of the mind, by other great Mooder scientists, the human brain, and all it's functions, problems & mysteries.

The side effect of her practice? People went insane in their dreams. They only experienced pure joy. Pure ecstasy. But the human conscious, as she studied, was meant to thrive in a cocktail of stress & rest. A harmonious balance between both, is what gave life meaning, not the fantasy of dreams or the obliteration of pain.
