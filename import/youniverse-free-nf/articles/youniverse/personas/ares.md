### A summary of the life of Ares
The story of Ares is an example of how someone doesn't have to be physically abused, to be emotionally tormented. The things you say carry a weight to them, and a sensitive person, will remember the things you told them. Ares hit her breaking point when she tried asking for help

"Most people are horrible, but you aren't like most people."

Ares, was exhausted of this world. People had hurt her more than anything. Even the one person she thought she could trust, stabbed her in the back one day. She was completely alone, and on the verge of ending her own life. "I want to live in a world without humans." Ares, immersed herself in her own imagination, building her worlds out of the things she loved. Though she never let anyone close into her life, she met great people who felt exactly like her. Betrayed, hurt & exhausted. Ares then learned, she could have a life without people. They didn't have to rule over her, she was free. 

In her worlds, she was anybody she wanted to be. So, Ares gave herself friends. The first world however, was a world without human beings. Ares herself was the only human being left on Earth. She would spend an eternity in this world, and created a character: "The Child." Her child-self, was free to play, and explore this world. Animals & plants were no longer toxic, the world was perfect. But as Ares grew older in the world, she realized it did not feel real. Why did it matter that it was not real?
