# Serena
Motherhood personified

"This world is not your home my child, but if you stay here with me, it is more dangerous."

![](assets_task_01k1qfvg8tfe4vy5wa5t8ask3x_1754208842_img_1.webp)

Serena was a doctor in her life. When she died, fragments of her soul combined themselves into serenity, a world of peace. Her parts gave birth to the ancient lands of Youthanasia, carved from her immense love, it shaped the Youniverse itself.

"When someone dies, the lights go out of their eyes, and somewhere up above, a star explodes, leaving only scatterings of light. But they make room for someone new to come to this world."

"I wish I could save every little one in this world, but I am only one girl."

"Life is bittersweet, my sweetheart. But do not forget, who you are, the brightest light in the Youniverse."

"The world is a scary... Please don't leave me alone." the child begged to Serena. She looked into the child's eyes: "I want you to go see it. The big world." The child sobbingly insisted: "Please don't leave me." Serena hugged the child, softly wispering: "Yes. I know. But... it's more dangerous to stay with me." 

"You might lose your eyes out there in the world, but if you stay here with me, you'll lose your soul."
