I remember when my best friend ended his own life. I can't tell you how much that hurt. I hated everyone man. I can't believe people could be so cruel... worse than animals sometimes. But when I saw him again... after we had been split apart by my father... I saw him... he had changed so much. He was so much stronger than me. When he saw me I was so happy... but he had this look in his eyes... it's like he hated me too. After I saw him my heart was broken again... this time, I didn't want it to heal anymore. It was too painful to keep breaking & healing. I just wanted to die. I couldn't trust anybody in this world anymore. I hated everyone. I felt completely alone. And then, I heard the news, that he ended his own life. Ever since that day, a part of me is missing, forever.
