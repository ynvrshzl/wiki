# The Life of Yami
"And we lived like when we were kids, like when life felt like a medieval fairy tale"


Yami eventually ran away. And he lived just like a rebel. Without home. Without shelter. Without the "protection" of his father. He had no home. No food. No clothing. No hope. No plan. No future. But he had something... which was worth more to him than anything in this world. Yami — had **freedom.**


His father made a city-wide mission to find his son and retrieve him, but he was nowhere to be found. Eventually, they found him living with Joey Doom & Katerina. They discovered that their parents were taking care of them... and that is when they faced a cruel death...


"But i protected you from the world my son. All this time. And you chose to run away?"


The childhood of Yami was heavy with sorrow, scars but healing wounds. He found a way to survive the pain, both in his body & in his mind. He wanted to die every. single. day. However, soemthing that kept him going was the will inside him, no matter what, he was going to stay alive & be the person he never had. Be his own best friend. Fight for himself. Understand himself. Love himself. Protect himself. Protect his inner, wounder child self.
