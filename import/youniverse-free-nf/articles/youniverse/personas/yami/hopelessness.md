# '"It hurts to be alive sometimes."'

![](https://youniverse.free.nf/media/img/yami-snow.jpg)
Yami. That's my name. That's the name that was given to me. I can't remember by who. They said it was a special name... becuase I was a mistake... that's what they told me. Heh... sometimes I wonder why it hurts... it hurts so much.

When I see alot of people laughing... friends... having a good time together... I feel really sad & alone inside. My heart... my chest... there's something inside there... it hurts. I try to be my own best friend... but... I feel so lost in this world. Sometimes I don't know if I'm doing the right thing... or if I'm just... not meant to be here.
