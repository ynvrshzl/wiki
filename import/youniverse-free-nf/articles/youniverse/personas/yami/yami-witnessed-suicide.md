Yami thought to himself: "How can people just live like this everyday?"


He couldn't eat anything for weeks after that.


"That was a person... they had a life like me... and now they're gone. And I saw them in their last moments..."


When Yami witnessed this, it changed him forever. His heart was scarred, and he saw for a glimpse, behind the curtains of everyday life. He not only witnessed the final hopelessness of a human being, but the carelessness of the people around him. Everyone else on the city was just trying not to get involved. They don't see a human being who just ended their life, they saw a situation that could lower their status in the city. And in the city of Lost Angelis, [status is everything for a good life.]()
