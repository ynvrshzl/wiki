# The day Yami got out of isolation prison

Yami turned his situation aroudn when he realized he was in the best place of his entire life. He was isolated. He had nobody. But he had himself. He had all the time in the world to tap into his mind, lhis inner worlds, and build true strength. Eventually he became so powerful both physically & psychologically, that he was able to break free from the prison using his thoughts alone. He didnt' have to fight it with his strength... it was all in his imagination.

He was isolated for all of his teenage years.


Yami cries while hugging himself "Emotions are the most beautiful cruel thing in life. But thank you. Thank you so much for not giving up. Thank you."
