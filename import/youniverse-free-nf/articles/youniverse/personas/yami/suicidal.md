# Don't end your life.
"Don't end your life... unless you have nothing left to try... that's what I would tell anyone who was in my position. Just look at life, all around us. Everything change. Everything changes. So you and me must change too. This won't last forever... death? Death is forever... death... truly is the worst thing in this life... and nobody... nobody really wants to die. We just want to live. We want to live & feel. Feel everything. where even the bad things, have a purpose."

"Build your world. Strengthen your petals. Like the petals of a flower, be beautiful, be yourself, especially when you are surrounded by ugliness, that is the place you will be the most beautiful."
