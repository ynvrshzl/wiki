# Yami saves Joey Doom

![](https://youniverse.free.nf/media/img/yami/scream.jpg)
[Yami]() sees [Joey]() tying a rope around his neck. Yami comes close to Joey & puts his hand in the knot, stopping Joey from tightening it around his neck. Yami asks Joey calmly: "Hey, what are you doing?" Joey opens his exhausted eyes and stares at Yami, lifelessly. Beneath his tired breath, Joey wispers: "I want to die." Yami's eyes expand with shock. He thinks to himself: "He feels the same way I do... he's alone in this world." Yami puts his hand deeper in the knot, and JOey Doom has no energy in his body to fight back. He struggles as he slowly tries to kick Yami away: "Why are you doing this. You people are all the same. Why won't you just let me die."
