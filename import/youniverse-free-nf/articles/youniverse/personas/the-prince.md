The prince of Lost Angelis was the first founder of the city. He had no wife, nor family, but decided to create a world where suffering & pain didn't exist. Assembling a group of scientists, artists, thinkers and architects... with the few survivors of the world above, he was the mind that led them to create the city underwater. The city, of the lost angels. In memory of his daughters whom died in the war above... He created statues & a city, for them. "To andryssa and alessa, with love, your father."

Having golden ebony skin, he was a target from everyone, at all ages of his life. But he proved them wrong. 

"We live in a world where algorithms can solve, and yet, we couldn't solve children dying in the streets, or fathers losing their daughters. We've created a curse... or perhaps... we were the curse all this time... Time... Time itself must be the curse... If only we could stop it... if only... there was a way to freeze time... so that we could have only what we love... and erase our memories... If only... such a world existed."
"In the ancient tombs of the city, there are documents locked away from the old earth. I've read only a few lines... my stomach churning inside out... What I have read... Has damaged my psyche beyond all repair. We are warned of a collapse. It will start with the young... and work it's way up to the youth... When the young lose hope... there will be a rebellion against life itself... far worse than anything we have ever witnessed."
