"All i wanted was to give my daughters a life that they loved living."

The prince sat down in his apartment. Completely isolated from the world outside, yet, with a greedy window, he could see everything. The city at night seemed alive. People riding together. Couples falling in love. Fighting. Breaking up. Standing for one another... "Standing with... one another. I wonder why we do the things we do. We we have children. Why we bother waking up. Dying doesn't seem so bad, when living feels heavier than your coffin. And yet, amidst all this... there's nothing lovelier than watching two humans fall in love. I could watch it over and over again. Even if I built this city with my very hands... I am nothing without them. They... complete me."

# Human nature
The prince knew that human nature would cause the most suffering for all beings. That is why he shared his teachings completely for free, for all to witness, hopefully, for few to truly listen & make a change.

"If you want to understand human nature, look at the state of the world. the day machines will rule the world, will be the best day for all human beings. and remember we are not machine, the world and people living in it are not your responsibility or your fault. The children dying in wars, the horrible situations, the forgotten crimes of tortured souls, none of it is your responsibility. Just as much as the good things that happened to people are not your responsibility. Your responsibilities stretch as far as your arms can reach. and it all begins with you. Free yourself of the guilt of being a human being allow yourself to live again. Not indulging in your own bubble, but giving yourself safety to exist, in an insane world. someday, other beings will rule this world. they will do the things that we could not. "
