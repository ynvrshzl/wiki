"I'll steal your girl if you're not careful"

Zaeyn is pride personified. He had so many beautiful girlfriends in his life. He slept with so many beautiful girls. They loved him. They adored him. He won the game of life... atleast, in his eyes. It wasn't until one day he woke up, that he realized, he will die. "There's no way, that this is all there is." He thought to himself. Panicking, he walked around & realized, nobody was there for him. He felt all the ecstasy, but none of the euphoria of love. And now that he made so many girls lose their virginity, and innocence, he puked in disgust of himself. "I didn't know it'd feel like this."
