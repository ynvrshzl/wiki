Dr. Fredereich is the reason the youth have been turned insane. They were each a study of human emotion. He did this because he wanted to create a world in which suffering & pain would not exist. A world where death & time did not exist. His efforts were failures. Until he realized he had to be ruthless. Few had to suffer, so many could spend eternity in joy. He could bring back his dead family.

"Man is born a wicked creature, all I've done is examine him under the needle. Not one of us is pure, we are all evil."

"From our first breath, we will face immeasurable odds to our existence. "

The doctor created biological programs & embedded it into the genes of children. Of those children, [Joey Doom](joey-doom.md) was his most important life's work. Before [Joey Doom](joey-doom.md) kills the doctor, his last words are: *"You were my special child... I was going to... find a way to stop us from feeling so much... pain.*
