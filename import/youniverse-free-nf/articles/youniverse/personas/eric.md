# Eric
Depression personified


![alt text](depression.jpg)

Often, Eric is often seen covering his left eye. It burns & hurts so much. But what hurts more than the burns on his body, are the things his eyes have seen. He's witnessed the very worst side of people. Things you can't never come back from.

Eric often says something from his childhood: "Death comes for all of us, sooner or later." Vin teasingly scoffs: "Ugggg, Eric, stop being so dramatic man! You gotta' look at the _good_ things, man... Like _girls..._ and girls without clo-" Eric stops him.

Eric was burned as a child, and he suffered tremendously to find a safe place. People saw him as a monster, for something that wasn't his fault. It was from early in his life, that Eric saw the world, and people, as parasites. *"They look at me like a monster, but I see the monster inside all of them."*

"My innocence has been taken away from me.  they destroyed me. i have nothing left." Renee hears me talking to myself, and i look over to find him with his eyebrows raised... staring at me like im some sort of half-human...  I feel so stupid. i put a finger gun on my head & pull. "This world sucks." Vin comes from the back & looks condescendigly at me. *This fucking guy.* Everyone has been so terrible to me. I'm only here for one thing.

Eric began to see how precious & beautiful life was... even after losing someone he loved... the only person he ever loved. Yami became Eric's friend... that was... until Mary Beth looked him in the eyes & reminded him of the darkness. Just with her eyes, he fell back into the pit of desperation... *"You don't understand me, I see the truth, you see lies." "ERIC, You're WRONG. NONE OF US SEE TRUTH OR LIES..."*

Throughout Eric's life, random acts of kindness saved him from becoming a monster. "If life is so cruel, all I needed was one person to treat me like a human being, and that kept me from falling apart."

Eric & Raechel were best friends. Almost... too good of friends. Once, Raechel decided to kiss Eric on the lips. It freaked him out. "Didn't that feel good?!" she exclaimed excitedly to a flustered Eric. He just sat there, basking in a confused swamp of pleasure: "A girl just kissed me... she just... kissed me... am I still a virgin?" Eric & Raechel had sex together, but they wanted to remain friends. This somehow, only bonded them closer together.
