# Jack's breaking moment
And Yami showed Jack Mooder something he had never felt in his life: happiness. 

"Jack, I know... You have been miserable your entire life becuase nobody showed you how to life. I will show you Jack... It... it will hurt.... it... it hurt me when I first learned... but... it's beautiful when you learn to live your life."

Jack's eyes, in a glimpse of a moment, explores the entire youniverse... and for once Jack, is terrified that he will die.

For once, Jack feels what Yami has been telling him all this time... he feels the most powerful, simple human emotion: joy. 

It is to overwhelming, that he tries to throw himself off the building... but Yami catches him... "Hey! Hey.... hey... It's okay... I know... I know how that feels..."
