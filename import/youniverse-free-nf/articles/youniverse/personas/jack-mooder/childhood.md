As a young child, Jack asked the hardest questions. "Why do some people have food, and others don't? Why did I get all of this, and she didn't?" During Jack's childhood, he met a homeless girl, his age, sleeping on the cold streets of the city. He gave her blankets & food, and helped her survive. When his parents discovered this, they immediately broke their close connection. Jack screamed, helpeless & confused: "Why are you doing this?" His parents, having seen how this ends, wanted to spare him the suffering: "You don't have anything to do with the outside world. We — are your family."

Rebellious as he was, he decided to go against his parent's rules and sneaked out, one cold night, to save the girl. But when he went looking for her, she was already gone. This broke Jack's heart so terribly that he looked up to the sky & wondered if there even was a creator, like his parents worshipped. Even as a young child, Jack, showed signs of an old soul. Understanding the evil & cruelty of this world.
