Jack Mooder was deeply traumatized as a child, but not becuase of what was done to him, but becuase of what he saw with his own eyes. It was from an early age that Jack realized how selfish & disgusting human beings could be. His family seemed to be livig in ignorant bliss, while he was pondering the existence of a designer of the cosmos, or a reason for all this suffering.
