"People suck, Rika, but you have me."  Rika has horrifying trauma, which helped her connect with Mary Beth. Riko, on the other hand, is a gentle giant. Behind his big arms, is a very sensitive soul. 


Rika and Riko go together. They are inseperable best friends. They saved each others' lives. They have a deep brotherly-sister bond, not the romantic kind. Riko treats Rika like his little sister, and Rika loves Riko, as her big brother. They aren't related by blood, but by bond. Riko is a gentleman and he protects his little sister with his life. *"If you ever lay a hand on her, i'll batter you up like fresh pancakes on a pan, punk."*
