Directionlessness personified 

"I have travelled these mountains alone for years. There is not a thing in this world that frieghtens me."

![](https://youniverse.free.nf/media/imgs/waylen/viking-winter.jpg)


"Where else is there to go, when you've been going in circles your whole life?"

"When you don't know where you're going and you look behind you and everything looks the same you've been running in circles."

"When you don't know what to do next it is like a maze I have become trapped within these walls. I wish I could predict I wish I could see beyond the walls I wish I could know what to do next I wish I could know where to move next."

Is persona is perhaps one of the most important to me because they represent a time in my life where I felt completely lost and burdened with extreme potential I had dreams that were starting to weigh heavy on my shoulders because I wasn't able to do anything with them. 

Slowly but surely I began playing detective in my life and I learned to use the clues to create better actions for myself the action was the thing that took me out of the stagnation in my life but it was the internal battlefield that mattered most.
