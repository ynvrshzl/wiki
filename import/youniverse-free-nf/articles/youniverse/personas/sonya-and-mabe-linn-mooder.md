# Sonya & Mabe Linn Mooder

Sonya & Mabe Linn are twin sisters, belonging to the royal Mooderian family of Lost Angelis.
The twin sisters lived their lavish lives, the most expensive, dazzling luxury. Until one of the sisters developed a horrifying skin disorder. Slowly, everyone in her life abandoned her, including her own sister. It was then, that she was on the streets... "I am one of them now." Where she used to see the homeless bums of the city... now she was walking alongside them. Nobody could recognize her... until... she met the doctor.

"I have a perfect life... i should be so happy... but there's a hole inside me... it's eating me alive."

"THey look at me like i'm some sort of creature... but i'm just like them... i'm miserable. I wish I was born a normal person, then i'd know what life is really like."

"Being grateful to just be alive... it really is the most beautiful feeling." — Mabe Linn 

Mabe linn mooder: "What good is beauty, if all those animals had to die for my face to be pretty? What good is a table full of food, when a family is starving to death?"
