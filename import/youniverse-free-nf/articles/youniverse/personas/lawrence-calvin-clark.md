# Lawrence, Calvin Clark

![](../imgs/lawrence.jpg)

Calvin, Clark, known by his nickname: "Law" is **intelligence personified.** 

# Childhood
When he was a child, his intelligence was so freightening, that the city pushed him into an asylum for the insane. *"Did you see that boy? He was staring at the sky for hours, and his hair has turned grey from all the stress of thinking. Someone like him cannot be allowed to roam free in the streets... he's a monster."* Calvin found his place in life, after seeing horrifying events, over & over again. He saw things that others did not want to see. *"I have seen into the blackest black nothingness of this world. My very bones are crumbling. Without a reason to exist, I am afraid I won't last in this world much longer. Without justice, there is no reason for existence itself"* 

# Finding occupation
Calvin eventually, got into the detective agency of [Lost Angelis.](#lost-angelis) THere, he met his good friend & work partner: Officer Nihil. They were young and bursting with passion, excited to live, as Calvin would say: "To sweep the streets of evil & extinguish the light of crime. " Officer Nihil would laugh "Relax Cal, there's no need to take the world on your shoulders you know. There's plenty of trouble living inside all of us." 


Clark laters forms ["the black leaf party"](#the-black-leaf-party) which goes on to get rid of all criminal activity within lost angelis, **by any means necessary.**
