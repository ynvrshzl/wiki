# The life of Serena
The mother of the orphans knew that they had no future, unless they were taught the highest principles of being a human being:

1. Empathy
2. Respect
3. Love
4. Gratitude
5. Discipline

She became known as the Mother of the children: Serena. She brought serenity to [the orphanages of Lost Angelis](lost-angelis/orphanages)

# Death
Serena, in another world, is an ascended worker of light. A Queen. Her twin sister, Helena, poisoned & killed Serena.
