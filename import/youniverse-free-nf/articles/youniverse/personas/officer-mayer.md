# Dealing with chronic pain

Towards the end of his life, the body of Officer Nihil was almost entirely synthetic. The few parts of him that were human were the only parts that could not be replaced.

Officer Nihil had to workout constantly to aleviate his extreme chronic pain all over his body. It waas here when he learned the art of meditation, by focusing on the pain, it was fuel for his body to continue. He thought about ending his life often, as it was [a popular choice of death amongst people of the city.](public/youniverse/worlds/lost-angelis.md) What kept him going in his darkest, most crushing of days, was the dream of having a family. A beautiful woman, he would make her a mother, and himself, a father. That dream alone, for his future family, he would fight until the ends of the earth.

Officer Nihil was once a detective in Lost Angelis. One day, he abandoned that line of work, realizing he had to take care of someone more importna than even his own life: his daughter. Nihil got together with his girlfriend, just months before she gave birth to their only daughter, she left him & took all of his possessions. Nihil was left with nothing in the city, and become homeless. Without a home, a name, or even a family, he decided to venture into the depths of the city, in search for meaning & a job to sustain his daughter. His heart was shattered into a million pieces, but determined to do whatever it took to get his daughter the help she needed, Officer Nihil marched forward into the darkest corners of the world. It was there he met The Merchant.
