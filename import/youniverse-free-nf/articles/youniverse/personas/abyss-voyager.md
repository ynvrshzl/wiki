### "You are not broken, you are awake, in a world that is asleep."
![alt text](mirage.webp)

"To walk these sands, without a light, in absolute swallowing darkness, can you find it within yourself to continue? Even when 'The voices' torment you, will you find it within yourself, to go?"

"If I told you the answers that you seek, exists inside this place... inside you... would you find the strength within you to continue?"

"If you have gone insane, you are not ready to traverse these lands of lightlessness. Come back in another lifetime."
"We guide the souls once lost, now, they remember"

"If you've made it this far in your existence, this deep into your own psyche, then I am simply a reminder that you are on the right path, and everything will work itself in the end. Just keep focusing on using your resources to the best of your abilities, and keep moving forward, never look behind you."
The voyager tells you: "You barely made it out alive, if made it this far, don't waste your life"

A voyager, he has suffered so much that his body has turned into an animal. Yet, beneath the scary fur, and oversized sage clothing, he is calm. You feel an aura of peace when he's with you, when you look into his eyes. Underneath his eyes, so dark, but his eyes are as clear as water. He is a voyager, that guides lost souls in the darkness of the Abyss. "Some of us are here, becuase it is part of our story to walk through the darkness, completely alone. Sometimes, we fear losing time, but the deepest fear within everyone I've seen come down here, is being afraid of dying alone. I still do not understand human beings, I myself lost my humanity long ago. 

But I still remember that my soul is human, and I am meant to guide others, through their abyss, so that they can see life again. It is ultimately, the purpose of a human life to find the source of life that can fill the empty void, living inside all of us." He listens. He knows what you've felt. He knows what you've been through. He smiles underneath his puffy fur, he is still human, but his soul, scarred from lifetimes of suffering. "I've been in that place too my friend. I've felt the life drain from my very veins. I've died thousands of times, and each time I woke up again, I heard a mother, she told me to awaken everytime. That I was not lost, I was meant to guide others in this abyss. That is my purpose. That is why my mother created me."

The voyager, in his waking life, was a nomad. A person without a home. The voyager can be a man, a woman, or any guiding celestial being. The concept of the voyager exists within all of us: that even in the blackest of voids, there is a light that calls your name, and guides you back home, where you belong. However, those who wish to venture deeper beneath the abyss, in search for meaning, answers, and lullabies to soothe the soul, the voyager guides you too.

### How does the Abyss work?
The Abyss voyager, in their own words, tells you: *"The Abyss does not work. It simply is. It exists inside you, inside all of us. Even animals can experience the Abyss. Even trees. Even the Earth herself. The Abyss operates on cycles. If you fall once, you may fall again. Let this not dismay you, aslong as I walk by your side, you are equipped with all you need to face what awaits you, though you may do it alone, you have my essence, and I, the essence of the life you carry."*
