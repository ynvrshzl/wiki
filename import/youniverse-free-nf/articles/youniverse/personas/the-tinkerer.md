# Childhood
As a child, he was so physically sick, he spent most of his days in a bed, fatigued, exhausted. His parents had no idea what they could do to help him, neither did the top doctors in the Mooderian city at the time. *"A rebel boy? We're not treating any families from outside the walls."* The mother & father, worried of their child, decided the only way to give him a good life, was to end both of their lives together. That way, atleast the city would take him in the orhphanges.


He was a genius. At a young age, a complete social introvert, but his mind, was incredible at work. While he was asleep, he would dream of his work.

As an elderly man, who could barely stand upright, he would traverse the deep underground, on a broken cane. Though most people forgot his name, he was happy enough to have been one of the authors of such a beautiful, hopeful, bright world. A world where children would be safe from the terrors outside. A world where technologists, where medicine, where creativity, and love, and all that makes us human beings, be. A world where all this would be kept safe, within the walls of the protectors, of his tinkered creations. 

A master of turning scrap metal into living, breathing walks of life. He was assassinated by the Mooders.

The Mooders, were relentless in hunting down anyone & anything that challenged ["the vision."]()
