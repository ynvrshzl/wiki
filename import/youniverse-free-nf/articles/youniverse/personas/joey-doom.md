# Yami saves Joey Doom from killing himself
This is one of the rare times Doom fights against his best friend, Yami. Doom decides to unleash the full force of his terrifying "Yin" self, a darkness so complete, it would swallow hope in anyone. But Yami, had more than hope...

Joey Doom, lifelessly begged to Yami: "Let me die." Yami screamed: "NO." Joey Doom slowly looked up to Yami, and opened his eyes.


Joey Doom's birth name was Zero. He was born in a biological research farm, led by the scientist Dr. Fredereich Mooder. Origninally, Zero was designed to be half human, half biological weapon. The puprose of Zero was to observe pain in human nature, and edit genetics until suffering was no longer a part of human life. Zero was given pain of all kinds, and from the time of birth, was in constant shock. Somehow, the biological weapon within Zero, kept the body alive, past it's breaking point. 

Zero fights against suicidal thoughts everyday. They were born in a time where technology was not "sanitized" — and so — zero had to learn how to live on their own. 


They are a souldier without gender. Posessing attributes of both male & female. Their main mission in life is to survive suicidal thoughts everyday. It is a mental war, thoughts placed in your head are like mines in minefields, becuase one slip and you could end up dead. However, you come back stronger and can resist more types of thoughts when you overcome one. They multiply but so do you. Hence the name, Zero. Which is an imaginary number that cannot be divided, multiplied, or subtracted against itself. It is a war with yourself everyday. But just as much as it is a war, it is also a deep passion, a love for life, that few have ever experienced.
