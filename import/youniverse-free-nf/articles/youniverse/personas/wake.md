# Wake
Awakening personified

Wake is the evolved form of Yami. whenever he is faced with pain and suffering in his life, instead of being crushed by the weight, wake is instead fueled with extreme passion: *"you think this pain is enough to kill me? BRING IT ON."*

"Wake is the side of me when I realize I came so close to dying, now I am fighing back with all i've got."
