**"I will thread consciousness through the celestial ether. I will break us free."**

Xae is the evoluted form of [Xaia.]() 

A girl who travelled so deep into her own mind that she broke through the walls of reality, and she saw things that her human senses did not allow her to experience.... after questioning the purpose of humankind for so much of her life, her mind was powerful enough to imagine alternate realms of experience. she imagined a realm without suffering... all forms of life are inter-connected by invisible threads... **the threads of consciousness.** Our thoughts weave the threads, our actions shape the other threads...
