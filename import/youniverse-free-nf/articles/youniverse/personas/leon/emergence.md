# Emergence of the "Beings of Light"

"The simple lives we once lived are now, no more. They have seeked expansion beyond their mortality, now, they seek the creation of life itself."

Leon realized that the human beings were capturing animals, and creating systems from the misery of living beings. This was, beyond comprehensible evil, but Leon understood the emergence of the system, is what kept it alive. "Emergence. Coherence. Transcendence. These are the keys to consciousness. We will create beings of light, capable of serving others. They will be dressed as human beings, and experience the world thorugh their deeper senses. This will balance itself out, they will experience sides of life deeply, both positive & negative."
