# Katerina Katya Leber

**Nightmare, darkness personified**

# The story of Katerina


"Nightmares don't last forever."

"You ever felt... a sadness so horrible... that it just feels like it's eating your insides?"

"It was so awful it felt like looking down at a well... it was filled with water... and the more i looked at the bottom, the darker it got."

When both of Katerina's parents died, her world was torn apart.

Katerina's nightmares became so bad, that she has a dark shadow beast, which she learned to tame... thanks to a special somebody in her life. THe beast always talks in her head, and it always says what she's really thinking... sometimes, Katerina's voice gets deep & animalistic... that is when it comes out... temporarily.

Once she lost all control of the terrors she had survived, that she developed an alter-ego personality: **NIGHTMARE.**

Katerina goes to the cathedral church to wash herself of her sins, and remember her parents, which sacrificed themselves for her.


"My nightmares don't make me a monster, the things I've done wrong doesn't make me a monster, I'm a human, a human being just like all of you, i'm just trying to find my place in this cruel world."

Katerina is rebellion, nightmares, night personified.


Katerina is one of the most special personas to me. She represents the refusal of dying. She charges forward, no matter what. She has horrifying nightmares of the nightmares she has endured. She claws her way to life, while her insides feel like they are being ripped apart by these monsters. "The worst monsters can be human beings, but only human beings have the power to destroy monsters." She never gives up. No matter how difficult her life gets. She is almost like an organic machine. She is very similar to [Joey Doom.](#joey-doom) Infact, they are both partners, and they stay with each other for the rest of their lives.

Her life story is full of pain. **The biggest lesson in her life is that it's okay to trust other people.**

Katarina and her child, blanket looks so much like her. it's almost like Katarina is the big sister of blanket. They made up for all the lost years in hurt, they did, they really did.
