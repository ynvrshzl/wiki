# Sister Ann-marie
The nun

"This world is cruel, rest with your father."

![](etc/youniverse-free-nf/media/img/nun/img.webp)

"Mother?" a child says to the nun. She sweetly responds, closing her eyes: "Yes dear?" The child finally asks: "Why did God make me?" The nun opens her eyes, the child looks into her crystal clear, grey eyes. Tears puor down her face, as she looks around. She wispers, her voice trembling: "Even though I was born blind, I believe I have a gift with my other senses. God... my dear child, will never abandon you. He has shared a piece of him inside you & me."

The nun told Ares: "Our father formed you the way you were meant to be. You were not born in the wrong body, but perhaps, in the wrong world." Ares opened here eyes wide. With anger & humility, Ares didn't want to exist in this world anymore, it was far too cruel. But she had a dream... to have a happy family one day... to be a mother, and give her child the life she never had. "You're wrong. I was born in the perfect world, and the wrong body."
