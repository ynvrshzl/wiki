music-personified.md

"I am half man, half rythym. I am the chord, and the ear. I have incarnated as a G chord."

An artist inspired by Jhin (from League of Legends) who puts his pain into art, suffering and his art ends up being the most beautiful tragic poetry.

One of the hardest things for a creative human being, is getting through the wall of amnesia and creating a bridge between what you feel in your life and what's your work reflects. 

Experience is the input. Expression is the output. I am a vessel for this cycle.

Bend words, sounds & worlds.
