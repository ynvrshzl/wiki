# Jaeydn
*"If you could see who you'll become, little man... oh if you could have seen."*

![](etc/youniverse-free-nf/media/img/jaeydn/future.webp)

"I see the light in you, stay strong."

Jaeydn visited his own inner world of anger and met alot of figures, both from his past, and his future. The versions of him he could have become, and the versions of himself he abandoned. The child he once was, still broken & furious at the world.

"Would you believe me if I were to tell you there are world inside you. There are worlds inside everyone you meet. Some are deeper, some are lighter. Some are dying, others are being born again. What the hell is this life? It's hell my friend, this life is hell, and you were made to endure it."
