
This is a letter to you, my best friend. I wish you knew how lucky you were. We made mistakes, but that was the way we were made, to live... to learn.

"Life truly is a tragedy... but a beautiful one. When I was younger, I remember seeing so clearly... everything was light... everything was... peaceful... and so easy... and so... warm. I have been in the dark for what seems like all my life... but something... somewhere inside me keeps me alive."
