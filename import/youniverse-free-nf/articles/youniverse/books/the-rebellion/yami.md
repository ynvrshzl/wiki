Yami has a sack filled with food. He gives them out to the kids & poor of the city. He then takes a break and grabs his favorite food from the sack. The sky a painterly orange sunset — just like his hair. He happily smiles & looks forward, enjoying the cool breeze as it wafts against his face.


Slowly but surely, our family grew. We travelled all across the city, in search for anyone that could help us on our mission. Lots of the silent kids in school finally found a place where they belonged. We made a family, here, in The Rebellion — *The New, Rebellion.*

## Chapter 8
And whenever Yami was faced with the terrors of his memories, he would close his eyes and imagine a place so peaceful, so warm, with a loving family, and a loving partner. *"I gave birth to this place... though I wanted to die, I also wanted to keep living... in my world... Youthanasia..."*
