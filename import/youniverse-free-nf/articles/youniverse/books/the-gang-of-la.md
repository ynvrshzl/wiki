# Author's note
This is one of the most painful stories i've ever written. The characters feel alive, and their pain can be felt in even the most blissfully ignorant of us. This is not entirely a work of fiction, but rather, a written collection of my imagination, a bridge between me and characters existing in other planes of existence.

This story follows a non-linear plot. Instead of chapters, we encourage you to read this in the order that makes most sense to you.
