# The Rebellion

![]( https://youniverse.free.nf/media/img/the-rebellion/cover.jpg)

"We are not born evil by design. Every living creature has the right & dignity of living. We pledge our lives to serving the rebellion. The rebellion against destruction. The rebellion against evil. We will create, never destroy. It is our duty to protect human beings against the greedy. It is our duty, be it costing our very lives. To this meaning, we will live for, all our days, death do us part."
