**This is the purpose of pain, in my eyes.**

Some levels of pain are unliveable, that's suffering. What I'm talking about are those moments where you don't know if it's even worth staying alive to endure it. Usually, that's where the most experience lives.

I've learned that pain not only humbles us, but it's the only way we can experience the good parts that make life worth living. It's more than just being grateful or appreciating, it's literally being able to experience good things in life.

Throughout the Youniverse, there are countless people who were spoiled with everything they could ask for, but who would ask for pain? Nobody! They never experienced the hardships that others their age had to, and so, they lived in a walled garden, where there was no pain, and eventually, nothing they had was enough. They felt empty, emotionless, numb. 

That's a scary place to be.
