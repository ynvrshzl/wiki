Context:
As Kateryna was walking back home at night, she did no longer fear the night. She had been through so much, she had seen so much, she was too exhausted to care.

Scene:
Out of the shadows, a man approaches her from behind.
Katerina immediately senses him. She continues walking.
Katerina: closes her eyes and captures the sound of a metallic shrill, brushing against cloth. Immediately she realizes; "It's a knife."
Katerina: without warning, flips over and almost in slow-motion, she bends backward.
Katerina: with all her force, kicks the man away form her, just like Doom taught her.
The man: falls on the ground, and drops the knife on the ground 
Katerina's eyes grow blood red with fury. She feels; They robbed her of life's innocence once, they will not get her again. 
The man: fears her, more than she fears him. Before he passes out, he looks at her in the eyes and feels pure terror.

Analysis:
In this scene, Katerina exudes this dark fierce energy, not exactly evil, but the same intense energy. Captured in her quote > "Darkness, I no longer fear you, for I have become something more powerful than you. You can no longer take my mind, my body or my soul. I have seen beyond you, there is life, behind the shadow."
