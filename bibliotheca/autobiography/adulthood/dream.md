# My dream
### What do you want?

This is a fundamental question, and a crucial moment, in my journey of healing. In such a simple question, immense depth & layers of complexity emerge, as I stumble on my words, trying to find what it is that I love most about life.

My dream is to live a live a life so beautiful, i won't want to die. I want my death to mean something — more importantly — I want my life to mean something. And i've learned, that you cannot have meaning without suffering, it's almost as if, human life was a word, the meaning comes from suffering.

This is my dream biography, it's somethign that  motivates me to keep going, especially during the nightmarishly-long episodes of depression.
