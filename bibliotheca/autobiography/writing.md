# The power of writing
those words are a direct channel from my soul... in lossless fidelity... it took years and millions of words and thousands of hours and aeons of walking through the deserts of my soul just to express those few lines... but it's all worth it... 

And I know that's how I'm gonna feel when I finally see myself heal completely. And I can see my child self happy again, just to live. And I will make it happen, and I will survive to that day. That's how I've programmed myself. That's how the designer designed me.
