# A moment of clarity
I realized what makes me so happy about being a trans girl, I chose to be happy in this life I chose to love myself and embrace myself and accept myself and people I truly am even if it's difficult and even when I wanted to give up. because I only have one life and I'm going to make the most of it and live a beautiful life ❤️

Life is too short & too painful to not live for the highest meaning you can follow.

I always felt like my body was ruined by puberty until they found the opportunity to turn myself into a pretty girl, and I said I'll turn myself into the pretty girl I always was ❤️
