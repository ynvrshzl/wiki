The idea of the youniverse came from all of the hidden parts inside each person that only we can see in ourselvs. But beyond that, it also meant that we are completely alone in our perception of life.

I feel like I have such a deep, rich inner world... worlds of worlds, and that only I can take care of myself and understand myself the way I need to. That doesn't mean I don't need human contact, infact, it means I need to socialize becuase my inner worlds are strengthened by the worlds outside!

Perception, emotion, thoughts, feelings. While these are things that are completely private to us, we can build bridges to the outside world, so that others can feel what we feel. Through non-destructive matter like art, music, games, stories, poetry, images
