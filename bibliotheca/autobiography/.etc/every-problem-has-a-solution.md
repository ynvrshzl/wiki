# "There is a solution to every problem."
That quote is from one of my favorite personas, [Leo Vincinatti]()

The quote may seem cheesy & idealistic, but if you have lived long enough, you begin to see truth in this world. Truth that only time  can show you.
Everybody has problems that are unique to them. But inheritly, we are all human, that means we all have a solution to every single problem, awaiting to be discovered. THe hardest part about depression, isn't problem-solving, it's having a reason to care. A reason to be terrified. A reason to feel. **Having a reason to exist, is the most important thing.**
