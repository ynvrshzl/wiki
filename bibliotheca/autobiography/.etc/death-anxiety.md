# Death anxiety
My death anxiety is more about fearing what I would lose, rather than dying itself. I accept the mystery of life, **but I so do not accept living a life any lesser than what I dreamt of for myself.**

# Desperation to create
I feel the desperation to create. I have thousands, probably millions of scenes existing within me right now, but it's like I have tongue paralysis... I can't seem to speak even a single coherent word without everything falling apart. But even this, I feel is part of my life's story.

One of the most difficult things i've struggled with is learning to express my thoughts, as purely as I imagine them & feel them. No matter what i've tried, there is always some essence lost, and perhaps, this is meant to be. To capture exactly what I see through my eyes, could very possibly create trauma for someone that hasn't lived my life.

One of the greatest challenges in my life has been creating things of the highest quality. As a neurodivergent mommy, I want the very best that I can give, but it's very rare to capture what I feel, into an external body of work. Awareness is the key to solving any problem, but what comes after awareness? Action. The essence seems to be lost. Perhaps too technical and we get deeply immersed in the details.
