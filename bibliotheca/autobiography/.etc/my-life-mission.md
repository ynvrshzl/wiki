# My life mission
My life mission is to understand complete human nature, both the darkness: evil, suffering, pain, aswell as the light: happiness, abilities, transformation, belonging. 

I am a sensitive soul, and I've gone full circle, many, *many* times. From agonizing, sharp pain, to mind-numbingly beautiful experiences. Life, is something to be experienced in full color & contrast. The darker my black, the brighter my white becomes. However, I also realize life is not a game of who can suffer the most, or who can feel the deepest... life is... I don't know actually. But i'm here to explore this journey with you. That is entirely, the only reason i'm writing this.

Being a human being is confusing. Psychology is the most important part of our lives, and my mission is simply to share what I'm learning. This knowledge could save your life, becuase it saved mine, but remember your only responsibility is to heal yourself first, not the world.
