# Validation
I want others to see my pain. I want others to acknowledge that I've survived something... that I'm worth something. This is how I feel when I desire social validation. It's not bad nor good, to want others to see you, but remember, you can always start by seeing yourself, first.

it's hard to be around people becuase i enter the 'social' mode & i lose who i really am, in return for a social mask that protects me. i get nervous & sweaty when someone is talking to me, regardless of family. i just dislike people. but i don't hate human beings. i am a human being too, and i realize i belong somwhere that has yet to exist (my youniverse community) someday, i'll find my people. but i've realized us autistic/adhd/out-the-box folks don't belong forcing ourselves within the box that others live in. we are meant to shape new things. solve problems in the world. we are the wierd human beings that mother nature designed with a different purpose, perhaps to carry our evolution to heal the mother...
