# What makes human life worth living?

I infact, am writing this feeling the exact same way. I know, it's hard to believe. But according to my studies, there are only a few things that make human life worth living, everything else, is just filler. This? This is the good stuff.

1. Expression
2. Emotion
3. Experience
4. Human relationships
