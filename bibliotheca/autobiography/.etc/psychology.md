## Psychology 
Psychology is **the most important aspect of human life.** A salad, or a walk outside won't fix trauma.  Our experiences drive beliefs, world-views, and thoughts. These are the psychological cogs that drive the engine of consciousness. Therefore, experiences have the most impact on our psychology.


On a more serious note... as if i wasn't serious enough! I want you to know that what you're feeling is part of the human experience. Though there are emotions & desires that I cannot mention due to censorship... you know what I mean.

The hardest areas in human psychology are: **contradiction, communication, clarity, amnesia & blind spots.** These are the primary areas that a therapist will focus on. But the issue lies in the nature of the problem: the only person who could ever understand you entirely is you, but you don't have the knowledge or skills to understand yourself entirely. And that's actually okay. **You don't need to understand everything, you just need to know the most important things.**
