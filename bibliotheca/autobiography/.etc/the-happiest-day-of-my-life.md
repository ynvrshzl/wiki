# A day in my life: the happiest girl.
This is a short story, imagining myself living in an alternate life, perhaps, it could become real, someday, though currently, i'm just surviving everyday.

We woke up together. The sun has been up for a couple of hours, toasting me through the windows as I softly lay in bed. My face is so soft & skin so gentle, pressing against the marshmallow pillow. 

We go to Chick-fil-A for some quick afternoon food, recharging & resting from all the shopping we've done that day so far
