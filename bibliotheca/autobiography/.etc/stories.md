Stories are the written form of human imagination. And if you get really good at imagining, then your stories will become more vivid, more alive, immersive, and best of all, they will help psychologically heal. 

But ofcourse, in today's world written stories are difficult to access, ironically, we have almost every story written, available digitally, yet attention span, amongst other issues, have made it almost impossible to deeply immerse yourself & get lost in these written works.

Luckily, there is always a positive: even if the written story format is becoming replaced with other forms of storytelling, we can use it as a base for all other things to sprout.
