I can't solve the world's problems, or even the problems in existence itself. 

But what I needed during my most depressed moments was not to save this world, but instead, to live for something that made my life meaningful. 

Once I had the energy to take care of my body, my health slowly recovered. My sleep shifted from being completely nocturnal, to waking up with the sun. It was rough, very very difficult. But it was worth it becuase I was able to live my life meaningfully, by doing meaningful things. And that was what I was missing. There is so much pain, cruelty & evil in this life, but once I did something that justified atleast my own existence, I started realizing what I was created for, whoever, whatever created me. This is where I belong. 

I believe most people like me don't have clinical mental illness, but rather, this world makes it nearly impossible to find meaning & then build a life worth living. I dedicate my life's work to making this a little easier atleast. Help someone like my past self, to find what they exist for. Becuase in the coming years, directionlessness will be one of the leading causes of death.
