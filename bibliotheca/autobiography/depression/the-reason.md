# The only reason I'm still alive today
The only reason I'm still alive today is becuase of all the little things that kept me alive, in those excruciating, unbearable moments. The spaces where everything in my life fell apart, my very soul felt obliterated & crushed by the weight of evil, suffering, cruelty & silence in this world. Little things kept me alive. Like verses from the bible: "The Lord is close to those who are crushed in spirit." I imagined an awesome gothic figure handing me little verses like these, and it was up to me to hold onto them, like the thread of my life. It was, many times, where I found myself, only being able to imagine a future worth living for. I felt fucking miserable. I wanted to die. I didn't feel anything inside anymore. Everything felt so blurry. But then, I remembered: "in this moments, though suicide may seem like the only truth, I am experiencing amnesia. I'm forgetting all of the suicide survivors who risked their lives to bring me the knowledge that life is truly worth living. They were all glad their attempts didn't work. That must be then, what we human beings were created for... whaterver or whoever created us... I don't give a fuck anymore. I will just live. I will find a reason to care. I will find something to love. Someone... something... that makes me want to stay alive, so that I don't lose them. Live a life so beautiful, you're scared to lose it."
