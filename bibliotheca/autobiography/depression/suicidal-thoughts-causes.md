# Causes for suicidal thoughts
These are some of the major causes of my triggers for suicidal thoughts. Not an exhaustive list, but these are the ones i'm most aware of. **It's the ones I often forget, that are the most dangerous.**

1. Feeling stuck. Repeating the same day. (Directionlessness)
2. Feeling like nothing changed, no matter how hard I tried. (Futility)
3. Feeling like I've wasted my life. (Fear of missing out)
4. Human nature. Realizing how dark & depraved we are at our animalistic biological level. Especially sexuality in *mostly* all human beings. (Over-awreness)
5. Feeling like even if I have amazing ideas, I can't get anything done becuase of my health. That makes me really upset & sad, to the point where I just drop everything & feel nothing.
