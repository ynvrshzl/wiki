# Anhedonia: lack of emotion, excitement, aliveness
This saved my life: realizing that even if in the moment I feel nothing, I can look back on this moment once it becomes a memory (a couple of days, weeks or months) and I can feel a deeply powerful nostalgia, that sometimes, is to overwhelmingly beautiful, I want to cry.

regardless of anhedonia, i can still feel. i am not a ghost. music, dancing, stories & art help me express my emotions, but i have thoughts that need logical expression. i do that through code & obsidian. obsidian is my private space for my thoughts.

I can write & share as much as i want in this website... however, throughout the years, i've learned that talking out loud helps me process my thoughts & feelings way more than writing.
