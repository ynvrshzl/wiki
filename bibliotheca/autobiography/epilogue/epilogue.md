# Epilogue
This is my Autobiography, a collection of my written memories, remembered as best as I could remember then, and capture these moments in my mind's theatre.

These writings will someday, live far beyond my existence. I am choosing to spend my time creating things, rather than sinking deep into hopelessness. It is, the most difficult thing I have ever done in my life, to choose to stay alive, in a world as sick as this. However, What if i told you that within this insanity, i've found a reason to exist... within the years inside these walls of my room... inside the walls of my mind... isolated from the life outside... I actually came full circle & discovered an reversed flower... blooming onto itself... a singularity... a flower, that makes all of this suffering, not only possible, but necessary. 

# Read the full story before judging
Just like a story only makes sense at its full length, if you keep repeating the same things everyday and someday you change your lifestyle, when you look back you will get the fresh perspective and emotions that you should have felt when instead in the moment you are desensitized to the glory of your existence
