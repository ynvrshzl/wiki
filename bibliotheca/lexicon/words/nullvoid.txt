this word is a paradox, we are expressing the same idea twice in the same word, avoid and no, the idea of nothingness. the reason this word exists is because it describes how, without god, or a source of life, there is literally no purpose to living, at least existentially. it's difficult to understand this word because we don't want to get carried away with meaning... but essentially without god without love, without morals, without a virtue, there is no reason for anything.
