this project is inspired by "The Dictionary of Obscure Sorrows" by John Keonig
[Source](https://www.thedictionaryofobscuresorrows.com/words)


essentially we are capturing our observations of life and creating words that can symbolically store our experiences. 

while there are many other fields and mediums that we human beings use to express our experiences, language is fundamentally the most powerful expression tool that we human beings have ever created, because language is entirely symbolic, we give it meaning, and that's why it helps us navigate the world when we don't understand something: we assign a word with meaning. and meaning is the only aid in chaos.
