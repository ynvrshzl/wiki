this theory is essentially my way of understanding the limits we have as human beings and yet how it's possible that human imagination can be as complex as the universe... 

and maybe this is a way of the Gods humbling us... or maybe God, but whatever is crazy theory, one thing is for sure; we cannot capture everything as human beings. and for someone with an extremely vivid inner world, at first this just felt like another layer of suffering to this already limited human life, but then I realized the fact that we can only Express a few things within the human lifetime... means that the little things we do Express become much more significant. 

oh in essence, the expression limits we have as human beings, is actually what makes our expression significant at all!

if we could capture everything, every detail every memory, and leave no trace 🫆 or artifact, I think we would essentially recreate the universe itself and first of all that would be impossible but even if that would be possible we would be drowning and noise and it would no longer be meaningful.

and this is essentially what we explore when we write about the purpose of art, is not to understand but rather to express and interpret.
