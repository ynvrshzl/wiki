Existential grief for all that is beautiful and good in life. 

This is only experienced by highly sensitive human beings that have survived unimaginable depths of torment. Somehow, having survived all of this, you can still appreciate simply being alive, despite all your body has endured. How is this possible? Becuase now you fall to your knees, you are humble,  not by choice, but by your very soul recognizing the immense weight of a single life.

And so, it hurts deeply, not only when you see cruelty and injustices without end, but when you see beautiful things. It hurts... it's almost unbearable to look another human being in the eyes. But you are one of them too, and yet, the soul is remembering it's celestial body... the consciousness before the human form... that's why you have a respect of life, that hurts the human heart, but harmonizes with the Designer.
