Expression and Retrieval

Think of it this way: when you are creating something, you don't really know what it's like when you'll come back to this to read, listen or witness it as if for the first time.

It's also related to the 'first-impression effect' 

Essentially, we are operating in 2 different domains:
- When we express, we are actively sending things. From our inner imagination, to an external medium
- When we experience/retrieve, we essentially do the opposite; we access the signal.
