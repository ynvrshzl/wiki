Definition
The realization that things that were once extremely important, were lost, forgotten, or became meaningless for some reason. And when you return to them, it's this feeling "how did I forget this?! How could I have forgotten this?! This was once so important to me... how did you forget this?!"

It's one of the worst existential feelings, but also beautiful, you feel a mix of grief-and-thankfulness.

Grief that you 'wasted' so much time away form where you belong... and thankfulness that you even returned.
