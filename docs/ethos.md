# Ethos
What is the mission/purpose of this wiki system? Is it just a bunch of articles and fancy writings on complex ideas? Or is it more? The inner workings of an author who is trying to make sense of a world, not outside, but inside the self.

This perspective, is probably, **the most important thing i've ever discovered in my life.** When I looked at the travesties of the world we live in, I wanted to die. But when I saw within myself, I could look at those same travesties, and not be crushed by it's weight.
