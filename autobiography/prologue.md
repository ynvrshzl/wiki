## Section 1 - Technology as medicine for the human soul.
It is inevitable. We cannot remove technology from our lives. Can we synthesize it as medicine? I am using the power of technology to learn more about us human beings and how to make the most of the human mind to solve problems in real life. I am learning, exploring, and tinkering with all different kinds of fields and subjects because I believe the connections and links between them is how we will discover the cure to the human condition.

Our work must fundamentally be based on ethics and virtues. While balancing the absurdity of being human, with play, comedy and humor.

To avoid straying into my own delusion or narrow-mindedness, our conversations must contain adequate friction. instead of synthetic arguments, we should study historical figures in human history, the most brilliant minds that have walked to this earth, and pull insight from their domains of expertise, in order to challenge our perspectives and tests are hypotheses, so that we filter what is real, from what is hypothetical.
