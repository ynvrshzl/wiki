"Varos Principle"
Ultimately the reason why "importance" itself is the key driver for living systems, especially human beings, is becuase; while our abilities may have no end, our time does. Living systems are not limited by ability; they are limited by physical conditions, and no living system can escape time.

This is why we cannot endlessly explore, or calculate, or analyze. At some point, we have to prioritize continuity over completion; becuase we are limited by time.

In everything we do, the gauge, must eventually converge on importance. Not everything in human life can or should be measured in quality of importance... 
βάρος (város) – "weight" (as in "weight of importance")
