"We are context-dependent creatures." An addition to "Kubernetes model". When we are presented with a set of choices, we don't merely perform binary operations in search-space; we require an external reason. It is the difference between "Here are 20 options" vs. "What are you looking for?" This requires context and meaning; which are symbtiotic and inter-dependent. Meaning and context come from relationships, connection, communication. These are the foundations of Cybernetics, and by extension, the continuation with "Kubernetics."
