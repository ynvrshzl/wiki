notes on the power of storytelling 
- literature uses human language as the primary medium. 
- human language is the most powerful invention we have to describe experience, instructions, and essentially recollect memories and time.
- literature provides the ability to author experience 
- there is a cohesive Continuum of storytelling experience types: fiction, non-fiction and semi-fiction, non-linear. these can be conceptualized as the states of matter equivalent in literature.
- the author, in literature, has the ability to switch perspectives. almost like a camera. 
    - this introduces the idea of literature / author objects, which are devices and storytelling. the metaphor, is another device/object/function.
- the camera is a significant object in literature, as it is essentially the ocular optic system, essentially it is the lens which gives attention. 
- in essence, a story can be described as a flowing passage of attention. this is powerful because it changes the idea of stories, as infinite potential in space, and redefines it as a flowing axes in time, through which attention is guiding the reader to reassemble the story, almost like a cognitive executable bio program.
