"Deprivation studies"
- Studying how we human beings need co-nervous-system regulation
- Not "forcing yourself to be social" but "we need other living beings to function and regulate."
- Psychological sanity and Empathy are core aspects of the necessity of co-regulation.
- Simply being around other living beings, does something existentially important for us; it reminds us the world inside our head is not real; the world inside our head is an engine, which requires external stimuli, internal processing, and external output.
