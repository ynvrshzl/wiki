# On painful parts of human life
I believe **human experience** is synonymous with **sample data**, in Science.

This gives significance to otherwise **extermely painful series of events in human life, whcih often beg the question "why?"**

Perhaps by understanding it as sample data...

Some sample data is just really expensive to collect, but data is information about the world, and for finite creatures, the ability to know parts of the infinite... and what matters within the matter...nothing is more important than that.
