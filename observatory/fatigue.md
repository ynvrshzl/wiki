# Battling Fatigue
On battling fatigue, and all it's forms.

Topics include: [Chronic Fatigue (CF)](), [ME/CFS (Clinically Recognized: Myalgic Encephalomyelitis/Chronic Fatigue Syndrome)]()

## "Should I work myself through fatigue?"
I believe this is one of the most important questions a person struggling with CF could ask. However, it is __crucial__ to distinguish between:
- "Working myself to get through something"
- "Working becuase I have something worth my participation."

In order for this to work, I have observed in my own local life; it is important to dinstinguish between [Clinical Fatigue]() and [Functional Fatigue]()
