In relation to time management; sleep may not be the terminal definition of a functional, well-being human life.

Radical discovery; regardless of sleep quality & quantity, the most important part of the day in the human life is time. 

From personal local experience, I have studied the problem of sleep, time management, stress and burnout, for most of my adulthood.

Time is outside of our control; but our perception of time is influencable; and our circadian rhymthm (energy levels throughout the day) are evidence of our agency to change the quality and functioning of our finite human lives.
