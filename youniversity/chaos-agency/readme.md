# Chaos Agency

Chaos Agency is: __The study of navigating chaos and complexity, in everyday human life.__

## Practical applications of this field

- Help us human beings navigate complex systems. We can observe the patterns and learn the language of chaos and order.

## Structural dimension of information
If we think of information as geometric, then it is useful to imagine lists as being two-dimensional hierarchial branches. Why is this relevant? If we can condense information, into collapsible categories, then we are effectively witnessing organization.

## Components of Chaos Agency - "Titles"
In Chaos Agency, a title not only collapses depth of information, but also does something
miraculous; it creates associative (something). For example, when a group of
tasks are in a file with a title, the tasks may be incredibly difficult to
read, but the title hides away the content, and creates an associative link.
This is the basis of managing chaos. Reducing input, but not reducing agency.
