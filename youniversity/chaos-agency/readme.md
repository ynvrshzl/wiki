# Chaos Agency - The study of maintaining signal amidst noise, in everyday human life.

## Structural dimension of information
If we think of information as geometric, then it is useful to imagine lists as being two-dimensional hierarchial branches

## Components of Chaos Agency - "Titles"
In Chaos Agency, a title not only collapses depth of information, but also does something
miraculous; it creates associative (something). For example, when a group of
tasks are in a file with a title, the tasks may be incredibly difficult to
read, but the title hides away the content, and creates an associative link.
This is the basis of managing chaos. Reducing input, but not reducing agency.
