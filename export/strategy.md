__"How do I deal with 100s of notes?"__

Here are the most viable, tested methods for algorithmic strategies for editing notes, and managing overwhelming noise information in this wiki system.

1. __(LEA) Linear Exploratory Alphabet strategy:__ Linear, top-down approach. Travelling from alphabetical order in folders. 
2. __(TFQ) Topological + Filtering + Query strategy:__ Travelling via topic. With the use of [[tools]] and **Obsidian Search** to filter keywords, folders and topics, to avoid becoming lost in noise.
