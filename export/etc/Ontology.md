# Ontology of Authoring: What is a story? 

## Prototype 
defining the Prototype of the story, independent of a substrate. To understand what substrates implement bias and abstractions to better represent our stories. 

In principle, the essence of a story may be described as imagination encoded through devices, movement, fluidity, flow. Ever-flowing, Metaphorical, Dramatized, Emphasized. Threads fabric.

But the structure of a story is multifactorial; interconnected like that of a microcosm, or life
