the youniverse stories ultimately try to capture something impossible; phenomenology; what The Human experience is really like. explored from different perspectives, different references, and doing our best to create worlds as diverse as life, they're all intimately answer the question that began the youniverse, "why?" we often say, the universe began with a why, the letter y, but also the question why. for some reason, when we ask why, it seems to be the most powerful object of introspection, problem solving, meaning. all of the other ontological objects of inquiry, who what where when how, specify the existence of things, descriptions, properties, qualities. but the question why, answers a different kind of existence, 

capturing the full resolution of human experience, is most likely not possible, but it is also perhaps, paradoxically, insufficient for the study of human life. in some sense, our universe stories, are the mythic lens of scientific inquiry.

throughout my life, there are many significant experiences that made me realize, we are not just biological phenomenon; there is something which we are in the process of understanding and studying, in essence, that which makes life worth living.
