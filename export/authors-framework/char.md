# Principles of Characters in Literature

A phenomenon; what defines a character, from a background actor, or a randomized set of features? This is fascinating becuase it implies; stories are mechanical + human effort processes.

"A character is not merely a construct of formula, a character emerges from phenomenon, lived experience, and the processing engine that is the human anatomy."

In other words; characters seem to not be force-able; one cannot merely "design" a character. A character is born from an extrinsic or intrinsic source
