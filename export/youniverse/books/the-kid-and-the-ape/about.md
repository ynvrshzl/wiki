The main character of the story is a little child, whose name is simply "kid." This is really important because it illustrates the raw power of child imagination, and the necessity of adult embodiment, amongst other themes. 


The story is secretly a philosophical anthropological study of human beings, wrapped as humor. 

The story begins similar to Alice in wonderland; _"how did that kid get here?"_ 

and it is anthropomorphized animals, essentially studying the human condition, in reverse. It is the inverse human condition. As emphasized by supporting characters; Cornelius and Sir Charles Frederick. Cornelius is the quiet isolated genius, and Sir Charles Frederick is the cynical tired grandpa.
