## What is a chaos system? 
in chaos field mechanics, we define chaos systems as **"any set of processes and interconnected relationships in which the local influence of any unit, cannot be calculated, due to the interconnected relational nature of nonlinear systems."**

given this rigorous definition, we can map these ideas to real life;
- social relationships are inherently non-linear, a friend of a friend could connect to another friend. in fact there is a theory called (six distance) studies this exact idea. 
- productivity and work, another core domain of the human life, also resembles a non-linear system. our ability to map goals, metrics, responsibility, ability, and more natural aspects, all work together, in parallel, integrating into a larger system which we experience as our ability to work and focus and be productive.
