# Hyper 
## Definition
hyper fixation is not merely a diagnosis for individuals with adhd, it is also applicable to any analysis paralysis or decision making fatigue, which is experienceable by most human beings.

## Problem
how does one avoid getting lost in infinite abstraction detail, or hyper focused on minutiae? this is actually a significant issue for individuals struggling with ADHD, perfectionism, etc. 

## Discovery
from what I discovered, the most compressed workable model is: continuous motion and rate of change, is the only definitive property of experience. 

## Biological functions
this means, things that remain still, motionless, are essentially perceived as static background, by our human senses. things that are moving, are prioritized. 

## Model
Given this biological function... we can understand both of the causes of, and operational function.
- __Cause:__ we become stuck in tasks, relationships, decisions, when we can no longer process movement, rate of change, or meaning outside a model. [^1]
- __Function:__ we pay attention, we tend to notice things that are moving, integrated into a larger life cycle, or...
