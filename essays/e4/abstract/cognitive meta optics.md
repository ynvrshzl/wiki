# Beyond UI/UX: The cognitive ergonomics 

Cognitive-based engineering remains a fundamental tool of orbit for this field.

when we design user interfaces and user experiences, we are actually working with two layers. 
- the graphical systems (cybernetics)
- what the graphics mean (semiotics)


this means that the fundamental idea for a ui/ux engineer is really about asking the right questions rather than merely picking the "aesthetic" layout. the whole purpose of this field is to understand how aesthetics emerge from purpose. because there is a language for aesthetics...

we asked the right questions to guide...

this is the framework of question modeling
- what information am I trying to make accessible here? what information is actually useful?
