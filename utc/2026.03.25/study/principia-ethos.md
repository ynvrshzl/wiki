# Principia Ethos 
This is a composition of personal life beliefs, not as a principle on how to be a perfect human being, but rather serving as a reference to the belief systems that keep me on the path of virtue ethics and good, with a touch of farting wombat jokes for good measure. 

 
## Principle 1

Stress is not the enemy. A system that is sophisticated and useful must have the ability to withstand chaos. consequently, experiencing the stress in your life, is a sign that you are integrated. and that is always the baseline of a ethical human life. without stress, for prolonged periods of time, our problem solving abilities, erode and fatigue. a stress-free life is also usually a sign of isolation, which is absolutely one of the core states to absolutely avoid in our principia ethos document.

## Principle 2
Never have pride but strong beliefs. our beliefs should be open to change, but stable to belief. nobody should be have the ability to enter your life and change you. but we should be open to the world around us, if our paths are not aligned with the highest good.
