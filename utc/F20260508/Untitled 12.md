---
title: "Crafting and mechanics of the human schedule"
---

throughout my life I have often heard, the importance of order in one's life. however, there is no fundamental understanding of how to craft order in one's life especially in the domain of the schedule which is the base strata for allocation of:
- time 
- energy
- plan

in this sense, a schedule is less of a burden, and more of a rhythmic strategy.

There is a fascinating aspect, that reveal deep human parts, when we see the schedule of some of the most brilliant people whom positively impacted our world.
