in principia cybernetica, we introduce the idea of physical constraint and real world modeling, establish a framework, A structure. in the classical sense of the word framework, a structure.

this might seem like reverse progress, since the whole point of code is to be as abstract away from having to write verbose computer instructions as possible. however, we are pursuing a different vision. something fundamentally more constrained, while providing expressive power. in the form of dissectable, generationally developed physically modeled architecture and components. 

not merely reusable components, but specific units of function. like logic gates or mathematical symbolic notation, but in the form of computation period
