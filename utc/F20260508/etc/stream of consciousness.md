The steam of consciousness is a super speed, one-way information highway connecting the subconscious with the conscious parts of the persona. The stream travels as a one-track-mind, bubbling from the subconscious to the superficial in the form of imagination image bytes. These images can be surreal, thought, aural, memory, or rarely full-imagination sensory depth.

This is the key part of the persona, as imagination can internally influence and change the current persona.

The form of these images are equally important: thoughts as spoken language is the most common form. This means we have agency...

There are those who do not have an inner voice, this still applies to parts of the psyche

Usually there is a voice, or many voices. That is not simply an inner critic; it is a distinct part of the self. 

The self is not one part, but many modular parts.

This is why the persona is a single self; the complexity beneath makes it impossible to function otherwise.
