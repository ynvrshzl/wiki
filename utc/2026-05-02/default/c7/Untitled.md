"Various theory on human autonomy"

We often want products, finishing lines, output. But we neglect the hidden steps between beginning and end, that is essentially life, the story.

What if our desire for product, neglects the very life that shapes the machine? What if it is in the exhaustion of muscle, that strength is built, not in final form, but in movement... in autonomy.

"What if... it is exactly in the moments where I am lost, that i remember my real path?"

This does not exclude suffering or pain from life, rather, it reframes it as a necessary part of the ever-flowing cycles of life of change and exhaustion, of autonomy and ability.
