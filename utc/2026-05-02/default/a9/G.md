[Joey Doom]() walks up completely beaten, blood and wounds pouring from his black charred, wet skin, ripped clothes, black tears in his eyes. He looks like someone who has been completely destroyed... but his headband still on. He still gets up.

He's almost like a broken victorious warrior that continously somehow survives things his body should not physically withstand.

This is how the human being feels when they survive trauma, or suffering. "How can I still be here? How can I still take this?"

And then Katerina says **"We survive. Just like him."**
