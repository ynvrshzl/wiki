# Microcosmos Theorem
This is as close as we've ever gotten to a unifying cohesive model of the human condition.

- The inner microcosmos suggests every human being, has something akin to an inner "youniverse"
- This microcosmos is a web, at substrate level, neurons, systems and cells.
- At emotional, real level, we have memories, dreams, lessons, ideas, thoughts, lessons, personality, identity, beliefs.
