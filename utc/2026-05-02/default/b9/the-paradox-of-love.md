The paradox of love is that: it is self-sufficient in reason. It is the point. Just like you enjoy music, it does not need further analysis, or understanding. You do not need any other reason to love someone; you simply do.

What is this paradox? What is the name for something that is inherently, nameless?

_"Just as we walk, without knowing how to walk, or knowing why. Just as we love someone, without knowing why, just as our heart continues to pump and keep us alive, and we do not know why, love, not knowing why, itself, gives reason to all things."_
