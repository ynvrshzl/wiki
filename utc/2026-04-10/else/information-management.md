# Imagen (Information Management)

A subset of Chaos Agency 

Described as a proto-PKM, this branch of study focuses on managing the chaos of information without necessarily having agency ot understanding.

For me, I'm learning to manage all my plain text writings!

## Hypothesis: all information is akin to an image 
Information management treats all information as an image. An image is an object of information that is fundamentally tangible, but contextually incomplete; e.g. "a picture is worth a thousand words."
