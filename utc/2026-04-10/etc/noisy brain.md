# The noisy brain

🗝️ Key: the most important thing is to release, not to organize or to silence or avoid (or even calm. it's like spraying water on a volcano.)

Living with (or inside) a 🔊 noisy mind is essentially, like having a chaos engine ♻️ constantly running. 

## Relief 
💤 Sleep won't make it stop.  The only thing is: release. This is why expression is the vital tool for loving with, and possibly, channeling the noise in the mind, to something beautiful.

## Symptoms 
The noisy mind wants to control everything. "I don't understand what I'm doing wrong... Why am I still suffering?"

In the mind of noise, everything is important. Ideas, stories, memories, notes. When everything is meaningful and important, nothing is; because importance requires urgency, significance and hierarchy, and when an entire structure (noisy mind) has urgency, everything overheats

## Examples of noise in the mind

- Thoughts
- Imagination
- Memories

## Defining noise
What is noise in other fields? (e.g. electrical engineering (EE))

## Defining signal

In 🔌 EE, noise is generally unwanted as it ⚠️ interferes with signal... but what actually is 💡 signal in the first place? In a circuit of ⚡ electricity 🔌... what is considered signal? Is signal significant because of...

- 🚫 silence (no noise)
- 🪄 clean voltage (+5v lack of, or no noise)
- or because of 🌪️ noise itself?

## Noise is not the issue
♻️ Noise is not the source issue, it is a symptom of unresolved circuits in the mind. Circuitry reveals something beautiful; even mechanical processes, require a loop, a cycle, that is the lifecycle of a system, that is the definition of a system; a feedback loop!


Therefore, we can determine that noise itself is 🚫 not  the issue; __infact noise is what distinguishes signal from background.__ The issue is not enough 🔎 filtering and ♻️ channels in a system.

## Capture like a Tesla Coil
💨 unreleased pressure in a system could be described as a signal filtering fault... there is not enough distinguishing between signal and noise, not because the system is not organized, but because the system (which would make organizing possible) doesn't even exist! 

✅ So then the solution is not necessarily to organize the system, but to create a system at all that can capture the intense voltage of noise in the mind. once this Source can capture voltage, it becomes sort of like a Tesla coil.

## The power of writing
✏️ Now you can shape and mold and filter and work with the thoughts. it's also important to recognize that it's impossible to capture every fleeting thought, and so the mind has a built-in filtering system, essentially the things that continuously repeat, the things that orbit, the things that you keep remembering, are the things worth capturing, because they are important. 

This is also incredibly relieving because it means you don't really forget about things that are important, because they always come back. (citation needed)

## Capture systems are everywhere
writing is just one example of many expressive capture systems. music, coding, you are designed, poetry, storytelling, filmmaking, playing the piano, singing, drawing, illustrating, these are not hobbies, for some of us they are the only thing that keep us from collapsing by internal noise.
