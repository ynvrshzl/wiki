# "Cyberethos"
Combining organic human + the cybernetic self. A merging of worlds? Or continuation of life's many forms?

Our ancestors lived in a completely different world from us. But we still have their same anatomical architecture. Are we fucked? No! No! No! Infact this is the continuation of life's many forms, only this time we are in charge of our own evolution.

This is a proposal of a cyberethos: what should be allowed to exist digitally and how do we protect the human anatomy while it is still evolving?
