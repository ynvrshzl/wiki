"Elemental tools of the Polymath."

These are essentially the primary drivers of the polymath autonomy.

- compression, abstraction
- integration, unification
- process
- structure
- navigation
- discoverability
- inheritance
- systems
- inspiration
- study, research
- experiment, sampling, play
- routine, cycles, rhythm, ritual
- scheduling [^2]
- reference [^4]
- management, organization [^3]
- equilibrium [^1]


[^1]: Most important

[^2]: Significant because time is one of the only things we cannot control. [[time]]

[^3]: Our work can only be as integrated as we organize it.

[^4]: Reference, not as research reference, but as reference of the completed works by other people, as inspiration source. [read more here.](reference.md)
