if we were designing life os, this product, for real life human application, then it reframes the product, from a personal research project, to a collaborative social focus tool for the computer. arguably, it would be an essential part of the modern operating system.

essentially, the main idea of Life OS as a product, is a cognitive surface area, sitting superficially above the device, either mobile or desktop. in which the surface area becomes a fully editable workspace, 
- infra: integrates with the system beneath (such as operating system-specific operations) 
- supra: an interaction layer above (how the human being interacts with the computer)
- meta: the feedback cycle between these systems and layers, e.g., how the interaction layer, also serves as output information, and how the output information is structured, so that the human being can make meaningful decisions in the computational workspace. 

the goal of this product is to transform the operating system into a malleable cognitive surface area, designed not for life management, but for integration and focus. 

For this reason, a major aspect of the program, is the ability to create multiple workspaces, of specific worktypes.
- a doctor may use multiple workspaces to organize deep focus, documents, and more. 

the most important structural design choice of this program/product, is that it is essentially a projection interpretation of source data. meaning, The Source data always lives locally on the human beings machine. it can be used by any other program, or edited, or transported, or shared. this is because every aspect of the program is stored as plain text in the system. there are no proprietary systems, because the philosophy of this product, is to provide a surface area that can adapt to human needs, rather than a technology that serves ephemeral trends. 

In this sense, the technology acts as evergreen cyber units, which corresponds with our guiding principles, _[The cyber ethos.]()_
