# Etymology 
- Hemisphere
- Stratosphere 

hyp. "hemispheres, or domains of being, are the phenomenological ecosystems of perception, direction, and episodic momentum for the human being."

What this proposed is, two-fold, there are elements to the human beings life. and there are influence-able, changeable properties. our memories, thought patterns, social life, and more elements which create what we call "my life".

Not only does this hypothesis try to conceptualize the mechanical systems that support a human being, but as well as the actionable, operational properties and protocols that truly shape the human being. in this sense, the hemisphere acts as a stratosphere, a feedback cycle.

this suggests that we as human beings experience the world not only through our senses, but through the environment, and through our internal processes , such as filtering life through the lens.
