# How do we human beings create new things, without understanding everything?

Creation Theorem

Abstract: How do we human beings create new things, without understanding everything? 

## 1.0 Introduction
We cannot possibly understand everything in the universe, and yet, we build systems that are larger than we can understand... what is this paradox? More importantly, what does this practically reveal about chaos and order, in everyday human life?

Phases of creation

The stable attractors in both of these dynmaic phases are:
- Constraint
- Modulation

Exploration phase

- Unlocks possibilities
- Breaks constraints
- High entropy
- What is possible?

Architecture phase

- Selects and stabilizes
- Creates constraints
- Low entropy
- What should be possible?
