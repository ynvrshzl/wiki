Association

This part of [Chaos Agency](import/utc/2026.04.07/chaos-agency/readme.md), references heavily the neuroscience of [association in human memory](public/youniversity/neuroscience/association.md) reveals how we navigate chaos in everyday life. 

Even our very own memory is a chaotic web of interconnected information and signal. That would completely overwhelm our senses, if we were able to access everything, all at once. 

So instead of absorbing everything at once, our biology intelligently __selects__ what is important. Selection is based on: what is needed now (urgency, context), what is important (?) and... [^3]

Even without intentional design, the only way to navigate complex systems &mdash; such as information retrieval in websites and software &mdash; is to architect, using __association__ as a base mechanic.

## Practical mechanics of association

We associate trails of thought (citation needed). This means we can stack layers to both hide complexity, and establish navigation.

Practically speaking, we cannot be bombarded by information, and maintain sanity. This is why the human brain, contains an deep organic filtering engine, which protects you from being overwhelmed by all of the information we are exposed to everyday.

(We purposefully forget, and ignore, not to discard, but to protect.)
