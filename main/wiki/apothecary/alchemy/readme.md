# Alchemy


###### 0.0 Introduction
Alchemy, in the context of our [apothecary](apothecary.md), is our study of human emotion, psychology, and mythology, role-playing as mad scientist witch, brewing the perfect concoction of spells to cure any disease. Okay, maybe that last part is a little magical, but some emotions are indistinguishable from magic, so we shall approach them as so!

Seriously though, this is perhaps one of the most foundational cornerstones in our work. The study of human emotion, as of 2026, does not exist. That is, until now.
