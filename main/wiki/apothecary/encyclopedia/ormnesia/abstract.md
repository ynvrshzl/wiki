**Abstract — The fundamental issue in human nature, might be "Oramnesia".**

Amnesia of good things might stem from what we repeat everyday. If we do not move towards a positive vector, we forget the expansion in life.

Why do we lose sight of what matters most in life? Why do we forget what is most important? _"You don't know what you have, until you lose it."_ Why is this quote only true, when it's too late?
