# Logosomnia
_n. "a deprivation of meaning"_

###### Etymology
From the greek words "somnia", of deprivation, and "logos" which translates, roughly, to "meaning." 

###### Context
This emotion/phenomena describes the sudden awareness that you are are where you belong. Your most meaningful enviornment. It's almost a sudden realization *"I have stepped out of the mist... where was I? I never want to go back there."* Do not panic, for this is a cycle, this place will not dissapear!
