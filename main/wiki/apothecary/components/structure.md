## 3.0 "Everything is a structure" - A conceptual model for human flourishing
The fundamental model for human flourishing, could possibly be conceptualized as "everything in human life is a structure." If we imagine relationships, ideas, decisions, opportunities, to all behave as structures... then we can treat them as we would a physical thing, even if these definitions are primarily abstract parts of human life.


## Component models of the psyche and human being
- Identity of self
- Perception of self
- Self-narrative (self-talk, the story we believe everyday, about our life, and ourself.)

  ... these are core principles that impact the self.
