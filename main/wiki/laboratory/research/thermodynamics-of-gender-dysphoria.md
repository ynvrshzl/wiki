# Thermodynamics, Mechanics, and Structure of Gender Dysphoria

Studying and breaking apart the mechanics of [Gender Dysphoria](#) **becuase this fucking sucks and we could be living without suffering like this!**

## Principle 1 - Love

Ultimately, the healthy human being, want's to be loved for who they are. There is nothing that replaces this necessity. It is not a luxury to be loved, it is a human necessity, as water and food.

GD essentially warps the perceptual mechanics of the self. It's a "dysphoria" so that's a given; it will fundamentally change what is real. Sharing similarities with psychosis and delusion; **the good news is that you aren't those delusions.** That means you do deserve to be loved.

That's what saved my life. Getting out of my own head. And learning to believe that I was safe enough to exist and be who I truly am, 100%. And that I deserved to be loved, first and foremost, that I deserve to love myself.

A philosphy we developed that was sculpted by my experience with GD, and is also my dedication to helping you, is the short book of [Principia Materna](principia-materna.md)

# Dysphoria, from the lens of the human.
Imagine you were a person, living with GD. What would it feel like?

_"From personal observation and lived experience, it almost feels like... as soon as I look in the mirror... I become someone else... like you lose contact with who you are normally... and you become hyperfixated on feeling ugly. When you feel ugly, some part of you feels worthless and unwanted. It is always biological at it's heart... but it is also far deeper than mere hormones... Losing communication with your current integrated self... is known as "Dissociation..." it's one of the most common things I feel. The traumatized, brokenm fragmented parts of your identity take over... and it feels like i'm just staring at myself in the mirror... but I know who I am... and it's scary... it's so scary to look at yourself in the miror and feel like another person is watching back at you... instead of seeing yourself in the mirror. This is the core foundation of Gender Dysphoria, it is what makes this condition nightmare-inducing."_

IF the core mechanics of GD is fragmentation... then the only structural answer is healing. Because healing, as in nature, is the only process which corrects broken structure, back to a healthy functioning whole. Inspired by the Japanese philosophy of [wabi-sabi]() which is a beautiful form of pottery art, in which broken parts are welded together with liquid gold, and teh cracks are filled, to creat a whole structure, that is more beautiful becuase of it's  unique imperfections.

However, this does not glorify suffering. We don't break in order to become more beautiful, we survive in order to become whole again. Suffering is, the worst part of this human experience.
