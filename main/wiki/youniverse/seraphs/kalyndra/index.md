# Justiciar Kalyndra

*"Justice of the high heavens"*

An exalted figure of justice and splendor, Kalyndra serves the high heavens as the lady of justice and valor. She establishes order and grace.

## Why do people suffer?
"The mortals suffer not for their ignorance, but for our lack of grace. We understand them, but we do not comprehend them. Perhaps it is we, that are ignorant."

## Life
Kalyndra was once human, who suffered voraciously, for her love of human beings. The solitude, poisoned her heart, and one day, she died. For her love was much too deep for this world. Now, Kalyndra watches over the mortals, and serves justice, preserving grace, regardless of their ignorance and cruelty.

And Justiciar Kalyndra spoke, in her magnificent voice, akin to trumpets harmonizing with the heavens: *"Human beings are ignorant, and most are very cruel creatures. Yet, we must preserve grace, for the innocent."*
