# Archael the archivist
Archivist of the Heavens

Archael the archivist, is an archangel of the high heavens in the youniverse.

The libraries of the heavens they are colossal and structures of light. You cannot physically change the structures of the heavens, because everything is light. this is why you have to be of a specific form in order to even experience having, light in its true form. that is why the angels are not merely humans..

there are seemingly infinite records and recordings of life in all its forms, and amongst these codexes of knowledge, the archangel is studying the void. it is only thanks to him, that we understand the void and how to fight against it. because the void seemingly does not exist. at least, that is it's illusion cast on the human beings. in the mortal realm. but in the spirit realm, though the void is still not visible, where there is an exclusion of light, there is shadow, void, absence of Life of light.

the archangel is tasked on eternal duty, to preserve the knowledge so that the human beings may someday be liberated from their suffering. most of the Angels gather information and memories and expand the library. but there are some angels that simply do not believe in the salvation of humanity. there are some angels that believe, their life stories have already been recorded and written in fate. and there are some angels that believe human beings have the power to change their own fate and write their own heaven recording.

but that is not of the concern of the archivist. he simply records knowledge.

he is in charge of recording information for the council of the heavens. he is an angel of tremendous splendor and brilliant magnificence. it is thanks to him, through his rigorous studying, we were able to define the void. their methods. and how to fight against them so that they do not destroy humanity. because the void does not truly want to destroy humanity, it wants to destroy all of existence. a noble cause does not equate to a noble deed. and the greatest trick that avoid ever pulled, was convincing the world it does not exist. that's a reference to a quote, the greatest trick the devil ever pulled is convincing the world he doesn't exist. because if you believe something exists, then it affects you and it changes you and it influences your actions. but if you do not believe something exists… it cannot persuade your action.
