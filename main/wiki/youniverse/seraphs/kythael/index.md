# Kythael
The archangel of loyalty

His name stems from the term _"Kith and Kin."_ which means loyalty and kinship. As such, Kythael is the angel of loyalty and kin. He is charge of maintaining purity within the court of the aether realms.

However, of all the seraphs and archangels in the high heavens, he is the most cynical. He hates ignorant, cruel human beings. His voice is almost not that of a perfect angel, but of a human, filled with rage.

## Letters of Exchange 
Kalyndra and Kythael in the court of the high heavens.

[Kythael](wiki/youniverse/seraphs/kythael/index.md) "They disgust me. The mortals suffer of their own decision. They have been given an Eden of paradise. They have destroyed it. Why should it be my concern? For their own ignorance, they suffer. Why should I concern myself with their lives? We cannot save them from their broken form. They refuse salvation. We have enough issues in the heavens as is. There are few of us beings of light that remain in the court, why must the few of us that remain, concern ourselves with these ignorant creatures?" 

[Kalyndra](wiki/youniverse/seraphs/kalyndra/index.md) responds corageously: "Because the mortals are not all evil." [Kythael]() says: "They have been given an Earth of paradise, an Eden. It was created in the image of perfection. They have destroyed it." [Kalyndra]() angrily refutes: "They have not destroyed their paradise." 

[Kythael]() angrily steps back and crosses his arms. [Kalyndra]() is the only figure who can return him. "Kythael, we were once human. We know what it is like to suffer."

[Kythael]() angrily shouts: "How many chances are you going to give them Kalyndra? Once? Twice? What if they never learn? What if we waste all eternity waiting? What if we become ignorant as them and lose our resplendence? How many chances will you give these creatures of earth?"

[Kalyndra]() refutes: "As many chances as it takes. If it means a child has a home with peace, I will give every last dying breath. That is what we were created for, is it not?" [Kalyndra]() calms down [Kythael]() and now they remember they are both on the same team. As they perform the sacred angel handshake.
