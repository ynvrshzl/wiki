# Authors Notes on "Joey Doom"
Joey Doom is my metabolization of trauma, suffering, and pain. He's basically my exploration of _"Okay, this pain is just unbearable. How do I live with this pain?"_

Why is pain so undesirable? Why is it so uncomfortable? How can you live with pain? Chronic pain? Suffering?

Joey Doom, is essentially, a personification of one of the hardest problems in human life; _"if life is so painful, why is it still worth living?"_
