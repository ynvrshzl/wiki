# Integros Yami
Yami as his ultimate infrasupra self, has glowing blonde hair, blonde eyebrows, and blind eyes. He resembles a Godly figure. As the storms of his inner Youniverse wash over him.

Joey Doom sees him, fighting for his life, in the void. "What are you doing here joey?" Doom tries to save him, but Yami seems to have become a torrent of nature. Yami smiles, "It doesn't even hurt anymore. I can't even be hurt anymore."
