The echo chambers are the most isolated part of the Abyss. They reflect back thoughts, imaginations & memories inside you. The more you listen to them, the louder they become. Echoing into the Abyss, forever, until something, or someone, stops it.

# The Echo-chambers of Torment
The echo-chambers of depression, objectified as caverns.

In the Abyss, there are underwater caverns and hydrothermal vents (just like in the real life mariana's trench.) Covered in sharp, needle-like cave structures, shaped like teeth. It is said, this place, is so dark, it swallows all light. At this point of the persona's life, they have either already died, or have given up on life and are ready to die, as nobody can escape the pressure, hunger, and torment of these caverns. 


It is an edge, where existence itself becomes parasitic, feeding on you. Everything seems dark. Here, absolutely no light can shine.  And yet, [bioluminescence](bioluminescence.md) only emerges in these shadows, the strongest form of all light in the Youniverse.
