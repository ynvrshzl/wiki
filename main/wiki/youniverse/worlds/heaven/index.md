The high heavens exists as an aether space, of light, and formless constructs. Meaning, the heavens are structures in hyper-dimensions, existing as forms of light, without shadow. Only the faces of the angels, seraphs, constructs

It is a place so beautiful, it would overwhelm the senses of the human being. _"This must be why the mortals cry when they witness beauty."_

The high heavens are floating, without gravity. They exist outside of time and space, in a completely different form than we can comprehend. And yet, they concern themselves with our mortal realm. **It is not a contract between the divine and the ignorant.** The angels each, of their very own free will, choose to protect and serve humankind, even in our ignorance.

This is most clearly exlempified by [Justiciar Kalyndra](wiki/youniverse/seraphs/kalyndra/index.md)
