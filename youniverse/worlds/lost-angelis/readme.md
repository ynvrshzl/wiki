"Lost Angelis" is one of the central settings in the youniverse. 

Lost Angelis itself is part of a subterranean, underground, almost Atlantis-like, system of human spaces. 

the history of the city is incredibly tragic, and it is simply a result of the old earth, which was completely extinguished from nuclear aftermath. essentially, to understand the city, we must understand the history of Old Earth. once a beautiful hospitable planet, now completely barren devoid of life, with no trace of consciousness or signs of life. a woman was responsible for a nuclear catastrophe, because she believed that human life was fundamentally meaningless, tragic and pointlessly cruel. she believed that in a final blaze of glory, humankind would be saved from further tragedy. she believed it was necessary. however, history repeats itself. and human beings have always found a way to adapt even to life's most harsh conditions. and it's almost symbolic because even though she was trying to save people from suffering, she created suffering of such scale that, she essentially became the thing she hated; human evil. 

the story of old earth, and capsulated in a single human being, the woman, is a symbol of ethics. essentially, it's our study in understanding at what scope does the human being functionally operate? because at such a global world scale, we lose meaning and orientation. because we were not meant to navigate the world, we were meant to navigate our nearest space, and to understand the inner world. the inner youniverse.
