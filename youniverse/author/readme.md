# Author
She is often seen crying and pondering both the beauty and terror od being human.

## Appearance

- **Brown hair with Pink streaks -** the pink highlights in the author's hair, symbolize her loyalty to the reduction of suffering and betterment of life, in all it's forms. In our Youniverse, the color pink symbolizes healing, youth, and protection from suffering.
- **Android bodysuit:** The author constructed a mechanical armor, which would eventually become her autonomous android anatomy.

The author wears an android-body suit. Inspired by [D.Va]() from [Overwatch](), her suit is primarily a white base with pink accents. Her hair is also pink., however, this is only one form of the office evolution. in her final evolution, her hair is entirely pink, and her body is entirely machine. She becomes an automaton with a human soul, because to the author, the body is simply a vessel of earth, unnecessarily comstrained. The imagination is infinite. It is the most powerful thing the human being has, but the hands to wield it, it's just as powerful and dangerous. the author imagines weapons at will and uses it to fight inside her own spirit realm. each weapon, symbolizes something significant in her life. 

- The Katana "Zero" is both a reference to one of her favorite games, katana zero, as well as zero, as in the story of Joey Doom, one of her most beloved imaginations

## Persona paradox 
There are a few times within the stories of the author, that her own characters become sentient and contact her. it is then that the most armored parts of the author, or revealed. the childlike playful imagination, the boss woman soldier, the engineer, and more.

the paradox is that the author herself is not a single human being but rather, she has been fragmented into several separate personas. they were born from drama. from survival. from pain. some of them were born from beauty. the authors life work this to heal the broken parts of her inner youniverse. The persona, is a lens of her. A refraction of human consciousness as pure light. This is how consciousness is interpreted in the youniverse.
