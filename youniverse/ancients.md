"The ancient civilizations of the youniverse."

Two civilizations fought great, ancient battles, throughout the aeons of the youniverse.

- The "Aesthetics" plated themselves in gold and researched a cure for death and sufferin. They were ultimately developing "Aetherium", an element powerful enough to freeze time in light form. They adored memory, time and permanance.
- The "Ascensions" coated everything in ashes. They believed pain was too great to justify existing.

We came to know these civilizations of human beings, as the very manifestation the youniverse: echoes of chaos, and the energy of light.
