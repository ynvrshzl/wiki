## 1.0
The very first persona that was birthed in our universe was elizabeth. we saw a profile picture of an anime girl online and we just imagined how cool it would be to create your own character and then people online would like put it as their profile picture. because during the time anime profile pictures were very popular and if you think about it that's like a persona, you're persona, your profile picture is like your persona and if you choose a specific anime character then we pretty much and body who you are or who you want to be. and so that's the birth of the persona. not essentially a mask but rather the embodiment of the true self.

## 2.0
After Elizabeth we wanted to give her friends, and that's where we started drawing a lot. and eventually inspiration sources of all walks of life, gave birth to the very first group, the rebellion. the name of rebellion, came from the sadness that so many young people are suffering today. we wanted to give them sort of like a group that represent our generation. there was so much depression and mental health issues in our generation and the passage generations pretty much live in a completely different reality. it's sad but it's also necessary for human flourishing. that's what we believe anyways, and that's really what the Rebellion represents. Rebellion against the demons within that try and steal life. Rebellion against the inner animal. Rebellion against the monsters within us. regardless of who created monster, the whole purpose of being in rebellion, a rebel, is that you refuse to give up, especially on life. and really this was our way of dealing with mental illness. and also on a more social scale, figuring out what the fuck we can do about all of this pain we feel. 

that is when things started becoming extremely existential and I actually slipped into severe depression and so for a vast period of time we just stopped writing stories. now, at the time of writing this it is March 12th 2026, and I think God that I'm alive and we are recovering our health and figuring things out!

joey doom came from the doomer meme... doomer used to be all about the depressive Doom aesthetic. someone who feels stuck in life, depressed, nocturnal, feeling like they're wasting their existence, it's a really sad miserable state for a human being. we wanted to encapsulate what it would feel like if you love someone, like someone in your family, but they are going through severe depression and they are isolated and alone. and you love them so much and you just want them to feel happy and to be engaged with meaning in life. and essentially that's where Yami was birthed. someone who loves his friend so much. she'll do anything to help doom feel better. 

however, as time went by, our stories also evolved and growed with us as a human being. and eventually Katarina became the Doom persona.

bloomer is yami evolved, although we originally imagined Joey Bloom to be the opposite of Joey doom, and they would meet up in the world called euthanasia. however we quickly realized that it would be strange for that interaction to not include Yami.
