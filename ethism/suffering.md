# 2 - "Suffering is not necessary"
The Driving Force of Ethism

"If life is pain, why live?" This is the core question that I ask myself everyday. It's the annoying little spark that keeps this engine running, even when it's overclocked and exhausted! I may not have the perfect answer, but we surely explore it.

Abstract: If life is fundamentally pain, and suffering can be reduced, mitigated, or even negated, then what is left? Meaning.

The mechanics of meaning are physical, even if it is pyshological in nature. Everything in human life follows a physical form, even the most abstract of thought.
